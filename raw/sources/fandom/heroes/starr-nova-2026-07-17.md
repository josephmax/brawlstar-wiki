Starr Nova (also known as Stella)<ref>[https://youtu.be/n8GposKb0dE Origin Story - How Stella Became Starr Nova! (Music: “polaris” by Aooo)]</ref> is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who has moderate health, a very fast movement and reload speed, and a variable range and damage output. She was the 103rd Brawler added to the game. She attacks by firing two piercing sparkles from her hair that shoot from right side then left. For her Super, she dashes forward, transforming into her alternate form, damaging enemies on her way. Starr Nova's alternate form attacks by swiping her sword in a wide and close-ranged arc, healing her slightly when she hits enemies with her attack. Her first Gadget, Floaty Time, she places a crystal that allows her and allies to fly over obstacles within its area-of-effect until it is destroyed. Her second Gadget, Shining Starr of Friendship and Justice, fires a projectile that damages enemies, and heals allies, while Starr Nova can use it again to instantly teleport. Her first Star Power, Power Level: Maximum!, increases her alternate form's attack damage after hitting a target. Her second Star Power, Mystical Starr Technique, allows Starr Nova's normal attack to heal allies it hits.

### Attack: Nova Hair, Go!

{{Quote|Starr Nova uses her glorious anime hair as a weapon, firing 2 blonde projectiles.}}
Starr Nova fires large, piercing sparkles from her hair in 2 waves that deal moderate damage. The projectiles have a slight horizontal spread. This attack is very wide and extremely fast, making it next to impossible for enemies to dodge.

### Super: Nova Crystal, Transform!

{{Quote|Starr Nova dashes and transforms into her ultimate form, conjuring her Nova sword! She gains movement speed, converts dealt damage to health, and her main attack changes shape. Transformation lasts 7 sec.}}
Starr Nova dashes forward, dealing low damage to enemies along her way and transforming into her alternate form for 7 seconds. While in this form, Starr Nova's movement speed is increased by 25%, she attacks by swinging with her sword, hitting enemies in a wide, 135 degree arc, and her attacks heal her for 30% of the damage dealt. The attack has no wind up, she isn't immobile while attacking and there isn't any travel time, so the attack instantly takes effect. Using Starr Nova's Super when she is already in her alternate form will reset the duration of it back to 7 seconds. Starr Nova's Super transformation can be cancelled if she is stunned, knocked back, or pulled during the dash.

### Gadgets / Floaty Time

{{Quote|Starr Nova deploys an anti-gravity device, allowing teammates to float inside of the area! Cooldown: 20 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
When aiming using the Gadget button, Starr Nova lobs a crystal that creates an area that allows her and her allies to fly in a 4.33-tile radius, similar to [[Janet]]'s Super. The crystal has 3000 health, and will be destroyed if Starr Nova drops another crystal. It also decays by 300 health per second until it is destroyed, lasting for up to 10 seconds if it doesn't take any damage. While allies are flying, they cannot attack or use their Super, Gadget, or Hypercharge, and if the crystal was destroyed the allies that were flying will descend. Allies can also descend by leaving the Gadget area.

### Gadgets / Shining Starr of Friendship and Justice

{{Quote|Starr Nova fires an energy ball that damages enemies and heals teammates for 1600. This energy ball can also be teleported to before it hits a target! Cooldown: 20 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
When aiming using the Gadget button, Starr Nova fires a projectile that damages enemies and heals allies along the way. In addition, she can use the Gadget again while the projectile is active to instantly teleport.

### Star Powers / Power Level: Maximum!

{{Quote|Hitting an enemy Brawler while transformed increases your damage by 5% to a maximum of 30%.}}{{StarPower|1}}
Starr Nova's alternate form attack damage increases by 5% after every target hit, up to 30%. The damage boost is retained if she uses her Super while she is already in her alternate form, but it is cancelled instantly after she transforms back into her normal form.

### Star Powers / Mystical Starr Technique

{{Quote|Starr Nova's main attack also heals teammates for 384, charging her Super based on healing.}}{{StarPower|2}}
Starr Nova's main attack heals allied Brawlers for 40% of the damage dealt. Healing allies with it also charges Starr Nova's Super very slightly. This does not apply to her transformed attack.

### Tips

*Starr Nova's base attack has the fastest projectile speed in the game, making her extremely useful at continuously chipping away at Brawlers while maintaining her distance. She is also good at bush checking as opponents won't be able to anticipate her shots easily.
**When fighting against her, it's generally advised to just outrange or burst her down, as it's basically impossible to dodge her attacks.
**Due to the extremely fast attacks, it will be difficult to get a perception of how much distance to keep away to avoid being hit, as the attacks will be difficult to see. Try to make a comparison with other similar lengths to get an idea of how far you should be.
*Starr Nova's extremely fast unload speed, decent range and survivability in her Super form make up for her lack of DPS when compared to other aggressive Brawlers. As such, always keep your distance from other aggressive brawlers and use your Super to escape whenever needed.
**Her instant unload speed also means that she can easily strafe around other assassins and aggressive brawlers.
**Do not use your Super to dive into enemy crowds: Starr Nova's healing is nowhere as potent as [[Edgar]], [[Mortis]], or [[Kenji]]'s, and she will often end up being defeated very quickly, which is why strafing from a safe distance is advised.
*Owing to her agility and fast unload speed, Starr Nova is best used to harass teams and act as a distraction instead of actually taking them out herself. However, her Power Level: Maximum! Star Power still makes her very capable of dealing heavy damage to opponents, especially in crowds, allowing her to force retreats or help to wipe out survivors.
*Avoid using your Floaty Time Gadget near important choke points or objectives, as you will often only end up sabotaging your team. Instead, use it as an escape from powerful enemy attacks when no other options are available, or place it near obstacles to overcome them.
**Do note that when confronted with a lot of enemy fire, the Gadget will most likely get destroyed in one or two seconds. Therefore, you should act fast in these situations.
**An alternative way you can use this Gadget when you have your Super is by faking a charge at the enemy and deploying it immediately. This will make your opponents waste most of their ammo and allow you to quickly retaliate.
**Alternatively, place your Gadget near to, but not directly in, a choke point, providing a safe zone where your allies can retreat to if the resistance becomes too overwhelming. Somewhere like behind a nearby wall is ideal.
*Starr Nova's Shining Starr of Friendship and Justice Gadget is a useful tool for ambushing opposing Brawlers or repositioning when in danger. Activate this Gadget when facing a bunched-up group of enemies and activate your Super before teleporting to their backs, as this will allow you to catch your opponents off guard or at least make them waste ammo targeting you from the front.
