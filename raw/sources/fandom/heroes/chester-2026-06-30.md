Chester is a {{Rarity|Legendary}} [[Brawler]] who has moderately high health, an extreme damage output, a fast movement speed, a long range, and a random Super, but a moderately slow reload speed. He was the 62nd Brawler added to the game. For his attack, he throws bells; how many bells he throws is based on a cyclic sequence. Chester's Super is one of five Supers that can either deal high damage, knock back enemies, and destroy obstacles; fire a candy boxing glove that stuns an enemy; spray a cloud of salt that deals damage over time; damage and slow down enemies in a moderate radius; or significantly heal himself over time. His first Gadget, Spicy Dice, changes his Super at random. His second Gadget, Candy Beans, gives either a speed boost, a reload speed boost, a damage boost, or healing over time at random. His first Star Power, Single Bell 'O' Mania, increases the damage of his first attack sequence. His second Star Power, Sneak Peek, allows Chester to see what Super he has before it is charged. His Hypercharge, Crunchy, Chewy, Gooey, combines three Supers into one that deals high damage and destroy obstacles, then sprays a cloud of salt that deals damage over time and slow down enemies in a moderate radius.

### Trait

{{Quote|This Brawler starts with a random Super that keeps changing after every use.}}{{Trait|Random}}
Chester will have a random Super out of the 5 possible Supers Chester can use every time he charges a Super. The Super selected won't be revealed until the Super is fully charged, with each Super having a unique icon. If Chester dies, a round is over in [[Knockout]], or a goal is scored in [[Brawl Ball]] while his Super is charged, he will respawn with a randomized Super. In these cases, as well as after using the Spicy Dice Gadget, it is possible for Chester to get the same Super consecutively.

### Attack: Cap and Bells

{{Quote|Chester shoots a sequence of bells from his cap, first one, then two, then three, and lastly four bells.}}Chester throws long-ranged bells that deal damage on hit. Chester's attack cycles in a sequence of firing one bell in a straight line, firing two bells with a spread, firing three bells with a wide spread, and firing four bells with a wider spread. Chester's attack cycles even if he doesn't hit an enemy, and if he is defeated, his attack starts with its first sequence.

### Hypercharge: Crunchy, Chewy, Gooey

{{Quote|Chester combines 3 of his Super abilities into one. He throws a sweet that explodes and deals damage, spreads a poison cloud, and leaves a lingering slowing area to finish.}}{{Hypercharge}}
Chester gets a new Super that is like Candy Popper, Salmiakki, and Pop Rocks Supers combined into one. Upon activating the Hypercharge, the icon of Chester's Super will change into that of his Candy Popper Super. He throws a projectile that acts like his Candy Popper Super and upon exploding, instantaneously spawns eight projectiles like that of his Salmiakki Super in a circle. 0.7 seconds after the explosion, a circular slowing area like that of his Pop Rocks Super spawns in. The impact damage of Salmiakki is modified to deal less damage equivalent to 15% of the explosion's damage compared to the other ticks. If Chester is defeated before Salmiakki spawns, it would deal the base damage. Pop Rocks is also changed to deal 8% of the explosion's damage per second instead of the normal amount. However, the effects of each component of his Hypercharge Super will be nerfed with respect to their regular counterparts. Candy Popper no longer knocks enemies back; the Salmiakki sprays are slightly slower, travel 1 tile less distance than the normal Super, and have a 2-tile wide blind spot at the center of the explosion; and Pop Rocks only lasts for 4 seconds instead of 10. Chester also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips

*If you forget how many bells Chester's next attack will fire, look at the attack spread. The 1-bell stage has a rectangular hitbox similar to most single-projectile attacks, while the other stages have cone-shaped hitboxes with varying sizes of spread.
*Consider getting close to enemies when using his 3/4 bells attack. If you have difficulty approaching, try using his Candy Beans Gadget or his Jawbreaker Super. When you are fighting against assassins, by having 3-4-1 sequences you can deal a massive burst of damage in a very short time which is enough to kill most assassins with 3-4 bells. When you are having a close combat with Tanks 2-3-4 sequence is recommended to maximize damage deal to enemies.
*Even though enemies might know what Super you have, his Candy Popper Super is effective at distancing enemies near your spawn, while not pushing them too far back to hit only one bell from Chester's attack.
*At the start of the game, shoot one bell so that Chester will have his second, third, and fourth bells waiting. With Chester's next bells, you will be able to kill most Brawlers. 
*He is a very viable option in [[Knockout]], very versatile and can come back from a 1v3 situation with his Supers.
*The best setup for Chester is the Candy Beans Gadget and his Single Bell 'O' Mania Star Power, and it's recommended to have the Gadget Cooldown and Shield Gears equipped in order to get 5-second buffs every ~13 seconds, which is extremely useful in modes such as [[Brawl Ball]].
*When using the Candy Beans Gadget, try to use it before combating to gain a massive advantage by having buffs or even healing from enemies' damage.
*Always have a plan for each of the Supers you might get so that even if your Spicy Dice cannot get the Super you wanted you will still know what to do with it. All attacking Supers besides Jawbreaker can be useful for teamwipes, especially when multiple enemies are affected by the Super it can recharge very quickly.
*He is also a very good last pick in [[Ranked]] if you are unsure of how to combat the synergy of the enemy team. However, don't pick him against multiple sharpshooters on an open map, as he has poor damage at far range.
