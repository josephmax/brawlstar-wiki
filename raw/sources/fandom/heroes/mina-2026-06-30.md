{{Quote|Mina is a b-girl who mixes capoeira into her breakdance battles like it's second nature. She hangs out at SK8 beats with [[Ollie]] and uses his mixtapes to practice new moves!}}
{{Portrait}}Mina is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who has a moderate health, an above-average movement speed, a variable attack range and damage output, a fast reload speed, and immense mobility with her main attacks and Super. She was the 95th Brawler added to the game. Her attack alternates between a sandal, a tambourine, and a wave of wind in a cone, after which she dashes in the direction she moves and gains a timer that resets back to the first attack sequence if she doesn't attack in that time. In the first attack, her sandal flies off, which has a long range, but low damage. In the second attack, she fires a tambourine that deals moderate damage and has a medium range. In the third attack, she projects a wave of wind in a cone shape that deals high damage but has a short range. For her Super, Mina sends a hurricane that damages and launches enemies in the air upon hitting them. Her first Gadget, Windmill, creates a circular barrier of wind that absorbs projectiles. Her second Gadget, Capo-What?, recharges her next Super upon damaging enemy Brawlers. Her first Star Power, Zum Zum Zum, heals her on the third attack. Her second Star Power, Blown Away, roots enemies caught in her Super. Her Hypercharge, Triplecane, spawns three hurricanes in a cone that also bounce off of walls.

### Attack: Capoeira Combo

{{Quote|Mina can cycle through three ranged attacks in a certain time period. If she doesn't attack fast enough her combo is broken an the cycle restarts.<br>Attack 1: Long range & narrow<br>Attack 2: Medium range & medium width<br>Attack 3: Short range & wide}}
Mina's attack sequence alternates between a sandal, a tambourine, and a wave of wind in a cone shape, and simultaneously dashes 1.83 tiles in the direction she moves. Mina will not dash if she is not moving at the time the attack is used. For her first sequence, her sandal flies off, which has a long range, but deals very low damage upon impact. For her second sequence, her tambourine flies off, which deals moderate damage, but has a shorter range. For her third sequence, she projects a wave of wind in a cone shape at a medium range that deals high damage and pierces through enemies. The third attack comes with a delay of 0.5 seconds, but Mina's dash is not delayed. For every attack, Mina gains a timer bar that lasts for 1.35 seconds. If Mina doesn't attack before the timer is up, her attack resets to the first sequence.

### Super: Furacão 3000

{{Quote|Mina hits the air, creating a powerful hurricane which deals heavy damage and launches enemies in front of her.}}
Mina sends a hurricane that damages enemies and launches them in the air upon contact, pulling them towards her by 1.2 tiles. This projectile is wide enough to affect enemies behind walls, though it cannot pass through them. While in the air, the enemies can't move or use their attack, Super, Gadget, or Hypercharge, but will be completely immune to all damage besides status effects and damage applied over time. However, they are not immune to jumping Supers of [[Bonnie]], [[El Primo]], [[Edgar]], [[Crow]], [[Kit]], or [[Mico]]. Note that the Super pulls in the direction of Mina's location at the time she used the Super, not towards her current location. Just like the main attack, using the Super causes her to dash and also resets her attack timer, allowing the combo to continue, although this does not modify the Super itself.

### Gadgets / Windmill

{{Quote|Creates a wall of wind that blocks projectiles for 1.5 secs. Cooldown: 22 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
Mina creates a 4-tile wide circular barrier of wind that absorbs projectiles from enemies for 1.5 seconds. This Gadget is similar to [[Buster]]'s Hypercharged Super, but it doesn't follow you while you are moving nor does it reflect the energy as a counterattack projectile. It also has no effect on projectiles that can break or pass through walls. Enemies can still move through the barrier to deal damage, and attacks of allies are unaffected by the Gadget. This Gadget's cooldown begins after it's activated.

### Gadgets / Capo-What?

{{Quote|Mina's next Super recharges instantly if it hits. Cooldown: 22 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
Activating this Gadget allows Mina to recharge her Super instantly upon hitting an enemy with a Super. A Gadget symbol will glow above Mina's head signaling this Gadget's activation, as well as a glowing Super joystick. This Gadget's cooldown begins after the Super is used.

### Star Powers / Zum Zum Zum

{{Quote|Mina's combo Attack 3 also heals for 50% of damage done.}}{{StarPower|1}}
When Mina's third attack sequence deals damage to enemies, she regains health equal to 50% of the damage dealt. This can stack with multiple opponents being damaged with the same attack.

### Star Powers / Blown Away

{{Quote|Mina's Super also roots enemies on the spot.}}{{StarPower|2}}
Mina's Super now roots enemies caught in it for 1.5 seconds, making them unable to move but still attack, similar to [[Lumi]]'s Super.

### Hypercharge: Triplecane

{{Quote|Super now fires 3 hurricanes in a cone shape, which can bounce.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Mina creates 3 hurricanes in a cone shape, which behave similarly to the one from her normal Super. However, the Hypercharged Supers have a faster projectile speed and can bounce off of walls, and also have a much stronger pulling effect of 5 tiles. Note that after the projectiles bounce off of the wall, the Super pulls towards the location where the projectile hit the wall, not towards Mina's position. She also gains a 20% speed boost, and a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips / Game Modes and Maps

* In maps like [[Bridge Too Far]], Mina can use Windmill effectively. Place the Gadget right on the lane to push even farther.
* In [[Brawl Arena]], Mina is an excellent pick. Use the middle lane as cover. Since Mina has good burst damage, she can either secure kills in the middle or quickly assassinate enemies that are too aggressive.

### Tips / Recommended Build

* When playing Mina in maps or game modes that favor marksmen or assassins, Blown Away is usually the better Star Power. If an assassin gets too close, use her Super to root them, then finish them with attacks. Or, as an alternative, when assassins are rooted, use her attacks to place as much distance as she can between them. Dash towards marksmen and use the Super to prevent them from getting away.

### Tips / Strategies

*Mina's attacks makes her dash in the direction she is currently facing. Use this mechanic to escape tough situations or to dodge more attacks when moving up a lane.
**However, be cautious about where Mina is facing when attacking. Bad directions might make you end up right in front of a [[Bull]], [[Griff]], [[Shelly]], [[Spike]], [[Darryl]], or a [[Leon]].
**When facing such brawlers, you can choose to not dash with your attack by briefly pausing in place right before you shoot your attack, as Mina doesn't dash when standing still.
*Due to the 0.5s windup of Mina's Attack 3, using Windmill during that period can help the ability not get interrupted by a crowd control projectile.
*Since Mina's full combo attack takes too much time to land, finish the sandal move right after using her Super. When the enemy lands, Mina can instantly deal high damage.
*Be careful when you go against enemies who have ammo-stealing capabilities such as [[Colt]], [[Mico]], and [[Pam]], because Mina needs all three ammo to use the combo and losing even one of them will make the combo more difficult to play. Try to use the combo when the opponent used their ability.

### Tips / Other

*Take note that Brawlers are launched into the sky when hit by Mina's Super, rendering them immune to any further attacks on the ground. Therefore, don't instantly use the second Super when using "Capo What?".
*Since Mina's Super acts like a pull, using it at point-blank range will make it not pull the enemy and therefore not send them into the air, making it useless against Brawlers like [[Kit]] and [[El Primo]], who can jump right on top of you and nullifying any distance.
