Eve is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who could be unlocked as a [[Brawl Pass]] reward at Tier 30 in Season 11: The Biodome. She has moderately low health, a very long range, and a variable damage output. She was the 55th Brawler added to the game. Eve's Trait allows her to move over water. For her attack, she fires out three eggs that each deal different amounts of damage. Her Super drops a nest egg which, after some time, spawns three hatchlings that chase down the nearest enemies to poison them. Her first Gadget, Gotta Go!, makes Eve jump forward as well as leaving behind a hatchling. Her second Gadget, Motherly Love, makes the hatchlings spawned from her next Super heal her allies. Her Mythic Gear, Quadruplets, makes her Super spawn an additional hatchling. Her first Star Power, Unnatural Order, makes Eve's attack shoot out the biggest egg first and the smallest egg last. Her second Star Power, Happy Surprise, spawns a hatchling when her biggest egg hits an enemy. Her Hypercharge, Generations, makes her nest egg spawn hatchlings every second before it explodes.

### Trait

{{Quote|This Brawler can move over water}}{{Trait|Water}}Eve's Trait allows her to be able to move on water.

### Attack: Egg Shooter

{{Quote|Eve shoots three eggs in a row, with each one being bigger and dealing more damage than the previous egg.}}
Eve fires three long-ranged eggs in quick succession. The eggs have different sizes, with the smallest egg fired first and the largest egg fired last. Larger eggs have a larger hitbox and deal more damage, but the Super charge rate remains the same. Eve's attack has a moderate reload speed and a very long cooldown.

### Super: Baby Boom

{{Quote|Eve releases a big egg, which incubates a group of hatchlings. Hatchlings attach to enemies and deal damage over time.}}
Eve throws out a nest egg over obstacles, and after 5 seconds, it explodes and spawns 3 hatchlings in a triangle formation around it in a radius of 4.33 tiles. If the nest egg is destroyed before it explodes, the hatchlings won't spawn. The hatchlings chase the nearest enemies, like [[Tick]]'s and [[Ash]]'s Super, and once close enough, they dash into their target, poisoning on contact to damage enemies over 4 seconds to any enemies in their path. The hatchlings also lose 10% of their health every second until they're destroyed. The hatchlings' health does not scale with Power Cubes.

### Gadgets / Gotta Go!

{{Quote|Eve jumps away and leaves behind a hatchling. Cooldown: 17 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}Eve jumps forward in the direction she's facing, similar to [[Brock]]'s Rocket Laces Gadget, allowing her to jump over walls, and leaves behind a hatchling. The hatchling has less health than a hatchling from her Super at 20% of Eve's maximum health, and its poison duration is cut in half. If required to cross over a larger amount of blocks, the distance will be increased up to a limit or to the nearest piece of valid terrain. Eve can use this Gadget while on water, and the hatchling will spawn on the nearest piece of ground.

### Gadgets / Motherly Love

{{Quote|Hatchlings spawned from an active big egg will now heal allies over time instead of dealing damage. Cooldown: 11 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}Activating this Gadget while a nest egg is active allows Eve's hatchlings from her Super to heal her or allies, instead of damaging enemies. This Gadget has no effect on hatchlings already spawned. The hatchlings heal for 25% more than their damage to enemies. Eve must be within 12 tiles of an active nest egg to use this Gadget. If there is more than one nest egg active, only the first one spawned will be affected. This Gadget's cooldown starts 1 second after it is activated.

### Star Powers / Unnatural Order

{{Quote|Reverses the order of the eggs shot by her egg shooter.}}{{StarPower|1}}With this Star Power equipped, Eve shoots the largest egg first and the smallest egg last.

### Star Powers / Happy Surprise

{{Quote|Every 5 seconds, the largest egg hitting an enemy from her Egg Shooter will spawn a hatchling.}}{{StarPower|2}}Eve gains a charge bar which takes 5 seconds to charge up, and when charged if Eve hits an enemy with the largest egg from her attack, a projectile will bounce forward 3.33 tiles and spawn a hatchling. The hatchling has roughly 30% of the health of a hatchling from her Super and its poison duration is halved. The bar gets used up when the largest egg is fired. This Star Power does not work against the [[Heist]] safe, the IKE turret in [[Siege]], or spawnables.

### Hypercharge: Generations

{{Quote|Baby Boom now spawns hatchlings over time!}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Eve's nest egg will decay by 16% of its maximum health per second until destroyed, but will last for up to 6 seconds if it doesn't take any damage. It spawns a hatchling every 0.85 seconds, spawning 6 hatchlings over the decay duration in a triangle pattern if it takes no damage, in addition to the hatchlings spawned upon expiring. The Hypercharge also modifies the Super projectile to take the same time to land regardless of the distance thrown. She also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips / Game Modes and Maps

* On maps with a lot of lakes like [[Island Invasion]], [[Flowing Springs]] or [[Sapphire Plains]], Eve can travel across the lakes to reach enemies faster and prevent taking much damage.

### Tips / Recommended Build

* Eve's Unnatural Order Star Power allows Eve to fire the biggest egg first. This allows her to deal more immediate damage. This also allows Eve to have slightly more range.
* Eve's Happy Surprise Star Power can be good against single-target Brawlers like [[Piper]], [[Byron]], [[Bea]], Fang, [[Pam]], [[Angelo]], [[Mandy]], [[Cordelius]], and [[Bonnie]] while in her cannon form, as they have no way to effectively deal with a hatchling invasion.

### Tips / Other

*Eve has moderate health, but Brawlers like [[Edgar]], [[Leon]], [[Mortis]], [[Crow]], Kenji, [[Buzz]], [[Spike]], or [[Mico]] can defeat her with ease because of Eve's slow unload speed. Her Trait perfectly counters this as short-ranged Brawlers like Edgar, [[Kenji]], [[Lily]], El Primo, Mico, and Mortis cannot reach her in a sufficiently large lake.
*Because of Eve's slow unload and reload speed, she can struggle in 1v1 situations with Brawlers with a high damage burst potential like [[Fang]], [[El Primo]], Buzz, [[Stu]], [[Emz]], [[Griff]], and Edgar, so you should play her passively from a long range, poking in and out of cover similarly to [[Mr. P]].
*Because Eve's Super takes five seconds to crack open, you may think it's a good idea to place it in the map's backline. However, the hatchlings' health decreases over time, so try to use it decently far, but not too far from enemies.
