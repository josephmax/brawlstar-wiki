{{Quote|When it comes to Karaoke, Melodie shows no mercy. Armed with her kawaii looks and devilish set of pipes, she never fails to steal the limelight.}}{{Portrait}}
Melodie is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who has great mobility with her Super, a long range, moderate health, and a variable damage output. She was the 78th Brawler added to the game. Her attack is a long-ranged monster note that deals very low damage. When she hits a target, notes appear and revolve around her, each dealing moderately high damage. Her Super has three dashes that can be individually used. Her first Gadget, Perfect Pitch, temporarily increases her notes' rotation speed and radius. Her second Gadget, Interlude, provides Melodie a shield based on the number of notes that revolve around her, which absorbs damage and decays over time. Her first Star Power, Fast Beats, increases Melodie's movement speed based on the number of notes that revolve around her. Her second Star Power, Extended Mix, increases her notes' duration. Her Hypercharge, Flash Mob, allows her Super to spawn a Hypercharge note around her after every use.

### Attack: Monster Notes

{{Quote|Melodie shoots tiny monster notes from her mic, damaging enemies and spawning orbiting notes around her on hit.}}
Melodie shoots a long-ranged monster note that deals very low damage. Upon hitting a target, a note revolves around her in a radius of 2 tiles, dealing double the damage of a long range attack note if they hit an enemy. Melodie can have a maximum of 3 notes orbiting her at any time. Her notes will disappear in the order they spawned after 8 seconds. She can also charge her Super and Hypercharge by attacking with the notes. When entering or exiting [[Cordelius]]' Shadow Realm, existing orbiting notes disappear, and Melodie has to hit to spawn them again. When hitting an enemy when there already is three notes, the duration of the note with the shortest remaining duration will be refreshed. Hitting an enemy with the notes charges as much Super as hitting an enemy with the main attack.

### Super: Catchy Chorus

{{Quote|Melodie can dash forward multiple times to catch her enemies.}}
Melodie dashes forward a distance of 5.33 tiles. She can use her Super thrice after it is charged. A number under the Super icon indicates how many dashes are left to be used. Her Super can only be recharged after she uses all three dashes. Additionally, if Melodie respawns for any reason after using one or two of her dashes, she will lose any remaining dashes and have to charge her Super again.

### Gadgets / Perfect Pitch

{{Quote|Melodie's notes get 25% increased movement speed and 60% wider orbit for 5 seconds. Cooldown: 14 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
Upon activation, Melodie's monster notes' rotation speed is increased by 25%, and their orbit radius is increased by 60% for 5 seconds.

### Gadgets / Interlude

{{Quote|Melodie gains 10% Shield from each note orbiting her. Cooldown: 19 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}[[File:Hardcore Icon.png|left|40x40px|link=]]When Melodie has a monster note that revolves around her, she receives a shield that initially blocks the 10% of her health dealt to her. The shield's health is increased by an additional 10%, if there are more notes, up until the shield blocks 30% (60% with her Hypercharge) of her health dealt to her. However, despite the description, the increase in Melodie's health due to [[Brawl Arena]] level ups do not increase the shield amount. The shield decays by 5% every 0.35 seconds, so the damage it can block decreases over time. If an attack deals more damage than the shield's current health, the shield disappears and Melodie is damaged by the remaining damage. [[Colette]]'s attack and Super will deal damage based on Melodie's max health when this shield is active. Enemies damaging Melodie with Interlude can still charge their Supers.

### Star Powers / Fast Beats

{{Quote|Melodie gains 6% increased movement speed from each note orbiting her.}}{{StarPower|1}}
When Melodie has a monster note that revolves around her, her movement speed is increased by 6%. Her movement speed is increased by the same amount if there are more notes, up to a maximum of 18% (36% with her Hypercharge) speed boost.

### Star Powers / Extended Mix

{{Quote|Melodie's notes last for 25% longer before vanishing.}}{{StarPower|2}}
With this Star Power equipped, Melodie's notes last 25% longer, increasing their duration from 8 to 10 seconds.

### Hypercharge: Flash Mob

{{Quote|Melodie adds up to 3 Hypercharge notes using Catchy Chorus. Hypercharge notes do 504 damage.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, every time Melodie uses a Super during the duration of the Hypercharge, a Hypercharge note revolves around her in a radius of 2 tiles, which acts identically to a regular note but has a reduced damage of 480, or 504 damage with the Hypercharge damage boost. Melodie can have a maximum of 3 Hypercharge notes orbiting her at any time, which overrides the normal note limit, allowing her to have up to 6 notes. The Hypercharge notes also affect Melodie's Interlude Gadget and Fast Beats Star Power, but cannot have their duration extended with the Extended Mix Star Power. She also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips / Game Modes and Maps

*Melodie in [[Brawl Ball]] is able to deal damage when she has the ball. Since her damage output with her monster notes is where the majority of the damage comes from, she is extremely viable for Brawl Ball.
*Melodie can dribble/pass the ball in Brawl Ball and Basket Brawl even more effectively than [[Mortis]] or [[Kenji]] since she uses her Super instead of her ammo to dash, which allows her to bring the ball across the map in a few seconds.
*Try not to play maps where there are a lot of water and walls because they can easily interrupt the Super dash.
*The Super dashes' extremely long total length allows her to conduct a surprise attack against the opponent's [[Heist]] safe because she can approach it very quickly from a far distance.

### Tips / Recommended Build

* The Interlude Gadget is more often the more useful Gadget because Melodie's playstyle involves dashing to the opponent with notes where having a shield is more beneficial.
** In those cases, the Perfect Pitch Gadget often goes against the playstyle. After approaching the enemies, the enemies will likely to be close to her and making the notes further away will make it harder to hit the enemies.
** Bear in mind that the shield from the Interlude Gadget decays quite quickly, so it's often a good idea to use the Shield Gear for additional protection.
*The Hypercharge can increase the damage output of one rotation of the notes by almost 30%. It also doubles the effectiveness of Interlude Gadget and Fast Beats Star Power as there are twice as many notes. All of these boosts are very helpful to win many interactions.
**Do not rely on the Hypercharge notes themselves for the damage as they aren't a major source of damage. It's important to couple them with regular notes to reap the best value out of them. As such, try to activate the Hypercharge once she has three regular notes.

### Tips / Strategies

* When hitting enemies with Melodie's notes, it is better to walk around the Brawler in a counterclockwise motion to increase the number of hits per unit time. This is even easier to perform with the Fast Beats Star Power.
* When approaching enemies that fire projectile(s) in a line as an attack (e.g. [[Piper]], [[Mandy]], [[Bonnie]], [[Brock]], [[Maisie]]), try not to dash in a straight line because the enemies can autoaim and land a hit easily. Instead, try to dash in a slight zig-zag pattern to avoid the shots better.
* When engaging in head-to-head combat, it's usually better to keep a distance, land three shots to get three notes, and then dash back into the enemies. This way, she can start the combat by unlocking full damage potential.
* When going against an enemy that can do area scan damage (e.g. Maisie's, [[Shelly]]'s, and [[Frank]]'s Super), Melodie can use the Super to first dash away from the enemy to avoid that attack and then dash back in.
* When you are very close to an enemy, try to dash in such a way that she passes through the point that is next to the enemy so the notes have a chance to hit them while you are dashing.

### Tips / Other

* Melodie's notes spawn from out of her not to the side of her, meaning at point blank her note can deal damage even if it's not in the notes rotation.
