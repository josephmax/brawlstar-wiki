Sam is an {{Rarity|Epic}} [[Brawler]] who could be unlocked as a [[Brawl Pass]] reward at Tier 30 in Season 14: Robot Factory. He has a short range, very high health, and a variable damage output, movement and reload speed. He was the 59th Brawler added to the game. His Trait lets him start a match with his Super already charged. He attacks by punching enemies with two quick punches that deal more damage if Sam has his Knuckle Busters equipped. For his Super, Sam throws his Knuckle Busters or makes them fly back to him, damaging enemies in its path, altering his attack and giving him an increased movement speed. Sam can also charge his Super by picking up his Knuckle Busters from the ground. Sam's first Gadget, Magnetic Field, pulls in enemies around his Knuckle Busters. His second Gadget, Pulse Repellent, makes his next Super knock back enemies around the Knuckle Busters when they land. His first Star Power, Hearty Recovery, heals part of Sam's lost health when picking up his Knuckle Busters. His second Star Power, Remote Recharge, automatically charges Sam's Super if enemies are near his Knuckle Busters while they're on the ground. His Hypercharge, Hyper Fist, makes his Knuckle Busters travel faster, and instantly charges his Super when they damage enemies in its path.

### Trait

{{Quote|This Brawler starts with a fully charged Super.}}{{Trait|Auto}}Sam's Trait allows him to start a match or respawn with a fully charged Super.

### Attack: Heavy Haymakers

{{Quote|Sam unleashes a double dose of hard-hitting punches. With his gloves on, he deals a lot more damage.}} With his Knuckle Busters equipped, Sam attacks enemies with two short-ranged piercing punches in a wide cone, and without his Knuckle Busters equipped, Sam's attack deal less damage but attacks quicker and in a smaller spread. Sam's reload speed also changes; with his Knuckle Busters, Sam reloads slower, and he reloads faster without them equipped.

### Super: Knuckle Busters

{{Quote|Sam throws his Knuckle Busters dealing damage to all enemies hit before landing on the ground. His next Super will return them back to his hands and also deals damage.}}
If Sam has his Knuckle Busters equipped, he throws them in front of him, piercing through enemies along the way before landing on the ground. After throwing his Knuckle Busters, Sam also boosts his movement speed by 25% for 2.5 seconds. Without his Knuckle Busters, Sam can charge his Super by either dealing damage to enemies, by picking them up, or by using his Remote Recharge Star Power; activating his Super makes his Knuckle Busters fly back to him, piercing through walls and enemies along the way and damaging them for half the damage they do when leaving Sam's hands. Sam can pick up his Knuckle Busters if they are on the ground, which will instantly charge his Super. There is a 1-second cooldown after they land on the ground before Sam can pick them up, though his Magnetic Field Gadget can be activated during the cooldown. The Knuckle Busters stay on the ground until Sam picks them up, they fly back to him, when he is defeated, a round is over in [[Knockout]], or a goal is scored in [[Brawl Ball]]. If Sam uses his Super to kick the ball in Brawl Ball or in another sports mode, the ball will consume his Super but will, however, keep his Knuckle Busters in his main attack.

### Gadgets / Magnetic Field

{{Quote|While the Knuckle Busters are on the ground, Sam can activate them to pull in nearby enemies. Cooldown: 17 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
Upon activation, enemies within a small radius around Sam's Knuckle Busters will be pulled into them. The pull strength is 2 tiles and the area effect radius is 2.33 tiles. This Gadget can only be activated if Sam's Knuckle Busters are on the ground and within 14 tiles of his location. This Gadget's cooldown starts 2.5 seconds after it is activated.

### Gadgets / Pulse Repellent

{{Quote|Upon landing on the ground, Sam's next deployed Knuckle Busters releases an electric pulse that knocks back enemies. Cooldown: 11 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
Activating this Gadget allows Sam's next Super to knock back enemies in a small radius around his Knuckle Busters when they land. The knockback strength is 3 tiles and the area effect radius is 2.17 tiles. This can only be activated if Sam has his Knuckle Busters and his Super charged up. A Gadget symbol will glow above Sam's head signaling the Gadget's usage, as well as a glowing Super joystick. This Gadget's cooldown starts 2.5 seconds after it is activated.

### Star Powers / Hearty Recovery

{{Quote|Upon recalling his Knuckle Busters, Sam instantly recovers 20% of his missing health.}}{{StarPower|1}}
Every time Sam retrieves his Knuckle Busters, he heals for 20% of his missing health. The amount of health Sam can heal can be affected by outside health buffs or healing debuffs.

### Star Powers / Remote Recharge

{{Quote|While the Knuckle Busters are on the ground and near enemies, they will charge up Sam's Super.}}{{StarPower|2}}
When Sam's Knuckle Busters are on the ground, enemies in a 2.17-tile radius around them passively charge's Sam's Super, similar to [[Buzz]]'s Trait. It charges his Super and Hypercharge faster if there are multiple enemies inside the radius, and the amount of Super charge per second is 5% multiplied by one more than the number of enemies present within the circle. This results in the Super charging by 10% per second each or over 10 seconds for one enemy, and his Hypercharge by 2.5% per second each or over 40 seconds for one enemy. His Super still charges even with invisible or bush-camping enemies. Ally Sams will have their Star Power radius shown, while enemy Sams will have their Star Power radius hidden.

### Hypercharge: Hyper Fist

{{Quote|Sam's Knuckle Busters travel faster and will fully charge his Super if they hit an enemy Brawler during the Hypercharge.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Sam's Knuckle Busters will travel faster while both throwing them and returning them. The Super recharge rate is tripled while throwing them, which will instantly charge his Super upon damaging an enemy. Sam also gains a 20% speed boost, and a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips / Game Modes and Maps

*Sam's Trait makes him very useful in [[Brawl Ball]], since he starts the match with his Super charged up and unless he uses his Super, he can use that to get the ball and have a super-kick ready to go.

### Tips / Recommended Build

*Sam's Magnetic Field Gadget makes his Super amazing for area control, since he can activate the Gadget when enemies bunch up together in the [[Hot Zone]], for example; to then kill them easily.
*Sam's Pulse Repellant Gadget is also very good for area control, since he can get enemies away from a spot, like the Gem Spawn in [[Gem Grab]], without much difficulty. Pulse Repellant is also good for close up fights. By throwing your Knuckle Busters at a pinned enemy against a wall, to knock them back.
*Sam's Hearty Recovery Star Power can be game-changing in maps with lots of walls, which Sam can use to throw the Knuckle Busters to and then instantly grab them. Repeating this process many times will put Sam to full health after a fight in a matter of seconds.
*Sam's Remote Recharge Star Power can help Sam get his Knuckle Busters back in open maps or in situations where can't just go and get them normally.

### Tips / Other

*When Sam attacks with his Knuckle Busters on he hits twice, first to the very left of the cone range of the attack and then to the very right. Moving counter-clockwise around him can effectively make one of his attacks miss, so to counter that - to make both punches land - don't stand too close to the enemy and move clockwise around the enemy.
*Since Sam's ammo recharges faster without his Knuckle Busters, try reloading all his ammo before retrieving his Knuckle Busters.
*Since Sam starts the game with his Super already charged up and it gives him a short speed boost after using it, he can use his Super at the very start of a game to get to a specific part of the map or to reach a specific thing faster than other [[:Category:Brawlers|Brawlers]] such as the Gem Mine, the Blue [[Bounty]] Star, etc. Be careful however, because throwing your Knuckle Busters close to where enemies are can make them difficult to collect. Due to this, this strategy works best in [[Showdown]] due to the large amount of space in Showdown maps.
*Sam's Knuckle Busters do not extend their range if their endpoint is water, making it possible to be unable to manually collect them. Be careful throwing them around bodies of water, as accidently throwing the Knuckle Busters into large enough water bodies will force Sam to fight without them until he can recall them, greatly hindering his damage output and making his Gadgets useless. This can be partially remedied with his Remote Recharge Star Power if the location of the water is close to enemies.
