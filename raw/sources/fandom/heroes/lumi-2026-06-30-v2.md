Lumi is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who has moderate health, a long range, and a variable damage output. She was the 90th Brawler added to the game. She attacks by throwing maces at her opponents and recalling them to fly back to her. For her Super, she creates three circular explosive areas with increasing size and damage. Enemies hit by the biggest explosive area have their movement temporarily stunned while still being able to attack. Her first Gadget, Hit the Lights, leaves a burning area on the ground around her maces, burning enemies if they touch it. Her second Gadget, Grim and Frostbitten, creates an area of ice that slows down enemies and makes maneuvering harder. Her first Star Power, 42% Burnt, makes her Super leave a burning area on the ground, burning enemies temporarily if they touch or stay on the area affected by the fire. Her second Star Power, Half-time, makes her attack temporarily slow enemies down when the maces fly back. Her Hypercharge, Drum Solo, adds one more explosive area on each side of the biggest explosion.

### Attack: Good Morning... Star

{{Quote|Lumi throws one of her morning stars. She can throw both of her weapons, with her third attack recalling them, dealing damage to any enemies caught in their path!}}Lumi throws her morning star at a long range that pierces and damages enemies while flying forward, placing a mace on the floor where the attack stopped. Her attack doesn't reload like normal Brawlers; she only has 2 ammo slots that do not automatically reload. After using both ammo, Lumi will have only one ammo yellow ammo slot; using it makes her maces fly back to her, damaging and piercing through both enemies and terrain along the way. This also sets her ammo count back to 2. They deal more damage and fly faster when returning than when they fly forward. If [[Buster]]'s Super deflects Lumi's mace, the damage will be blocked, but the mace will still be placed on the floor. The maces do not have a length limit to be recalled; Lumi can make them come from one side of the arena to another, though it would take more time for the maces to return. While maces cannot go through walls while they are thrown, they can go through walls when being recalled. In [[Brawl Ball]], Lumi can shoot the ball without consuming ammo. If Lumi attacks and has less than 1 ammo slot remaining, but there are not 2 maces on the floor due to her ammo being stolen, her ammo will be restored to 1 slot once the mace is placed. When both maces are on the floor, her ammo will immediately be reloaded to full if stolen.

### Super: Blast Beat

{{Quote|Lumi pounds the ground three times, causing explosions that deal area damage. The third explosion briefly snares enemies, making them unable to move but still able to attack.}}Lumi creates 3 circular explosions in a straight line in front of her in quick succession, staggered at 0.3-second intervals. Each of them deal damage based on the size of the explosion; the first explosion is the smallest and deals the least damage, while the third one is the biggest and deals the most damage. These explosions can go through walls, but not destroy them, and can affect multiple enemies. In order, the explosions are 1.8, 2.67, and 3.67 tiles wide. The spawn locations of the explosions shift with Lumi as she moves, and their travel time is essentially instant. If the second and third explosions are aimed so that they would go off the edge of the map, they will be offset away from the edge up to a limit, or if past this limit, not spawn entirely.

Additionally, enemies hit by the third explosion will become rooted, indicated by a vibrating yellow ring around the target. The third explosion's area effect is active for 0.7 seconds. Enemies caught in the radius and those who walk into it while it is still active are rooted for 1 second on hit, and the duration can be extended up to 1.7 seconds if the enemy remains standing in the area. Rooted enemies will be unable to move normally, but can still attack. An enemy's dash or charge can be interrupted by a root, but enemies can still use their dashes or charges while rooted.

### Gadgets / Hit the Lights

{{Quote|Lumi iginites her morning stars on the ground, producing flames which damage nearby enemies for 3.9 seconds. Damage per second: 360. Cooldown: 14 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}When activated, Lumi's maces each leave a burning area on the ground that lasts for 3.9 seconds. The damage per second is 30% of Lumi's attack damage when thrown. This area has a radius of 2 tiles, and the damage can hit 4 times if the enemy remains standing on the area. However, if the maces are placed on top of each other, the damage won't stack. This Gadget can still be used if Lumi has maces that are not on the ground; this will cause Lumi to create a flame area around herself. The Gadget can also be used while throwing or recalling maces, which will cause the area effect to spawn at the mace's location at the time of the Gadget's activation.

### Gadgets / Grim and Frostbitten

{{Quote|Lumi creates freezing winds around her morning stars on the ground, creating a slippery surface for 3.9 secs. Cooldown: 23 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}When activated, Lumi's maces each create a small icy, slippery area on the ground, similar to [[Lou]]'s Super (although it will not fill an allied Lou's Freeze meter). This area has a radius of 2.33 tiles. Any enemy who changes direction while moving on it will have difficulty maneuvering or escaping. This doesn't affect Lumi or allies. This Gadget can still be used if Lumi has maces that are not on the ground; this will cause Lumi to create an ice area around herself. The Gadget can also be used while throwing or recalling maces, which will cause the area effect to spawn at the mace's location at the time of the Gadget's activation.

### Star Powers / 42% Burnt

{{Quote|The explosions from Lumi's Blast Beat also set the area on fire for 4 seconds. Fire damage also scales with explosion damage. Damage per second for the biggest explosion: 560.}}{{StarPower|1}}Lumi's Super leaves a burning area on the ground that lasts for 4 seconds on each explosion. Each fire deals 20% of an explosion's damage per tick, and it can hit 4 times if the enemy remains standing on the area.

### Star Powers / Half-time

{{Quote|Lumi slows enemies hit by her recalled morning stars for 1 seconds.}}{{StarPower|2}}When Lumi's maces hit enemies when being recalled, enemies that have been hit will be slowed down by 30% for 1 second.

### Hypercharge: Drum Solo

{{Quote|The final explosion from Lumi's Super has an extra blast on both sides.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Lumi's Super gains one more explosion on each side of the biggest explosive area that similarly makes the enemies not move for 1 second on hit. If you use a Super and activate a Hypercharge right after using Super, the side explosions will appear too. She also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips

*When throwing the maces, try to keep enemies at the tip of your range to make it easier to hit them with the recalled maces. This is especially important when throwing the second one, as if done right, Lumi can instantly recall the second mace after it hits for an additional burst of damage.
*Against close range assassins such as [[Edgar]], [[Fang]], [[Sam]], [[Mico]], [[Kenji]], [[Shade]], [[Mortis]], [[Lily]], [[Alli]], and [[Buzz]], do not throw the maces far, as this will result in Lumi losing valuable damage due to the maces long travel time. Instead, in a similar manner to [[Carl]] and [[Charlie]], shoot the maces against a nearby wall to increase the amount you can throw.
*When Lumi recalls the maces, they can go through walls and obstacles. This can be crucial when opponents only need one more hit to be defeated but are hiding behind a wall. She can take advantage of this by throwing her maces and moving towards the enemy behind the wall before they return. allowing them to get an indirect hit.
*The fire from her Hit the Lights Gadget is very effective at restricting where an enemy is willing to go, often cutting off their escape route.
*Her Grim and Frostbitten Gadget can cover zones in [[Hot Zone]]. She can use it to her and team's advantage as enemies can't enter the hot zones without slowing down and being quickly defeated with ease from Lumi and her teammates.
*The Grim and Frostbitten Gadget can also be used to counter some assassins. If playing against Brawlers with a jumping Super and close range like Edgar, [[Kit]], [[Bonnie]], and [[El Primo]], make sure you haven’t thrown your maces, wait for them to jump, walk towards the jump while they are in the air, while using the Gadget so it leaves the slippery frost area behind, preventing them from moving, making it easier for you to kill them.
