{{Quote|Larry sells tickets to Starr Park visitors under the watchful eye of his twin, Lawrie. Larry loves rules, they make life easier! Lawrie doesn't love rules so much as enforcing them. They make a good team.}}{{Portrait}}
Larry & Lawrie is an {{Rarity|Epic}} [[Brawler]] who has moderately low health, a fast movement speed, and a high burst damage potential, at the expense of having a very slow reload speed. They were the 76th Brawler added to the game. Larry attacks by lobbing a bundle of tickets at a long range that explodes onto the ground in a small radius the first time and then explodes again in a larger radius, dealing damage to enemies hit by the blast twice. For his Super, Larry throws a signal that spawns his twin brother Lawrie, who has moderately low health and attacks by firing 2 waves consisting of 3 plugs in one wave, and 5 plugs in the other that spread out in a cone. His first Gadget, Order: Swap, makes Larry and Lawrie swap their attacks. His second Gadget, Order: Fall Back, makes Larry and Lawrie quickly dash to swap sides, healing them in the process. His first Star Power, Protocol: Protect, passively reduces some damage Larry takes, redirecting that same amount of damage done to Lawrie instead. His second Star Power, Protocol: Assist, reloads some of Larry's ammo when Lawrie deals damage to an enemy. His Hypercharge, The Three-Bot Problem, spawns a second Lawrie that has more health and deals more damage than the normal Lawrie.

### Attack: Ticket Dispenser

{{Quote|Shoot a ticket bundle over obstacles that explodes twice after landing, damaging nearby enemies.}}
Larry lobs a bundle of tickets at a long range that explodes in a small radius of 1.5 tiles the first time, and then after 0.6 seconds explodes in a larger radius of 2.67 tiles the second time, dealing moderate damage to enemies hit. Similar to [[Barley]], the damage can hit twice if the enemy stands in the radius of both explosions. However, his attacks reload rather slowly and travel slowly as well. Larry's attack will land on the ground sooner the closer he throws his ticket bundle to himself.

### Super: Call For Backup

{{Quote|Throw a signal to summon your twin Lawrie for help. He will seek out any trouble makers and serve justice with his taser gun.}}
Larry throws a signal that spawns his twin brother Lawrie who chases down and attacks enemies. Lawrie walks towards the nearest enemy even if they are in a bush, but unlike other spawnables, walks erratically instead of beelining directly towards the enemy and generally tries to stay near Larry. His attack comes in 2 waves, one wave consists of 3 plugs and the other 5 plugs, with the plugs spreading out in a cone formation. The order of these waves reverses with each attack, and the 3-plug wave is set to go first when Lawrie spawns. Lawrie's attack deals heavy amounts of damage at close range, where most or all of the plugs can hit an enemy. Lawrie will follow Larry if there are no enemies that are able to be attacked. Auto Aiming the Super will spawn Lawrie right next to Larry.

### Gadgets / Order: Swap

{{Quote|Swap weapons with Lawrie. Cooldown: 12 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
When activated, Larry and Lawrie swap their respective weapons, and Lawrie instantly fully reloads his ammo. The effect will remain active until another Gadget is used or until death. When Larry uses Lawrie's attack, the wave with 3 plugs will always go first. If one of the twins dies, that twin's weapon will revert back to normal, but the other will still keep the modified weapon. This Gadget can only be used if Lawrie exists on the map. This Gadget's cooldown begins after it's activated.

### Gadgets / Order: Fall Back

{{Quote|Dash towards Lawrie while Lawrie dashes towards you, healing both for 25% of max health. Cooldown: 17 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
When activated, Larry and Lawrie quickly dash to swap their respective positions. They can't use their dash to go through walls or over water, and the dash will be cut short if they try to do so. In addition, they heal for 25% of their maximum health. This Gadget's cooldown begins 0.5 seconds after it's activated.

### Star Powers / Protocol: Protect

{{Quote|While Lawrie is in range, transfer 30% incoming damage to himself instead.}}{{StarPower|1}}
When Lawrie is within 8 tiles of Larry, this Star Power passively reduces all damage Larry takes by 30% by redirecting the damage to Lawrie instead. It also reduces the Super charge enemies get from attacks by the same amount. The rotating beacon light on Larry's head will glow blue to show this Star Power is active.

### Star Powers / Protocol: Assist

{{Quote|While Lawrie is in range, reload 30% of your ammo whenever he damages an enemy.}}{{StarPower|2}}
When Lawrie is within 8 tiles of Larry, this Star Power passively reloads Larry's ammo by 30% whenever Lawrie's attacks hit enemy Brawlers. The amount Larry reloads increases with the more projectiles Lawrie lands. Unlike other abilities, this Star Power reloads 30% of one ammo per hit, not 30% of the entire ammo bar. The rotating beacon light on Lawrie's head will glow red to show this Star Power is active.

### Hypercharge: The Three-Bot Problem

{{Quote|Larry calls an extra prototype Lawrie bot for backup, which deals more damage and has higher health than plain ol' Lawrie. Gadgets that affect Lawrie only apply to the "original" model.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Larry spawns a second Lawrie that has 2000 more health. This Lawrie always spawns clockwise of the normal Lawrie relative to Larry. Despite the description, the second Lawrie does not deal extra damage compared to the normal one. There can be two Lawries active at once, and using another normal Super will only despawn the normal Lawrie. Larry & Lawrie's Order: Swap and Order: Fallback Gadgets only affect the normal Lawrie. Larry also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips / Game Modes and Maps

*Larry and Lawrie's decent range, great area control, and potent spawnable make them excellent in modes where control over long periods of time is the priority, such as [[Gem Grab]] and [[Hot Zone]].

### Tips / Recommended Build

*The Order: Fall Back Gadget is best used as a more tactical movement use for whether you're in [[Knockout]] or [[Showdown]]. If you spawn your twin brother Lawrie towards the enemy, they might play less aggressive considering you're the main target. Thus, you can swap places given the right opponent and fast attack them unexpectedly. This can be very useful in tight situations.
*The Order: Swap Gadget is not recommended, as while Lawrie can utilize Larry's Ticket Dispenser well, receiving Lawrie's Shocker neuters Larry's long range and damage. This makes Larry essentially useless against enemy throwers and medium range Brawlers like [[Pearl]], [[Surge]], [[Emz]], [[Tara]], [[Nita]], and [[Spike]], and the low damage of the weapon means he struggles to take down even close range Brawlers.
*Protocol: Protect is useful to defend against aggressive melee Brawlers like [[Fang]], [[Bull]], [[Shelly]], [[Darryl]], [[Rosa]], or [[El Primo]], making it a hard task to take him down. Against area damage melee Brawlers like [[Kenji]], [[Kit]], [[Shade]], [[Mico]], [[Mortis]], [[Edgar]], [[Ash]], [[Bibi]], [[Jacky]], [[Hank]], [[Sam]], [[Doug]], [[Draco]], [[Lily]], and [[Buzz]], keep some distance between you and Lawrie to ensure that Lawrie will not take increased damage from their attacks.
*Protocol: Assist is very effective, especially when paired with Order: Fall Back. You can continuously keep attacking as the Star Power boosts your reload speed when you're close to Lawrie and he deals damage, while healing each other and swapping positions when you use Order: Fall Back. Given that Larry's reload speed is slow, it's recommended to use this Star Power to boost his reload speed while dealing large damage.

### Tips / Other

*To maximize damage, try aiming ahead of an enemy. This is because Larry's attack explodes twice, so this increases you chances of the attack damaging the enemy twice. 
*Aiming ahead is also beneficial as Larry's projectile speed is rather slow, so aiming ahead will increase your chances of hitting the enemy. 
*Try spawning Lawrie closer to the enemy, or, if possible, in a bush. This will prevent the enemy from destroying him as easily or quickly, which will allow you to maximize his damage potential. 
*Sometimes, players will mistake Lawrie for the player (in a similar manner to [[Leon]]'s Clone Projector Gadget), which can distract them and/or cause them to waste ammo.
*Lawrie can act as a meat shield, especially if paired with the Protocol: Protect Star Power.
