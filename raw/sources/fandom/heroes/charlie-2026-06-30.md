Charlie is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who could be unlocked as a [[Brawl Pass]] reward at Tier 30 in Season 21: Bizarre Circus. Charlie has moderate health, a low damage output, and a long range, but immense utility with her Super. She was the 73rd Brawler added to the game. When she attacks, Charlie slings her yo-yo, dealing damage to any enemies hit while the yo-yo is flying forward or traveling back to Charlie, and consuming her only ammo which reloads when the yo-yo returns. For her Super, Charlie fires a bundle of hair that allows her to temporarily trap enemy Brawlers in a cocoon. Her first Gadget, Spiders, summons three spiders with low health and damage that chase down enemies, and have quick melee attacks. Her second Gadget, Personal Space, allows Charlie to spawn in a cocoon, and heals her when used. Her first Star Power, Digestive, decreases the enemy Brawler's health when they get hit by her Super. Her second Star Power, Slimy, makes her Super drop puddles of slime that slow enemies down. Her Hypercharge, Pestilence, summons three spiders around her Super that behave identically to her first Gadget.

### Attack: Yo-yo

{{Quote|Charlie slings her yo-yo forward, damaging the first enemy hit and then returning back to her.}}
Charlie slings a long-ranged yo-yo that deals damage to enemies while flying forward and when returning to her. Similar to [[Carl]], the yo-yo can return to Charlie through walls, but can't be slung through walls. If Charlie slings her yo-yo against a wall or an enemy, the travel time of her yo-yo is decreased. Charlie's attack doesn't reload like normal Brawlers, because once her yo-yo returns to her, Charlie reloads her only ammo bar. When Charlie respawns, her only ammo bar will reload in 2 seconds. In [[Brawl Ball]], Charlie can shoot the ball without consuming ammo. Charlie has an attack cooldown of 0.5 seconds.

### Super: Cocoon

{{Quote|Charlie throws forward a bundle of hair wrapping around an enemy on hit, disabling them until the hairy cocoon is destroyed.}}
Charlie fires a bundle of hair that, upon hitting an enemy Brawler, traps them in a cocoon. The Super ignores pets and spawnables, and the projectile will simply pass through them. The cocoon has high health, and decays by 20% of its health until it's destroyed, lasting for up to 5 seconds if it doesn't take any damage. While the enemy Brawlers are trapped, they can't move, heal, or use their main attacks, Supers, Gadgets, or Hypercharges, and in [[Gem Grab]], they will drop any Gems they have upon hit. Brawlers cannot reload while in the cocoon, but Brawlers with auto-charging Supers such as [[Darryl]], [[Edgar]], [[Kit]], or [[Chuck]] can charge their Supers. Brawlers such as [[Cordelius]], [[Buzz]], [[Lily]], or [[Shade]] can also passively charge their Supers if a cocooned enemy Brawler is within a radius of them, due to their Traits.

### Gadgets / Spiders

{{Quote|Charlie releases 3 spiders that seek and attack the nearest enemies. Cooldown: 20 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
When Charlie uses this Gadget, she summons three spiders in a triangular formation around her that follow and attack the closest enemies. They each have 20% of Charlie's maximum health, deal 50% of her damage within a range of 2 tiles every second, and decay by 20% of their health until they're destroyed, lasting for up to 5 seconds if they don't take any damage. Charlie can only have a maximum of three spiders on the map, so if she uses the Gadget again, or uses her Hypercharge to spawn more spiders, or uses the Gadget after using her Hypercharge, the previous spiders will despawn.

### Gadgets / Personal Space

{{Quote|Charlie cocoons herself, healing 50% of her total health. Cooldown: 15 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
Activating this Gadget causes Charlie to cocoon herself, which behaves identically to that of her Super. When used, she also heals for 50% of her maximum health. This Gadget's cooldown starts 2 seconds after the button is pressed.

### Star Powers / Digestive

{{Quote|Cocooned enemies lose 25% of their health while cocooned.}}{{StarPower|1}}
When the enemy Brawler gets hit by Charlie's Super, they lose 25% of their remaining health.

### Star Powers / Slimy

{{Quote|Cocoon leaves a slimy trail behind it for 5 seconds that slows down enemies walking over it.}}{{StarPower|2}}
Charlie's Super drops puddles of slime on the ground that follow the Super's travel path. The trail of slime is 2 tiles wide. The puddles last for 5 seconds each, and slow enemies down by 350 points for 1.05 seconds on contact.

### Hypercharge: Pestilence

{{Quote|Cocoon spawns 3 spiders that seek and attack the nearest enemies.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Charlie's Super summons three spiders in a triangular formation around the cocoon that behave identically to those of her Spiders Gadget. The spiders will spawn even if Charlie misses her Super. She also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips

*When an enemy Brawler is in a cocoon, [[Penny]] can either splash onto your or onto her teammate, which can get the enemy out of the cocoon faster. Because of this, when facing a Penny in Duo Showdown or 3v3, it is better to not be close to the cocoon.
*The cocoon can be used for a teammate like [[Shelly]], [[Griff]], [[Spike]], or [[Leon]] to catch up and wait for the enemy to be released. After that, the teammate can quickly burst and kill the enemy.
*Since Charlie's attack comes back after hitting one target, it is more reliable to use auto-aim than [[Carl]].
*Charlie's Super can cancel out any Super, so when a Brawler like [[Bull]], [[Buzz]], [[Fang]], [[Kit]], [[Berry]], [[El Primo]], [[Crow]], [[Surge]], or [[Chuck]] is attempting to come towards you/your team, you can use her Super to save you and/or your teammates from harm.
**Using the Super when someone is chasing you also gives you time to heal up.
*Charlie's Spiders Gadget can be used to follow enemies that are hiding in bushes. It can also be used to tank shots from single target Brawlers such as [[Piper]], [[Nani]], [[Bea]], [[8-Bit]], [[Colt]], [[Pam]], or [[Mandy]].
*Charlie's Super can be used in [[Gem Grab]] to disarm the Gem carrier, which makes them drop the Gems. In [[Brawl Ball]], her Super is handy, as she can disarm the ball carrier.
*Since Charlie's ammo reloads after her yo-yo returns, she attacks much quicker at close range. This makes her an effective counter to short range Brawlers like [[Mortis]], [[Kenji]], [[Buzz]], [[Lily]], [[Ash]], [[Draco]], [[Mico]], [[Shade]], [[Bibi]], or [[Edgar]], If the enemy is a longer range Brawler, using her Super can allow her to close the distance on them and do very high damage to them once they escape.
*Charlie's Hypercharge can spawn 3 spiders even if the Super doesn't hit the target, so use it at long range.
