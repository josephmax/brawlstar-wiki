## Visible Page Text

Shelly is the Starting Brawler that is unlocked upon beginning the game.

She has moderate health, a high damage output at close range, a long range, and a fast movement speed.

Her attack is a burst of shotgun shells that deal high damage up close.

Her Super pierces and knocks surviving enemies back and destroys obstacles, similarly dealing high damage up close.

The page also includes usage notes that describe Shelly as strong in Brawl Ball, good at bush checking, and effective against tanks and close-range assassins when her Super is available.
