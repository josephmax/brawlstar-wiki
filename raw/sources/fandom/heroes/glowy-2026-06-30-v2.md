Glowy (formerly Glowbert) is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who has moderate health, a long range, and can provide consistently low burst damage to enemies and healing to allies, but has immense utility with his Super. He was the 99th Brawler added to the game. His trait allows him to charge his Super by healing himself and his allies. His attack is a long-ranged glow beam that damages enemies and heals allies over time. For his Super, Glowy reveals his face in a wide cone that deals low damage while also temporarily scaring them. Feared enemies will run away, have their movement speed slowed down, and can't use their attacks, Super or Hypercharge. His first Gadget, Slippery Savior, he quickly dashes and heals himself and nearby allies. His second Gadget, More Lumens!, temporarily doubles the attack's damage/healing. His first Star Power, Biotic Ecosystem, reduces the enemy's damage and increases the ally's damage when Glowy's attack is tethered to both of them simultaneously. His second Star Power, Parasitism, makes his shot heal him when he tethers to an enemy. His Hypercharge, You Need a Friend, makes his Super fire in all directions, which marks enemies that temporarily increases any damage dealt to those enemies.

### Trait

{{Quote|This Brawler charges Super from healing done.}}{{Trait|Heal SC}}
Glowy can charge his Super by healing himself and his allies, similar to [[Berry]] and [[Doug]]. The Super charge per heal on an ally is proportional to the healing done; healing an ally gives the same amount of Super charge as damaging an enemy for the same amount. Unlike charging Super through damage, the amount of Super charged through healing depends on the actual amount of healing done, not the nominal healing value. The Super charge rate of this Trait scales with Power Levels, and also with any damage buff or debuff.

### Attack: Glowbeam

{{Quote|Glowy fires a glow beam. If it hits, it creates a tether which damages enemies and heals teammates. Glowy can be tethered to an enemy and one teammate at the same time. If the tethered target is too far away or out of Glowy's line of sight, the tether breaks.}}Glowy fires a long-ranged glowbeam, creating a tether that deals a tick of damage over time to enemies every 0.2 seconds. However, if the glowbeam hits an ally, it instead heals them for health that is slightly less than the damage dealt to an enemy. The damage/healing effects don't stack, meaning you can't attack/heal the same target multiple times to increase its effect. However, attaching to a different target will switch the target that is being damaged/healed. Additionally, hitting the initial target multiple times can deal extra burst damage compared to simply using one ammo; the subsequent attacks still deal the initial damage for every hit. Glowbeams can be established on exactly one enemy and exactly one ally at the same time. 

The tether will flicker if the targets are out of range or if Glowy is behind an obstacle for 1.5 seconds, causing it to despawn, although he can reconnect to them during that time. However, while the tether is being flickered, no healing or damage will be done. If the tethered enemy is flying (e.g. [[Mico]] or [[Alli]]'s jumping attack), the damage will be momentarily stopped and resume once they are back on ground; the tether will still be retained.

### Super: Creep from the Deep

{{Quote|Glowy reveals his face, causing any enemies facing him to become feared, and run away! He will also fire damaging and slowing projectiles.}}After a 0.325-second delay, Glowy reveals his face, dealing low damage and piercing through enemies in a very wide cone shape. Feared enemies will run away and their movement speed will be slowed down by 40% for 2 seconds, and they can't use their attacks, Super or Hypercharge. However, only enemies facing Glowy's direction will become feared, but the damage and slow effect still apply. The Super does not apply to enemies that were flying when the Super were used.

### Gadgets / Slippery Savior

{{Quote|Glowy dashes in a target direction, healing himself and teammates 2500 HP on arrival. Cooldown: 17 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
Glowy dashes in the aimed direction (or the direction he's facing when the gadget is pressed) for up to 4.33 tiles and, after finishing the dash, he heals himself and allies in a 3.33-tile radius around himself, for 32% of Glowy's maximum health. However, if Glowy's dash get interrupted due to stuns, walls or water, then Glowy will not heal himself and his teammates.

### Gadgets / More Lumens!

{{Quote|Glowy supercharges his glowbeam tethers, doubling damage/healing for 2.5 secs. Cooldown: 13 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}When activated, Glowy halves the time between each tick of damage/heal from the tether, from 0.2 seconds to 0.1 seconds, for 2.5 seconds.

### Star Powers / Biotic Ecosystem

{{Quote|If Glowy is tethered to an enemy and teammate at the same time, it will reduce enemy damage by 20% and increase the teammates damage by 20%.}}{{StarPower|1}}
When Glowy is tethered to an enemy and an ally simultaneously, it decreases the damage output of the enemy by 20%, while increasing the damage output of the ally by the same amount.

### Star Powers / Parasitism

{{Quote|Tethering to an enemy also heals Glowy for 50% of the damage dealt.}}{{StarPower|2}}
When tethered to an enemy, Glowy will heal for 50% of the damage dealt by his tether, but not including the initial hit.

### Hypercharge: You Need a Friend

{{Hypercharge}}
{{Quote|Super now surrounds Glowy, and any enemies hit will be feared! Feared targets now take 25% extra damage from all sources.}}
When activated, Glowy fires his Super in all directions and fears any enemy hit, no matter the direction they're facing. Feared enemies will also be marked for 2 seconds, making the damage taken by them increase by 25% from any source. However, the radius will be slightly shorter than the normal Super. Glowy also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.
