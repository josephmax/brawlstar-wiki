{{Quote|A cursed puppet who brings an unsettling vibe, Gigi and her bonded magician's glove are never found apart. You can find her in the Oddities Shop, dancing between the bookshelves.}}{{Portrait}}
Gigi is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who has a short range, moderate health, a consistently high damage output, a very fast movement speed, and an extremely fast reload speed. She was the 97th Brawler added to the game. Her Trait allows her to passively charge her Super if an enemy Brawler's projectile is within a radius of her. For her attack, Gigi is momentarily sent into a continuous crazy spin, increasing movement speed and allowing her to damage enemies she gets close to for its duration. For her Super, she teleports to a target after a short delay, dealing damage to enemies when she returns back. Her first Gadget, Longer Strings, temporarily increases her Trait radius. Her second Gadget, Disappearing Act, temporarily places her circus tent that turns her teammates and her invisible within her area-of-effect. Her first Star Power, Plié Protection, increases her Super charge rate after not taking damage. Her second Star Power, A Helping Hand, heals her when she teleports with her Super. Her Hypercharge, Pas de Deux, instantly deals damage when Gigi teleports to the other teleporter.

### Trait

{{Quote|This Brawler charges their Super from staying close to opponent projectiles and melee attacks.}}{{Trait|Dodge}}Gigi's Trait is a 2.4-tile radius around her. The moment the hitbox of a projectile enters the radius, Gigi charges 6% of her Super. The Super charge counted per projectile, so Brawlers who attack with multiple projectiles will cause Gigi to charge the Super multiple times (such as [[Spike]]'s attack). This includes projectiles that deal no damage (e.g. [[Damian]]'s and [[Edgar]]'s Gadget projectile). This Trait doesn't work with game objects such as the ball in [[Brawl Ball]] (even if an enemy shot it), Brawlers who use themselves as a projectile such as [[Mortis]] and [[Mico]], and attacks with no travel time such as [[Jacky]]'s, [[Kit]]'s, and [[Shade]]'s. Ally Gigis will have their Trait radius shown, while enemy Gigis will have their Trait radius hidden.

### Attack: Pirouette Power-up

{{Quote|Gigi spins, dealing damage to all enemies around her, and gains movement speed while spinning.}}
Similar to [[Carl]]'s Super, Gigi continuously spins around, dealing damage to all targets she's close to, including enemies behind walls. Gigi's movement speed is increased by 35% while using her attack. There is no direction of travel to this attack, so aiming is not required. If the player taps on the attack joystick, Gigi only attacks once, and if the player aims the attack joystick, Gigi continuously attacks. Gigi stops attacking when the player lets go of the joystick or her ammo bar is depleted. Her ammo drains when she attacks and she automatically reloads the bar when not attacking. When holding the attack, the hit frequency starts slowly and progressively accelerates the less ammo she has. If the attack isn't stopped, the attack takes 1.2 seconds to complete.

### Super: Shadow Puppet

{{Quote|Gigi charges up for 0.8 sec, then appears at the targeted area. Gigi can charge up again for 3 sec to return to her starting position, dealing damage when she leaves.}}
Gigi creates 2 teleporters: one on top of her, and one in the direction she's aiming. After a 0.8-second delay, she teleports to the other teleporter and instantly charges her Super. Once the teleporter is activated, Gigi gains a yellow bar that takes 3 seconds to deplete. If she uses her Super again, she instantly teleports back to the first teleporter and damages enemies where she leaves in a 2.33-tile radius. If she doesn't use her Super again before the timer is up, the teleporters will despawn and she loses her Super charge.

### Gadgets / Longer Strings

{{Quote|Super charging area is bigger. Cooldown: 18 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
Activating this Gadget increases Gigi's Trait's radius by 94% (to 4.67 tiles) for 4 seconds. However, the Super charge rate will be decreased to 5% (from 6%) per projectile. This Gadget's cooldown starts after the duration ends.

### Gadgets / Disappearing Act

{{Quote|Gigi creates an area that makes herself and allies invisible while inside. Lasts 3 seconds. Cooldown: 15 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
When this Gadget is activated, Gigi drops a circus tent below her that creates an area that turns her and her allies invisible in a 2-tile radius for 3 seconds. This doesn't follow Gigi when she is moving.

### Star Powers / Plié Protection

{{Quote|Increases Super charge rate by 30% if no damage has been received for 2 secs.}}{{StarPower|1}}
After not taking damage for 2 seconds, Gigi's Super charge rate is increased by 30%.

### Star Powers / A Helping Hand

{{Quote|SHADOW PUPPET now heals for 2400 after charging up.}}{{StarPower|2}}
After teleporting initially, Gigi heals for 29.3% of her max health. However, she doesn't heal when after teleporting back to her original position.

### Hypercharge: Pas de Deux

{{Quote|Gigi's Super deals damage when appearing at a target location, as well as returning to the original location.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Gigi also instantly deals damage in an area around her when initially teleporting, in addition to when teleporting back. She also gains a 20% speed boost, and a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips / Strategies

* Attempt to charge your Super by dodging attacks from Brawlers who attack with more projectiles in one attack, such as [[8-Bit]], [[Griff]], [[Lola]], and [[Colt]], as more projectiles will enter the radius and charge your Super faster.
