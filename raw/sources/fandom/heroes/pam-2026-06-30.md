Pam is an {{Rarity|Epic}} [[Brawler]] who has high health, a long range, a fast reload speed, a high damage output at close range, and can provide a lot of healing to her team. She was the 17th/18th Brawler added to the game. Her main attack shoots a spread-out burst of scrap metal in a sweeping pattern. Her Super deploys a turret that heals her and allies within its area of effect. Her first Gadget, Pulse Modulator, triggers her turret to emit a pulse that heals anyone in its area of effect a moderate amount. Her second Gadget, Scrapsucker, allows every piece of scrap metal from her next main attack to remove ammo from any Brawler they hit while restoring Pam's ammo. Her Mythic Gear, Super Turret, increases her Super's healing. Her first Star Power, Mama's Hug, allows her to slightly heal herself and allies in range whenever she hits an enemy with a bullet from her main attack. Her second Star Power, Mama's Squeeze, allows her healing turret to deal damage to enemies within its area of effect. Her Hypercharge, Mama's Love, provides a shield that absorbs damage and decays over time when Pam or allies stay inside her Super.

### Attack: Scrapstorm

{{Quote|Pam sprays a large area with bursts of scrap metal. Safety goggles advised!}}Pam shoots nine pieces of long-ranged scrap metal in a spread-out volley that sweeps from left to right twice. This wide spread of her attack allows it to hit multiple enemies, but makes it very inconsistent and inaccurate when dealing with one single target at a range. However, at close range she can hit all her bullets and deal very high damage. This attack has a slow unload speed, and it takes 1.05 seconds to complete.

### Super: Mama's Kiss

{{Quote|Pam's healing turret will fix up her and teammates who stay in its area of effect.}}Pam's Super deploys a healing turret that heals allies by 10% of Pam's maximum health each second within a small radius similar to [[8-Bit]]'s damage booster turret. However, it can't heal spawnables like [[Jessie]]'s turret, [[Penny]]'s cannon, [[8-Bit]]'s turret, [[Nita]]'s bear, [[Mr. P]]'s home base, or another Pam's healing turret.

### Gadgets / Pulse Modulator

{{Quote|Pam triggers her turret to emit a pulse that heals allies inside the turret's range, including herself, for 2000 health. Cooldown: 12 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}Pam will instantaneously heal any allies and herself in her healing turret's area of effect for two healing ticks. She may use it even if she isn't presently standing in the turret's area of effect. However, Pam has to be within 12 tiles of the turret to activate this Gadget. This Gadget's cooldown begins after the attack is used.

### Gadgets / Scrapsucker

{{Quote|Each hit from Pam's next attack removes 25% of opposing Brawler's maximum ammo. Pam reclaims 50% of the ammo for herself. Cooldown: 17 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}Upon activation, the projectiles from Pam's next attack will each remove 25% of an enemies' maximum ammo. Meanwhile, Pam adds half of the stolen ammo to her own ammo bar. If an enemy Brawler's ammo bar is empty, Pam will not receive any more ammo even if the projectiles hit them, but Pam can still remove enemies' ammo even if her ammo bar is full. This Gadget does not work against the [[Heist]] safe, the IKE turret in [[Siege]], or spawnables, but it will work against the bosses/robots in [[Boss Fight]] and [[Super City Rampage]]/[[Robo Rumble]]. A Gadget symbol will glow above Pam's head signaling this Gadget's usage, as well as a glowing attack joystick. This Gadget's cooldown begins after the attack is used.

### Star Powers / Mama's Hug

{{Quote|Whenever Pam hits enemies with Scrapstorm, she heals herself and nearby friendly Brawlers for 100 health.}}{{StarPower|1}}Every time Pam hits an enemy with a bullet from her attack, she emits a pulse in a 4-tile radius around her that heals herself and teammates for 1% of her health. This doesn't include hits on spawnables such as an enemy [[Jessie]]'s turret, [[Nita]]'s bear, an enemy Pam's healing station, [[Tara]]'s shadows, [[Larry & Lawrie|Lawrie]], or [[Mr. P]]'s robo-porters and their home base.

### Star Powers / Mama's Squeeze

{{Quote|Healing turret now also damages enemies for 1000 damage per second.}}{{StarPower|2}}This allows her turret to do damage per second equal to 10% of Pam's maximum health if an enemy Brawler stands within the healing station's healing radius. This includes turrets and other spawnables.

### Hypercharge: Mama's Love

{{Quote|Pam's turret heals on deploy. It continues to heal Brawlers over their max health by providing them with a decaying shield.}}{{Hypercharge}}[[File:Hardcore Icon.png|left|40x40px|link=]]
When activated, Pam's turret heals on deploy worth three regular healing ticks. This instant healing radius is equal to the turret's radius and can be seen while aiming the Super. In addition, the turret grants her and her allies a shield, which stacks if they stay inside it more, up to a maximum of 6000. The shield health increases at a rate that is 80% of the turret healing, and decays proportional to its health at the time that it was last refreshed. If an attack deals more damage than the shield's current health, the shield disappears and they are damaged by the remaining damage. [[Colette]]'s attack and Super will deal damage based on their max health when this shield is active. Enemies damaging allies with the shield on can still charge their Supers, but allies with Traits allowing them to charge Super from taking damage cannot. Neither the healing on deploy nor her Pulse Modulator Gadget can contribute to the shield. Also, the turret's health is increased to 7240. Pam also gains a 20% speed boost, and a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips / Game Modes and Maps

*Pam is a versatile Brawler for [[Siege]]. It is recommended to place her Super near the middle of the map where bolts are spawned. Her Mama's Hug Star Power works well to keep her and her allies alive and continuously take control of the area. She can also potentially grant a great control by her Mama's Squeeze Star Power usage.
*Pam is also useful in [[Boss Fight]] and [[Robo Rumble]], both for the high damage she can do from a safe range and for her Super and Mama's Hug Star Power.

### Tips / Recommended Build

*Pam's Pulse Modulator Gadget is very useful in emergency situations. If you or a teammate is in need of healing, you can give them or yourself an instant heal to stay alive long enough to win an interaction or escape.
*Pam's Mama's Hug Star Power can easily counter tick damage, such as [[Crow]]'s poison. If you can consistently land your shots, your health will regenerate faster than Crow's poison decay. The same works against [[Byron]] and [[Angelo]].
*Pam's Mama's Hug Star Power, while not healing much, can still be a useful aspect in keeping teammates alive. Be sure to stay close to them to maximize healing. It is also useful for Pam in 1v1 situations.
*Her Mama's Squeeze Star Power can be used to pressure Brawlers hiding behind walls provided that there no throwers on the other team to take out the turret. This very useful in maps like [[Encirclement]] where brawlers spend a lot of time behind a central wall.
*Pam can place her healing turret on the [[Heist]] safe if she has Mama's Squeeze. This allows her to deal a very high amount of damage in total as the turret will keep dealing damage while it is alive. Coupled with her very fast reload speed and very high damage at close range, she can take out a major portion of the safe's health. Her turret's healing can also protect her other teammates and damage enemies during both offense and defense, making her very good in this mode.

### Tips / Strategies

*Pam's attack has a set pattern: middle, left, middle, right, middle, etc. This can be used to aim where the majority of the bolts (middle will hit 5 projectile) will hit an enemy to ensure maximum possible damage. at long range.

### Tips / Other

*You can get close to enemy Brawlers to deal more damage to them because Pam's main attack is somewhat weak and inaccurate if she's attacking from a distance. However, be careful; close-ranged Brawlers with high burst damage such as [[El Primo]], [[Edgar]], [[Darryl]], [[Shelly]], [[Buzz]], and [[Bull]], among others can easily defeat you before you can defeat them, due to Pam's very slow attack unload speed. Only try to get close to ranged Brawlers, throwers and Brawlers with low damage.
*Charge Pam's Super as quickly as possible and try to keep a heal station on the field at all times to effectively support your teammates and yourself.
*Pam is mainly a defensive Brawler, healing teammates while providing cover fire. However, she can also sweep bushes with her attack, though she should be accompanied by another heavyweight or support Brawler.
*Place Pam's healing turret behind walls, as the healing turret's radius passes through walls. This can ensure plenty of healing while keeping the turret itself safe.
*In tight situations, Pam's Super can be quick-fired to serve as an absorbing shield, sacrificing it in the process to increase the chance of escape when being chased, similarly to [[Ruffs]]' Take Cover Gadget. However, this should be avoided when you go against [[Penny]], [[Barley]], [[Mr. P]], [[Dynamike]], [[Tick]], and [[Brock]]'s splash damage, [[Carl]], [[Tara]], [[Janet]], [[Amber]], [[Chuck]], and [[Nita]]'s piercing attack, or [[Jessie]] and [[Belle]]'s bounce shots that can still damage Pam when she is behind/around her healing station.
