{{Quote|Bolt is a toy from the Battle Bumperz line, designed to deal damage by rolling into things. He was built to battle, but always wanted to be a race car, so he reaches dangerous speeds to live out his dreams. Don't get in his way!}}{{Portrait}}
Bolt is an {{Rarity|Epic}} [[Brawler]] who has high health and a variable damage output and movement speed, but suffers from having a very slow reload speed. He was the 104th Brawler added to the game. His Traits allow Bolt to charge his Super while moving, and to increase his movement speed by moving. He attacks by rolling into enemies, as well as being able to consume ammo to double his damage. The faster his movement speed is, the more damage he deals. His Super temporarily sends him into overdrive, gaining speed and a damage reduction shield in the process, while also leaving behind a trail of lightning, burning enemies if they touch it. His first Gadget, Oil Change, grants him a shield that absorbs damage and decays over time, which is based of his current movement speed. His second Gadget, Bouncy Ball, makes him jump, deal damage and break obstacles upon landing, at the expense of losing all of his movement speed. His first Star Power, Toss Up, knocks enemies back after reaching top speed. His second Star Power, Unstoppaball, makes him immune to crowd control and gains a movement speed boost while his Super is active.

### Traits

{{Quote|Charges their Super while moving.}}{{Trait|Move}}Whenever Bolt moves, he charges his Super. To fully charge, Bolt needs to move 186.67 tiles.

{{Quote|Movement speed increases while moving.}}{{Trait|Speed}}Bolt has a meter consisting of 7 lights underneath his health bar, as well as an arrow in front of him that shows the direction he is facing, accompanied by a box that follows it at a slower speed. Bolt's movement speed initially starts very slow, but as he moves forwards (indicated by the arrow staying within the box), his movement speed will gradually increase, filling each light as it does so. More specifically, it takes 0.5 seconds of moving forwards to charge 1 light bar, or 3.5 seconds to fully charge it. If Bolt takes a sharp turn (indicated by the arrow moving past the box), the lights will start to deplete and his speed will rapidly decrease until it moves back into the box. The lights are only a visual indicator of how much speed he has; the lights themselves do not give him the increased movement speed. If Bolt stops moving, all lights deplete, and he must begin moving again to regain them.

### Attack: Battle Bumper

{{Quote|Bolt can deal damage by rolling into other Brawlers! The faster his movement speed is, the higher his damage.<br>With his main attack Bolt vents excess heat, dealing double damage and gaining speed!}}
Unlike other Brawlers, Bolt does not damage with an ammo consuming attack, and instead deals damage by coming into contact with enemy Brawlers. Once his hitbox comes in contact with the enemies' hitbox, he will deal moderate damage to them, with a small cooldown between hits. The more speed he has, the higher is the damage; however, the damage only scales with the speed Bolt gains, not from any external speed buffs. Bolt can also continue healing from auto-regeneration even after dealing damage this way, as long as he doesn't use ammo. Bolt still has ammo like other Brawlers; using this ammo doubles the damage Bolt deals for the next 0.8 seconds and grants him the equivalent of 2 light bars of speed if he is moving. However, Bolt only has 2 ammo slots and a slow reload speed.

### Super: Overdrive

{{Quote|Bolt goes into overdrive, gaining speed and damage reduction, leaving a damaging trail behind him.}}
Bolt goes into overdrive for 4 seconds, granting him a 40% damage reduction shield, and leaving behind a trail of lightning as he moves. The Super does not grant a speed boost but rather it allows his lights to automatically fill much quicker than normal if he is moving (from 0.5 to ~0.14 seconds). Enemies who touch it are set on fire to burn while they stand in the lightning and for 1 second after they leave. If enemies touch it more than once, the burn duration doesn't stack but resets, similar to [[Stu]]'s Super.

### Gadgets / Oil Change

{{Quote|Bolt gains a 2000 - 4000 health shield based on his current speed. Cooldown: 15 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}[[File:Hardcore Icon.png|left|40x40px|link=]]
Bolt instantly gains a consumable shield that blocks a large amount of damage, and the health is based off his current speed. The shield decays for 5% health every 0.5 seconds, similar to [[Gus]]'s Super or [[Edgar]]'s Hardcore Gadget. If an attack deals more damage than the shield's current health, the shield disappears and Bolt with the shield is damaged by the remaining damage. [[Colette]]'s attack and Super will deal damage based on his max health when this shield is active. Enemies damaging Bolt with the shield on can still charge their Supers. The maximum and minimum amount of shield generated by this Gadget does not change with the Power Level.

### Gadgets / Bouncy Ball

{{Quote|Bolt jumps to a target location and deals area damage. Breaks environments but loses all speed when landing. Cooldown: 20 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
When aiming using the Gadget button, Bolt jumps a short distance. Upon landing, he deals moderate low damage in a small 2-tile radius and destroys all obstacles around it, at the expense of losing all speed. If required to cross over a larger amount of blocks or over lakes, his jumping distance will be increased up to a limit or to the nearest piece of ground. While in the air, Bolt is completely immune to all damage besides status effects and damage applied over time. Despite the description, Bolt loses all speed upon Gadget activation, not upon landing.

### Star Powers / Toss Up

{{Quote|Bolt will knock all enemies upwards when he's at full speed.}}{{StarPower|1}}
When Bolt is at full speed, he will knock enemies into the air (like [[Draco]]'s Uppercut Gadget) when coming into contact with them. This Star Power doesn't take effect if Bolt isn't at full speed.

### Star Powers / Unstoppaball

{{Quote|Bolt is immune to crowd control and gains 15% top speed during his Super.}}{{StarPower|2}}
When using his Super, Bolt is immune to negative effects and his movement speed is increased by 15%. This speed doesn't increase the maximum amount of damage he can do.

### Tips / Game Mode and Maps

* Play maps with few walls and water so you don't collide with them and lose all your speed.
* In a map with very long lines of bushes, you can hit-and-run the opponents in the bushes easily.
* In Brawl Ball and Bounty, you can quickly move to the middle to collect the ball/blue star.
** Note that in Brawl Ball, your maximum speed is reduced to 3 lights of speed while you have the ball. However, you are still able to damage opponents.
** Activate Oil Change right before you get the ball to increase the chances of scoring.
* Bolt is only useful in the later half of the game for Brawl Arena, where his damage is high enough to eliminate most enemies. Instead, focus on poking, clearing off bot hordes and finishing off retreating enemies for the first half of the game and only chase other brawlers when strong enough.

### Tips / Recommended Build

*Bouncy Ball can be used to finish 1 vs. 1 interactions or escape by going over a wall. However, ensure you can definitely win the interaction or escape, because you will be very vulnerable.
*Oil Change should be used when you're charging into a loosely dispersed group at high speeds, as it may require some sharp turns that will decrease your momentum. Hence, using the gadget may buy you some extra time to fight back or escape.
*Toss Up would only be best if you know your enemies cannot stop you, however the extra speed from Unstoppaball would be more preferred to allow better Super cycling.
*Bolt can heal while rolling, so the Healing Gear is recommended so that he can reliably retreat and get back in action.
*Bolt requires direct contact with enemies to deal damage to his opponents, and often deals small amounts of chip damage. As such, use the Shield Gear to protect yourself from accumulated chip damage and enemy retaliation.

### Tips / Strategies

*Make sure you have a clear path in mind before taking action.
*Use your Super when you don't want an opponent to be chasing you. If they follow you, they will be inflicted of the damage over time from the Super.
*Be careful of enemies that have slow, stun, and knockback abilities as they can easily thwart your charge.
*Bolt is very slow without his speed and it is needed to charge the Super, so try not to lose your momentum.
*Bolt relies entirely on his own speed, so make sure you don't run into walls or make turns that are too sharp or you will lose all your speed.
**When you need to make a sharp turn, use your Super so the speed boost from the Super mitigates the speed loss from the sharp turn.

### Tips / Other

*Bolt is best paired with Brawlers that can break walls (e.g. [[Colt]], [[Brock]], [[Frank]], [[Bull]]) and Brawlers that are stronger in open maps (e.g. [[Melodie]], [[Belle]], [[Bea]], [[Nani]]) but not with Brawlers that rely on the environment (e.g. [[Alli]], [[Carl]], [[Rico]], [[Doug]]).
*You can heal as long as you don't use your main attack, so don't stop moving.
