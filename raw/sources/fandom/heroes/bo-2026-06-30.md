Bo is an {{Rarity|Epic}} [[Brawler]] who has moderate health, immense utility with his Super, and a high damage output at close range, in exchange for a moderately slow reload speed. He was one of the 15 Brawlers who released at the first Beta launch. He attacks by firing three long-ranged arrows that explode in a small radius at the end of their range. His Super places three traps that can be triggered to explode a moment later by being walked on by an enemy, damaging and knocking enemies back upon exploding. His first Gadget, Super Totem, places a totem that increases Bo's and his allies' Super charging rate while inside its area-of-effect. His second Gadget, Tripwire, manually detonates his traps. His first Star Power, Circling Eagle, increases his field of view into bushes. His second Star Power, Snare a Bear, causes his traps to stun enemies momentarily instead of knocking them back. His Hypercharge, Catch a Bear, makes his Super deploy five traps.

### Attack: Eagle-Eyed

{{Quote|Bo releases a trio of exploding arrows, tearing up enemies like an eagle's talons.}}Bo shoots three explosive arrows at a long range that can deal high damage to enemies within a 1-tile splash radius on impact. They are fired with a small sweeping spread from left to right (clockwise). The attack has a slow cooldown, and it takes 0.9 seconds to complete.

### Super: Catch a Fox

{{Quote|Bo hides explosive traps in the ground. When triggered by an enemy, the traps explode after a short delay, knocking back and damaging enemies.}}Bo lobs an arrow over obstacles that lays three mines on the ground upon landing. This arrow cannot land on walls or water. If required to cross over a larger amount of blocks or over lakes, the arrow's travel distance will be increased up to a limit or to the nearest piece of ground. The mines spread out 2 tiles away from the arrow's landing point, unless blocked by walls or water. Upon spawn, the mines are momentarily shown before becoming invisible to enemies until they are triggered, but they will always be visible to teammates. When triggered, they will beep and flash before exploding 1.15 seconds later, dealing damage, destroying nearby obstacles, and knocking back any enemies by 1.4 tiles inside their 2-tile blast radius.

### Gadgets / Super Totem

{{Quote|Bo places a totem that increases allies' Super charging rate by 50%. The totem slowly loses its health over time. Cooldown: 19 seconds.}}
{{Gadget|1}}
Bo drops a small totem pole that charge his and any allies' Supers 50% faster within its area-of-effect. If Bo places mines while outside the totem's radius but they deal damage while Bo is inside the radius, he will get the increased Super charge, and vice versa. The totem has 2000 health and a radius of 5.33 tiles, and it decays for 100 health per second until it is destroyed. The previous totem is immediately destroyed if Bo places another totem.

{{Quote|The totem has significantly increased radius.}}{{Buffie|Gadget}}
With the Buffie, Bo's totem's radius is increased from 5.33 tiles to 7. The area also turns golden.

### Gadgets / Tripwire

{{Quote|Bo fires tripwires at his mines, detonating them and causing 1440 damage to anyone hit by a tripwire. Cooldown: 8 seconds.}}
{{Gadget|2}}
Bo fires tripwires at his traps that passes through enemies and obstacles. Each tripwire deals half of his Super's damage per trap and will either knock enemies back or stun them for 2 seconds if the Snare a Bear Star Power is equipped. If some of the mines were already triggered by the enemies when this Gadget was used, they will still explode at the scheduled time (1.15 seconds since the activation by enemies) but will have a bigger radius. However, this doesn't hide the mines that were revealed already.

{{Quote|Detonated mines have a bigger blast radius!}}{{Buffie|Gadget}}
With the Buffie, Bo's detonated traps have a blast radius that are twice as big.

### Star Powers / Circling Eagle

{{Quote|Bo spots enemies hidden in bushes from +150% further away.}}
{{StarPower|1}}
This Star Power allows Bo and his team to see 5 tiles into bushes around him, instead of the 2 tiles Brawlers normally get. This allows Bo and his allies to detect enemies in bushes before the enemies can see either Bo or his teammates.

{{Quote|Bo is no longer revealed when attacking from bushes.}}{{Buffie|Star}}
With the Buffie, Bo will not reveal himself when he attacks from bushes.

### Star Powers / Snare a Bear

{{Quote|Instead of a knockback, Bo's traps now stun the enemy for 2 seconds!}}
{{StarPower|2}}
Bo's traps now stun enemies for 2 seconds instead of knocking them back, rendering them vulnerable. The stun does not stack; instead, the duration refreshes when another mine hits.

{{Quote|Significantly increases trap placement speed.}}{{Buffie|Star}}
With the Buffie, Bo's Super projectile speed when placing the traps is increased by 50%.

### Hypercharge: Catch a Bear

{{Quote|Bo's Super deploys 5 mines instead of 3. These do not override any mines already placed by his Super, so it is possible to have more than 7 mines on the map!}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Bo's Super deploys 5 traps instead of 3. Also, the mine spread distance is increased slightly to 2.33 tiles. Placing these will not remove the traps from his normal Super, and vice versa. However, placing these will override previous traps placed from his Hypercharged Super. Using the Tripwire Gadget will activate all of his existing traps. Bo also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

{{Quote|Bo's main attack shoots a 4th arrow, and each arrow has increased area damage radius.}}{{Buffie|Hyper}}
With the Buffie, Bo shoots four explosive arrows, instead of three, at a long range that can deal high damage to enemies within a 1.33-tile splash radius on impact. It also decreases the interval between projectiles by 25%. Increased Hypercharge duration by 2 seconds.

### Tips / Game Modes and Maps

*If Bo manages to place his mines on the IKE turret in Siege or on the [[Heist]] safe, he can activate Tripwire as soon as he can to deal immediate high damage to the safe/IKE turret.
*Bo's traps can knockback enemies, so if placed correctly, it can knock enemies into the poison gas in Showdown. With Bo's Snare a Bear, Bo's traps will instead stun, giving you ample time to quickly defeat opponents without any risk of dying, making this effective in [[Knockout]], [[Lone Star]], and [[Bounty]]. In [[Duo Showdown]], Bo can be used with a high-damaging Brawler, such as [[Buzz]], [[Darryl]], [[Bull]], [[Fang]], [[Doug]], [[El Primo]], [[Griff]], [[Leon]], [[Frank]], [[Spike]], or [[Shelly]] to finish off a stunned enemy.
*Bo's fairly high health can be used to walk the Ball into the enemy goal in Brawl Ball. In addition, Bo's traps can be used to destroy enemy cover and goalposts. Clearing up certain maps could be beneficial due to Bo's relatively long range, especially if he has teammates with long-ranged abilities. Hence, Bo is a viable Brawler in Brawl Ball.

### Tips / Recommended Build

*Try to use Bo's Tripwire Gadget before the enemy steps onto it, since the 1.5 second delay allows the enemy to escape. Also, note that the 1.5 second delay takes longer than its normal 1.15 seconds.
*When Bo's Super is used, the third trap is set slightly after the other two. Thus, activating Bo's Tripwire Gadget immediately after using his Super only explodes two traps. In [[Brawl Ball]], this can be used to explode walls or obstacles in front of the enemies' goal effectively, which makes it much easier to score. This also leaves one trap hidden until an enemy steps on it, so you can use the last trap for other strategies such as bait for the Ball.
*When an enemy tries to disarm your traps, use Bo's Tripwire Gadget to catch them off guard and deal instant damage. Coupled with Bo's second Star Power, Bo can passively force enemies to not disarm his mines. This allows him to control open areas simply by selecting this Gadget.
*Bo's Circling Eagle is really useful on maps filled with bushes, with his doubled radius of vision in bushes. Some maps include [[Cavern Churn]], [[Feast or Famine]], [[Island Invasion]], [[Snake Prairie]], and [[Sapphire Plains]]. Since you have a sight advantage, avoid getting close to tanks or other high-damaging Brawlers. Place his traps to cover the lane with only one passageway while you can cover the other side with your increased sight. When played properly, Bo can quite easily dominate bushy maps. This is really useful in 3v3, for his increased sight in bushes is shared with teammates too.
*Bo's Snare a Bear Star Power can come into great effect in several Special Events. If one is stunned, you and your teammates can deal massive damage to them temporarily without being targeted. This includes the Bosses in [[Super City Rampage]], [[Robo Rumble]] and [[Boss Fight]].

### Tips / Strategies

*Bo's traps can be effectively lobbed over walls, making them an excellent tool for countering Brawlers hidden in bushes on the other side of a barrier. By strategically tossing a trap just ahead of their hiding spot, you can force them to walk into it, as they lack the ability to cross the wall unless they are airborne or equipped with a destructive Super. Additionally, with the Tripwire Gadget, you can inflict immediate damage on your unsuspecting opponent.
*Bo always shoots three arrows from left to right, so you can take advantage of this to control the spread of your arrows. If you want to concentrate fire in a small area, move left while firing, allowing more arrows to hit a single target. For an attack with more spread, move right. The space between each arrow will be increased, giving the attack a wider spread. However, the former strategy only works on stationary targets as Brawlers will most likely be moving and can thus dodge all three arrows.

### Tips / Other

*Bo's Super can be used to defend critical areas and choke points on the battlefield. Enemies heading in the area will take high damage when they step on the traps. It can be utilized to cover the Gem mine in [[Gem Grab]] to damage enemy Brawlers who are gathering Gems from it. It can also be used to cover key choke points in [[Showdown]], zone(s) in [[Hot Zone]] and Bolt spawning areas in [[Siege]], making Bo an ideal control Brawler.
*Because Bo's attack sweeps in clockwise direction, when going against another Bo, try not to move in clockwise direction. Moving in this direction cause many of the opposing Bo's attacks to hit, while moving anti-clockwise avoids some of the attacks.
