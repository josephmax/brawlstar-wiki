Meg is a {{Rarity|Legendary}} [[Brawler]] who has moderate health and high damage in her advanced form but can attain her alternate form that has very low damage and health. Meg has a very fast movement speed in her base form and a very long-ranged attack in both forms. She was the 51st Brawler added to the game. Meg attacks by firing two bolts in quick succession that deal low damage to enemies. Her Super transforms her into a Mecha that momentarily increases her health and modifies Meg's attack and Super. The Mecha's attack fires two streams of bolts, while its Super deals high damage in a wide arc. Her first Gadget, Jolting Volts, momentarily heals her Mecha while it is active. Her second Gadget, Toolbox, places down a toolbox that grants Meg and allies a reload speed boost inside its area-of-effect. Her first Star Power, Force Field, grants her a long-lasting damage reduction shield after her Mecha is taken out. Her second Star Power, Heavy Metal, deals damage and knocks back nearby enemies when her Mecha gets destroyed. Her Hypercharge, Tungsten Toughness, increases the range of her Mecha's Super, or grants her a shield that absorbs damage and decays over time after she creates her Mecha.

### Attacks / Basic: Target Acquired

{{Quote|Meg fires a short burst from her blaster that pokes at her enemies' patience.}}Meg fires two long-ranged bolts in quick succession that deal low damage to enemies. This attack has a very fast unload speed, taking only 0.4 seconds to complete.

### Attacks / Advanced: Crowd Control

{{Quote|A strong barrage of bullets that leaves nothing to chance. It's the quickest way to make crowds disperse!}}Meg's Mecha fires 16 bolts arranged in two staggered streams, which have a cone-shaped spread. This attack has a slow unload speed, taking 1 second to complete.

### Supers / Basic: Mecha Machina

{{Quote|Meg jumps into her Mecha for some colossal carnage!}} Meg's Super creates a Mecha after a 1-second delay. The Mecha has its own health and modifies Meg's attack and Super. However, Meg's movement speed, unload speed and range are reduced while she's controlling it. Meg's Super transformation can be cancelled if she is stunned, knocked back, or pulled, and she is unable to attack or move during the 1-second delay. If Meg is taken out, her Super charge will go to her Mech when she respawns.

### Supers / Advanced: Feel the Steel

{{Quote|The Mecha swings a formidable forearm that enemies will see and feel.}}The Mecha's Super is a secondary attack that is a wide, piercing swing that deals high damage after a 0.2-second delay, similarly to [[Bibi]]'s attack. If her Mecha is taken out while its Super is charged, Meg won't have its Super when she transforms back.

### Gadgets / Jolting Volts

{{Quote|Heal the Mecha by 490 health per second for 5 seconds. Cooldown: 17 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}This Gadget heals Meg's Mecha for 7% of its health over 5 seconds. This Gadget cannot be activated if her Mecha is not active. This Gadget's cooldown begins after the effect ends.

### Gadgets / Toolbox

Note that this Gadget does not affect the unload speed or cooldown time of a [[:Category:Brawlers|Brawler]]. This Gadget's cooldown begins after the turret is deployed.

### Star Powers / Force Field

{{Quote|Meg is protected by a 30% shield for 10 seconds when her Mecha is destroyed.}}{{StarPower|1}}This Star Power grants Meg a 30% shield for 10 seconds after her Mecha is taken out. The shield duration is cut short if Meg uses her Super again.

### Star Powers / Heavy Metal

{{Quote|Upon expiring, the Mecha suit explodes dealing 2400 damage to nearby opponents and pushing them away.}}{{StarPower|2}}After Meg's Mecha is destroyed, it explodes in a 2.67-tile radius, dealing 70.5% of the Super swing damage to nearby enemies and knocking them back 3 tiles.

### Hypercharge: Tungsten Toughness

{{Quote|Meg's Mecha has an increased swipe range when activating its Super. While out of the Mecha, Meg pops a defensive shield for protection.}}{{Hypercharge}}[[File:Hardcore Icon.png|left|40x40px|link=]]
When activated, both of Meg's Supers get a buff depending on what form she is in when she uses the Super.

While in the Mecha, the Super's range is increased by 2.33 tiles. While outside the Mecha, when she enters it, the Mecha gains a shield that initially blocks the next 3000 damage dealt to it. The shield decays for 150 health every 0.5 seconds. If an attack deals more damage than the shield's current health, the shield disappears and Meg is damaged by the remaining damage. [[Colette]]'s attack and Super will deal damage based on the Mecha's max health when this shield is active. Enemies damaging her with the shield on can still charge their Supers. Meg also gains a 20% speed boost, and a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips

*Meg is a dependable damage dealer when in her Mecha, making her particularly effective at dealing damage to the [[Heist]] safe or applying pressure in [[Duels]]. However, her reload speed and movement speed are reduced while in the Mecha. As a result, it’s advisable to avoid chasing down enemies in modes like [[Bounty]] or [[Knockout]].
*Meg is an agile poker without her Mecha because of her fast movement speed and fast unload speed, but with it, she becomes a devastating fighter and control asset as she can deal high damage in large areas with her attack and secondary Super.
*There's a slight delay before Meg hops into her Mecha, and her Super can be cancelled if she dies, gets knocked back, or gets stunned. Make sure to activate her Super where you'll be safe from attacking enemies.
*In [[Brawl Ball]], Meg can be very viable as a defense to oncoming opponents with the ball as her Mecha has a lot of health and can be used to significantly damage opponents. She can take enemy fire and provide some area denial to help the ball carrier score an easy goal.
*In [[Showdown]], Meg can be a force to be reckoned with due to her starting in her much stronger Mecha form. Combined with her Jolting Volts Gadget, Meg can duel even longer range Brawlers due to the Mecha's high health. However, Meg becomes much weaker after it is destroyed. Prioritize picking up Power Cubes and use her Force Field Star Power to increase Meg's survivability outside her Mecha.
*When the various upgrades the Mecha gives Meg are needed more than usual, using Meg's Jolting Volts Gadget can keep Meg in her Mecha significantly longer against long range Brawlers that can prevent it from healing. This is useful in Heist because Meg will have a very low impact on the health of the Safe in her standard form, but can deal substantial damage onto the safe when inside her Mecha.
*Be mindful when taking on high-responsibility roles such as the Gem carrier in [[Gem Grab]] or the ball carrier in Brawl Ball, because if Meg's Mecha is taken out, she will become an easy target.
*Avoid close combat and engaging with aggressive Brawlers after her Mecha is destroyed. Either Star Power can help by shielding some damage or pushing away enemies near Meg, but those won't completely save her from taking damage or being defeated.
*Meg's advanced Super will cancel its main attack. Keep this in mind if you want to deal substantial long-range damage.
*In [[Ranked]] games with the Big Friend [[Modifiers|Modifier]], both Meg and her Mecha will have their health scaled up. This makes her particularly difficult to fully take down and allows her to more easily recharge her Mecha once destroyed.
*In her Mecha form, Meg is able to deal a lot of damage and keep control of an area with her high-damaging attack and Super. This makes her an extreme menace in modes like [[Hot Zone]] and [[Brawl Ball]]. Since her range is shorter than most long-range Brawlers, she excels in maps that require mid-lane control.
