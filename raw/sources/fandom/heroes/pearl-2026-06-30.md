Pearl is an {{Rarity|Epic}} [[Brawler]] who could be unlocked as a [[Brawl Pass]] reward at Tier 30 in Season 20: Back to Ranger Ranch. She has moderately high health, a long range, and a variable damage output. She was the 71st Brawler added to the game. Pearl's Heat bar charges over time, and allows her to deal more damage the more it is charged. She attacks by shooting a spread-out burst of cookies in a sweeping pattern that gradually depletes her Heat. Her Super is a shockwave that deals very high damage and knocks surviving enemies back in a large radius while also destroying obstacles. Her first Gadget, Overcooked, allows her next attack to fire burning cookies at an enemy. If one of the cookies hit, it damages them over time. Her second Gadget, Made With Love, allows her next attack to heal allies over time. Her first Star Power, Heat Retention, decreases the Heat bar's reduction when using her Super. Her second Star Power, Heat Shield, reduces incoming damage while charging her Heat. Her Hypercharge, Pyrolitic, allows her Super to leave a burning area on the ground, burning enemies if they touch it.

### Trait

{{Quote|This Brawler's damage increases slowly over time.}}{{Trait|Dmg Increase}}
Pearl has a Heat bar that charges over time, will deplete when Pearl uses her main attack, and will reset when she uses her Super. Her Heat bar takes 9 seconds to charge up, and it gives Pearl up to a 75% damage boost to both her main attack and Super, depending on how much it's charged.

### Attack: Hot Cookies

{{Quote|Rapid fire hot cookies directly out from the oven, slightly cooling the oven while damaging enemies on hit. The higher the Heat the higher the damage.}}
Pearl shoots six long-ranged cookies in a spread-out volley that sweeps from left to right. This wide spread of her attack allows it to hit multiple enemies, but makes it very inconsistent and inaccurate when dealing with one single target at a range. However, at close range she can hit all her bullets and deal very high damage. Each cookie fired will decrease her Heat bar by an equivalent of 0.45 seconds of charging time, so the damage per bullet gradually decreases within the same attack.

### Super: Let Out Some Steam

{{Quote|Consume and release all the built up Heat in an explosion, damaging all nearby enemies. The higher the Heat the higher the damage.}}
After a 0.3-second delay, Pearl creates a shockwave that deals very high damage to enemies, knocks them back, and destroys cover in its 3.33-tile blast radius. If Pearl is hit with a stun, pull, or knockback during the delay, her Super is cancelled.

### Gadgets / Overcooked

{{Quote|Next basic attack shoots burning cookies, dealing 1040 extra damage over time. Cooldown: 15 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
Activating this Gadget allows Pearl's next attack to fire six burning cookies. If one of them hits, the fire deals half a cookie's damage every second for 4 ticks. A Gadget symbol will glow above Pearl's head signaling this Gadget's activation, as well as a glowing attack joystick. This Gadget's cooldown begins after the attack is used.

### Gadgets / Made With Love

{{Quote|Next basic attack shoots perfectly baked cookies ignoring enemies and healing allies for 2800 over time instead. Cooldown: 11 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
Activating this Gadget allows Pearl's next attack to heal allies. If one of the cookies hit, it heals for 8.9% of Pearl's maximum health every second (700 health per second at Power 11) for 4 ticks. The attack's projectiles will also turn green during the Gadget's activation. A Gadget symbol will glow above Pearl's head signaling this Gadget's activation, as well as a glowing attack joystick. This Gadget's cooldown begins after the attack is used.

### Star Powers / Heat Retention

{{Quote|Super will consume 50% less Heat when used.}}{{StarPower|1}}
When using her Super, her Heat will be reduced to half of its remaining amount.

### Star Powers / Heat Shield

{{Quote|While the Heat is over 80%, reduce damage taken by 20%.}}{{StarPower|2}}
When Pearl's Heat bar is charged over 80%, she shields herself from all damage by 20%. The effect is lost if the bar is under 80%.

### Hypercharge: Pyrolitic

{{Quote|Let Out Some Steam leaves a burning area behind for 4.5 seconds.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Pearl's Super leaves a burning area on the ground that lasts for 4.5 seconds. It deals 800 damage, and it can hit 5 times if the enemy remains standing on the area. She also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips / Game Modes and Maps

*Using her Super against the goal barrier in [[Brawl Ball]] will instantly destroy it, allowing for an easy goal for the remainder of the match.

### Tips / Other

*Never waste Pearl's heat. Her heat takes a fairly long time to charge up and wasting ammo will result in little damage output and not game altering at all. Instead, save up her heat and fire a burst of attacks at enemies to take most of them down. Her Heat Retention Star Power will further boost this and allow for a direct Super onto enemies and a quick burst of attacks shortly after.
