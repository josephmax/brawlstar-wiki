{{Quote|A smart scientist with a nostalgic soul. After years working in shady corporate laboratories, she left to build Windstock and help the planet, not harm it!}}{{Portrait}}
Wendy is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who has the lowest health of any Brawler in the game and a moderate damage output, but has a fast movement and reload speed, a long range, and great survivability thanks to her attack, Super, and Trait. She will be the 106th Brawler added to the game. Her Traits allow her to move over water, and to charge her Super from enemies damaging her shield. She attacks by firing a blast that provides herself and allies a shield that absorbs damage. Her Super is a wind turbine that passively reduces some damage Wendy and her allies take, redirecting that same amount of damage done to the turbine instead. Her first Gadget, Wind-Powered, makes Wendy jump forward. Her second Gadget, Green Grenade, makes her lob a green grenade that temporarily slows enemies down and prevents them from auto-regeneration. Her first Star Power, Slowing Shield, slows enemies down while inside Wendy's Super. Her second Star Power, Solar Shield, adds 2000 HP to the shield's health.

### Traits / Water

{{Quote|This Brawler can move over water.}}{{Trait|Water}}
Wendy's first Trait allows her to be able to move on water.

### Traits / Shield Damage

{{Quote|Charges Super when taking shield damage.}}{{Trait|Shield Damage}}
Wendy's other Trait allows her to charge her Super, whenever she or her allies take shield damage from enemy Brawlers or spawnables. The Super charge rate depends on the damage the enemy deals. It takes twice of the shield's max health of damage to charge her Super completely, and all damage is calculated after shielding and other effects. Wendy also spawns with a shield already active, which is equal to her maximum health. This shield does not decay, but can only be replenished with her main attack.

### Attack: Blow Dryer

{{Quote|Wendy fires a blast dealing 2200 damage and giving herself 1268 shield. When hitting a teammate, Wendy gives them 2000 shield.}}[[File:Hardcore Icon.png|left|40x40px|link=]]
Wendy fires a blast that deals moderate damage to enemies, has a long range, and provides herself a consumable shield that is 31.7% of her max health. However, upon firing the attack on allies, she instead provides them a shield that is 50% of her max health. Wendy's shield on herself does not decay, but her allies' decay for 5% health every 0.5 seconds, similar to other consumable shields. If an attack deals more damage than the shield's current health, the shield disappears and Wendy and her allies with the shield is damaged by the remaining damage. [[Colette]]'s attack and Super will deal damage based on the Brawler's max health when this shield is active. Enemies damaging those with the shield on can still charge their Supers, but allies with Traits allowing them to charge Super from taking damage cannot. The shield on allies does stack, up to 4 times its initial health.

### Super: Planet Protector

{{Quote|Wendy deploys a shield generator that absorbs 75% of damage taken by Wendy and her teammates within its range.}}
Wendy lobs a wind turbine that covers a 10-tile radius. The turbine passively reduces all damage Wendy and her allies take by 75% by redirecting the damage to the Super instead.

### Gadgets / Wind-Powered

{{Quote|Wendy's platform gives her a boost and she jumps a short distance. Cooldown: 18 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
Wendy jumps forward 5 tiles in the direction she's facing, allowing her to jump over walls or water. If required to cross over a larger amount of blocks or over lakes, her jumping distance will be increased up to a limit or to the nearest piece of ground. While in the air, Wendy is completely immune to all damage besides status effects and damage applied over time.

### Gadgets / Green Grenade

{{Quote|Wendy launches a wind grenade that slows all enemies hit and stops them healing momentarily. Cooldown: 18 seconds.}}
{{Gadget|2}}
Wendy lobs a wind grenade that explodes in a 2-tile radius, slowing the enemies hit and preventing them from auto-regeneration for 5 seconds.
