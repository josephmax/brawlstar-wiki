{{Quote|Ziggy is an uber theatrical who excels at using electricity in his act. He's a bit full of himself, but hey, he can literally control lightning. Just remember... they're not tricks, they're illusions.}}{{Portrait}}
Ziggy is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who has an above-average movement speed, moderately low health and damage output, a long range, and immense utility with his Super, but a moderately slow reload speed. He was the 96th Brawler added to the game. He attacks by summoning lightning strikes on a target. For his Super, he creates a very large electrical storm that travels very fast, but slows down when it damages enemies caught that are caught in it. His first Gadget, Electric Shuffle, hits the nearest target with lightning strikes every second for 5 seconds. His second Gadget, Now You See Me..., teleports Ziggy where his next attack hit. His first Star Power, Thunderstruck, temporarily slows enemies down inside Ziggy's Super. His second Star Power, The Great Ziggini, increases the damage of his next attack when one of his attacks hits an enemy. His Hypercharge, The Grand Vortex, makes his Super return to him when it reaches its max distance.

### Attack: Ta-da!

{{Quote|Ziggy summons a lightning strike at a targeted area. After a short delay, it appears (LIKE MAGIC!) and deals area damage.}}
Ziggy attacks by summoning long-ranged lightning strikes to a specific area, even locations behind walls or enemies. When the attack is initially used, a targeting circle appears at the attack location, dealing damage to all enemy brawlers in its radius 0.75 seconds later. The attack's travel time is instantaneous, and the splash radius is 1.5 tiles. Ziggy's attack has a slow cooldown and a somewhat slow reload speed.

### Super: Ziggy's Fantastical Storm

{{Quote|Ziggy unveils his greatest trick, a giant electrical storm that travels rapidly across the environment! It deals massive damage to enemies caught within and moves slower whenever an enemy Brawler is inside.}}
After a 0.2-second delay, Ziggy summons a very large, cylindrical-shaped electrical storm that rapidly moves forwards and deals high damage per second, dealing up to 8 ticks of damage. Ziggy can have multiple storms on the map at once. It stops moving when it reaches the end of its range or when its center would otherwise go over the edge of the map. The storm disappears after 3 seconds or when it reaches maximum range, whichever comes last. It slows down when an enemy is inside it, which increases the storm's duration. The damage scales with buffs and debuffs for every hit. If Ziggy is defeated while it is active, it would deal the base damage.

### Gadgets / Electric Shuffle

{{Quote|Ziggy hits the nearest enemy with lightning strikes every second for 5 sec. These attacks cost no ammo. Cooldown: 17 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
For 5 seconds, Ziggy targets the nearest enemy with a lightning strike every second. These strikes are mechanically identical to his main attack, however, they do not use up his ammo. This Gadget's attacks are limited by his main attack range, but it can be activated as long as there is an enemy visible on screen. This Gadget's cooldown begins after the duration ends.

### Gadgets / Now You See Me...

{{Quote|The next lightning strike also teleports Ziggy to the same location. Cooldown: 13 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
Activating this Gadget allows Ziggy to teleport where the next attack was shot after it completes. A Gadget symbol will glow above Ziggy's head signaling this Gadget's activation, as well as a glowing attack joystick. This Gadget's cooldown begins after the attack is used.

### Star Powers / Thunderstruck

{{Quote|Brawlers damaged by Ziggy's Super are slowed by 20% for 1 sec.}}{{StarPower|1}}
Enemies caught in Ziggy's Super will also be slowed down by 20% for 1 second. This Star Power can take effect multiple times if the enemies are in the storm for longer.

### Star Powers / The Great Ziggini

{{Quote|Hitting with a lightning strike increases the damage of the next main attack by 18%.}}{{StarPower|2}}
After hitting a target with the attack, Ziggy's ammo turns yellow, and the damage from his next attacks increases by 18%. If Ziggy misses the target with his attack, he loses the damage boost. Attacks summoned before the previous attack lands will not have the damage boost.

### Hypercharge: The Grand Vortex

{{Quote|A Hypercharged Super returns to Ziggy upon reaching its max range/lifetime.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Ziggy's next Super will return after it reaches its max distance. Unlike most returning abilities, the storm returns not to his current location, but to Ziggy's location at the time it reached max distance. The storm's maximum lifetime is effectively doubled, but no longer has a minimum lifetime. Ziggy also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips

*Apart from his Now You See Me... Gadget, Ziggy has no escape or defensive options whatsoever. As such, try to stick near teammates that can defend you or stay in the backlines as crowd control.
*Ziggy's Super is an incredibly powerful source of crowd control but can be easily dodged by moving out of its trajectory. As such, aim it at choke points where opponents are forced to go in. The same can be said of his regular attack.
*When an opponent is pursuing you, lead them around and aim slightly in front of or behind yourself, depending on their speed. That way you will be able to reliably hit them.
*Do not auto-aim with main attack. The main attack has long delay and auto-aiming makes harder to hit enemies, since the projectile appear on the center of Brawler.
*For escaping aggressive Brawlers with the Now You See Me... Gadget, always activate it before your opponent reaches you. This is because unlike most defensive Gadgets, Ziggy's Gadget requires some time to activate (on account of his attack delay), leaving him vulnerable to high burst Brawlers or Brawlers with stunning capabilities like [[Bull]], [[Buzz]], [[Frank]], or [[Fang]].
