Ollie is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who has high health, a low damage output, a fast movement speed, a medium attack range, and immense utility with his Super, but a moderately slow reload speed. He was the 88th Brawler added to the game. Ollie's Trait allows him to charge his Super from enemies damaging him. His attack is a soundwave in a narrow cone that pierces through targets. For his Super, he dashes forward and creates a large soundwave that damages nearby enemies while also temporarily hypnotizing them. Hypnotized enemies will move towards Ollie, and they can't use their attacks, Super or Hypercharge. His first Gadget, Regulate, makes Ollie jump forward and briefly hypnotize nearby enemies upon landing. His second Gadget, All Eyez on Me, allows his next attack to also temporarily hypnotize them. His first Star Power, Kick, Push, increases Ollie's movement speed while moving near a wall. His second Star Power, Renegade, provides Ollie a shield after dashing with his Super. His Hypercharge, Ain't No Half-Supering, makes him quicker and creates a larger soundwave that similarly damages nearby enemies while also temporarily hypnotizing them, but without depleting Ollie's ammo.

### Trait

{{Quote|This Brawler charges Super from damage received}}{{Trait|Tank}}Whenever Ollie takes damage from enemy Brawlers or spawnables, he charges his Super. The Super charge rate depends on the damage the enemy deals. It takes 2.4x Ollie's max health of damage to charge his Super completely, and all damage is calculated after shielding and other effects.

### Attack: Bring the Ruckus

{{Quote|Ollie's hoverboard fires soundwaves which deal damage and go through enemies.}}Ollie's attack is a medium-ranged, piercing soundwave that deals moderate damage in a narrow cone.

### Super: Hypnotize

{{Quote|Ollie dashes forward with a gnarly hoverboard trick and starts charging a music blast, dealing 1600 damage and hypnotizing all enemies around him for 2.5 seconds. While charging the music blast, Ollie will use up to 50% of his max ammo. Enemies will move towards Ollie and cannot be controlled while being hypnotized.}}Ollie dashes forward and, after a 1.5-second delay, he creates a soundwave in a 3.33-tile radius around himself that damages enemies while also hypnotizing them for 2.5 seconds. After the initial Super dash is complete, Ollie will lose 50% of his max ammo. While hypnotized, enemies will move towards Ollie, and they can't use their attacks, Super or Hypercharge. After 2.5 seconds, or if Ollie is defeated, enemies will no longer be hypnotized. Ollie's Super is cancelled if he is stunned, pulled, or knocked back during the dash, and he cannot dash through walls or water. The Super damage does not scale with buffs or debuffs.

### Gadgets / Regulate

{{Quote|Ollie jumps and does a rad kickflip, hypnotizing all enemies where he lands for 1 seconds. Cooldown: 20 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
Ollie jumps forward 3.67 tiles in the direction he's facing for approximately 0.86 seconds, similar to [[Brock]]'s Rocket Laces Gadget. He can jump over walls or water, and he hypnotizes nearby enemies in a 1.67-tile radius for 1 second upon landing. If he lands on walls or water, he will automatically be pushed to the closest valid piece of terrain. While in the air, Ollie is completely immune to all damage besides status effects and damage applied over time.

### Gadgets / All Eyez on Me

{{Quote|Ollie's next attack will hypnotize enemies hit for 1 seconds. Cooldown: 15 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
Activating this Gadget allows Ollie's next attack to also hypnotize enemies for 1 second upon hitting them. A Gadget symbol will glow above Ollie's head signaling this Gadget's activation, as well as a glowing attack joystick. This Gadget's cooldown begins after the attack is used.

### Star Powers / Kick, Push

{{Quote|While near walls Ollie kicks off his hoverboard and gains 20% extra movement speed.}}{{StarPower|1}}
While moving near a wall, Ollie's movement speed is increased by 160 points.

### Star Powers / Renegade

{{Quote|After dashing forward with his Super, Ollie gains a 3000 decaying shield over 4 seconds.}}{{StarPower|2}}[[File:Hardcore Icon.png|left|40x40px|link=]]
After Ollie dashes forward with his Super, he receives a shield that initially blocks the next 3000 damage dealt to him. The shield decays for 150 health every 0.2 seconds, so the damage it can block decreases over time. If an attack deals more damage than the shield's current health, the shield disappears and Ollie is damaged by the remaining damage. [[Colette]]'s attack and Super will deal damage based on Ollie's max health when this shield is active. Enemies damaging Ollie with Renegade can still charge their Supers, but Ollie will not receive Super charge from his Trait. The shield will trigger even if Ollie's Super is cancelled during the dash.

### Hypercharge: Ain't No Half-Supering

{{Quote|Ollie now dashes farther and faster! His music blast area is larger and it won't consume ammo when it starts charging.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Ollie's Super has increased range and dash speed. At the end of his dash, Ollie will no longer lose ammo, and the music blast area-of-effect has a 50% larger radius. Also, his Super has a slightly higher damage and Super recharge rate. He also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips / Game Modes and Maps

*In [[Brawl Ball]], Ollie can use his Super to grab the ball and walk it into the goal, as his high health allows him to survive longer from opponents' attacks and the Hypnotize effect prevents nearby enemies from attacking for 2.5 seconds, further enhancing his survivability. This is even easier to do when paired with his Renegade Star Power.
*When playing as Ollie, do not try to be overly aggressive, as Ollie does not have high damage output to support an aggressive playstyle. Instead, try to support your teammates and apply pressure on the opponents by using your Super, which is crucial in modes like [[Gem Grab]].
*In Brawl Ball, Ollie can use his All Eyez on Me Gadget after shooting the ball at the goal to prevent enemies from blocking his goal. His high health allows him to get close enough to do this trick.

### Tips / Recommended Build

*Be careful if the enemy has any knockback, pull, or stun abilities before using Ollie's Super. The Hypnotize effect is cancelled if he gets a knockback, pull, or a stun during the dash.
*The Renegade Star Power is recommended because it greatly improves his survivability, since Ollie has to get dangerously close to enemies in order to use his Super effectively. 
*The Regulate Gadget is also recommended since it can dodge dangerous attacks and Supers. However, his All Eyez on Me Gadget is generally better in Brawl Ball.
*Ollie can use his Regulate Gadget during his Super delay to allow him to bypass walls that would otherwise greatly hinder his Super. For instance, if an enemy is within hypnotism range but is behind a wall, using Regulate before the Super detonation would allow Ollie to defeat the hypnotized target.
