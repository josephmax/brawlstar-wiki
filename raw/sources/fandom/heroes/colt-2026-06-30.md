Colt is a {{Rarity|Rare}} [[Brawler]] who has moderately low health, a long range, a very fast reload speed, and an inconsistently high burst damage. He  was one of the 15 Brawlers who released at the first Beta launch. His attack is a long-ranged volley of six bullets. His Super is a longer-ranged volley of twelve piercing bullets that can destroy obstacles. His first Gadget, Speedloader, causes him to fire two rapid shots that slow down enemies. His second Gadget, Silver Bullet, makes his next attack a single piercing bullet that destroys obstacles and deals the damage of two of his main attack's bullets. His first Star Power, Slick Boots, passively increases his movement speed. His second Star Power, Magnum Special, increases his main attack's range and bullet speed. His Hypercharge, Dual Wielding, increases the width of his Super.

### Attack: Six-Shooters

{{Quote|Colt shoots six straight long-range shots out of his revolvers.}}Colt shoots a straight, long-ranged volley of six bullets. Each bullet by itself deals little damage, but if all of the bullets hit an enemy, they can deal a high amount of damage. The bullets are 0.33 tiles in width and come out in two streams 0.33 tiles apart. The bullets travel fast and the attack reloads quickly, allowing Colt to maintain constant pressure on enemies, but the attack has a slow cooldown, taking 0.8 seconds to complete.

### Super: Bullet Storm

{{Quote|Colt rattles off a massive burst of bullets that shoot extra far and destroy cover.}}
Colt shoots a straight, longer-ranged volley of twelve piercing bullets. The bullets are 0.67 tiles in width and come out in two streams 0.33 tiles apart. These bullets appear larger in size than his main attack, travel faster than his main attack, and can destroy obstacles. Note that the bullets destroy walls, but don't pierce through them, so bullets disappear upon destroying a wall tile. The Super takes 1.4 seconds to complete. However, he can't attack or reload while using his Super, and it is cancelled if Colt is knocked back, pulled, or stunned.

### Gadgets / Speedloader

{{Quote|Colt fires two quick shots that slow targets. Deals 640 damage. Cooldown: 15 seconds.}}
{{Gadget|1}}
When this Gadget is used, Colt instantly fires two long-range bullets that slow enemies upon impact. Each bullet deals the damage of a Super bullet, totaling 1280 damage at max Power Level. This Gadget does not consume ammo. Using auto-aim will cause Colt to fire at the nearest enemy. This Gadget's cooldown starts immediately after it's activation.

{{Quote|Each hit steals 0.5 ammo.}}{{Buffie|Gadget}}
With the Buffie, each bullet from this Gadget steals half an ammo each, allowing Colt to potentially gain one ammo and the enemy to lose one ammo.

### Gadgets / Silver Bullet

{{Quote|Colt fires a bullet that destroys the environment and pierces Brawlers. Deals 1200 damage. Consumes ammo. Cooldown: 17 seconds.}}
{{Gadget|2}}
Colt fires a piercing bullet in the direction the Gadget is aimed, which deals 167% the damage as one main attack bullet and pierces all enemies and the environment. It also generates Super charge equal to one regular attack bullet. Unlike the bullets from his Super, this projectile pierces through walls without disappearing. This Gadget's cooldown starts after this attack is shot.

{{Quote|Shoot a larger bullet that deals more damage.}}{{Buffie|Gadget}}
With the Buffie, the Silver Bullet is larger and deals 67% more damage.

### Star Powers / Slick Boots

{{Quote|Colt's movement speed is increased by 13%.}}
{{StarPower|1}}
Colt's movement speed is passively increased by 13%, allowing him to move faster than most other Brawlers and roughly the same as assassins such as [[Crow]], [[Mortis]], [[Edgar]], [[Mico]], [[Cordelius]], [[Kenji]], and [[Leon]].

{{Quote|Gain movement speed when dealing damage.}}{{Buffie|Star}}
With the Buffie, Colt gains a burst of movement speed whenever any of his attacks hit an enemy. Every attack that lands will give a 20% speed boost for 0.5 seconds, and the duration refreshes with multiple hits.

### Star Powers / Magnum Special

{{Quote|Colt's attack range and bullet speed are increased by 11%.}}
{{StarPower|2}}
Colt's main attack range is increased by 1 tile, making his range almost equivalent to [[Piper]], [[Bea]], [[8-Bit]], [[Belle]], [[Angelo]], [[Byron]], and [[R-T]]. The bullet speed is also increased to reach the target in the same amount of time at maximum range as normal.

{{Quote|Deal increased damage to distant targets.}}{{Buffie|Star}}
With the Buffie, Colt deals slightly more damage the further he is from the enemy he shot at, up to 5% at max range.

### Hypercharge: Dual Wielding

{{Quote|Bullet Storm is 120% wider.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, the separation between the two streams of bullets of Colt's Super is increased from 0.33 tiles to 0.73 tiles, increasing his overall Super width from 1.01 tiles to 1.41 tiles. He also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

{{Quote|Fire bullets quicker during Hypercharge.}}{{Buffie|Hyper}}
With the Buffie, the time interval between bullets is decreased by 50% for his main attack, increasing his overall unload speed during his Hypercharge. However, his reload speed is slightly slower while this effect is active. Increased Hypercharge Duration by 2 seconds.

### Tips / Game Modes and Maps

*Colt's low health makes him particularly vulnerable, so it's best to avoid using him in survival-based game modes like [[Showdown]], [[Hot Zone]], and [[Bounty]], where he often struggles in 1v1 lane encounters. However, he shines as a supportive teammate in [[Duo Showdown]], [[Brawl Ball]], and [[Heist]], functioning as a pseudo-sniper with high damage potential and the ability to break walls.
*Colt does best on open maps, where he can easily control the map, out-ranging close-ranged Brawlers with his attacks and Super. In addition to Colt's effectiveness on open maps, his Magnum Special Star Power increases his effectiveness on these maps as it grants him additional range and therefore increases his area of control. In Team Events, it's best to pair Colt up with Brawlers with stunning, pulling, and slowing abilities to make it easier to hit all of his bullets.

### Tips / Recommended Build

*The Speedloader Gadget is useful for defending Colt from attacks by close-range Brawlers like [[Edgar]], [[Mico]], and [[El Primo]], and can also be used to confirm eliminations due to the slow effect, ensuring Colt lands the majority of his shots. The slow effect also leaves the enemy vulnerable to attacks from other Brawlers, allowing your team to eliminate it.
*Colt's Silver Bullet Gadget can be used to clear obstacles, which is useful in a lot of situations. For example, in [[Showdown]], he can use Silver Bullet to destroy certain obstacles that enemies are hiding behind and finish them off. It can be used very effectively when breaking the barrier in front of an enemy goal in Brawl Ball or [[Basket Brawl]].
*Colt's Slick Boots allows him to move faster, making chasing or escaping to cover easier. Slick Boots is also excellent for dodging projectiles from other long-ranged Brawlers.

### Tips / Strategies

*Try to follow the enemy's movement. The full potential of your attack requires all of the bullets to hit the enemy. To do this, it is necessary to use a technique called "strafing," which consists of anticipating the enemies' movements by shooting right in front of them, where do you think the enemy brawler will be when the bullets hit. Predicting the enemy's movement allows all of the bullets to hit and deals massive damage. If they dodge a lot, shoot behind them so that they walk into your bullets. The inverse also works; If they keep walking into your attack when you use prediction to shoot, keep shooting that way.
*Mirroring the movement of an enemy you are attack will increase your chances of hitting all of his bullets with his main attack or Super. Always stay far away while being parallel to them.
*In [[Brawl Ball]], if the goal still has 2 blocks on the ends or if any goal walls are still present, you can kick the ball into a wall, then immediately use your Super or Silver Bullet to break the walls, enabling the ball to go through the goal with the new open space. However, don't destroy the walls on your own goal because it inadvertently allows the enemies to kick the ball through to your goal.
*If Colt attacks and presses the super button right after, his attack will be immediately interrupted by the super. This is useful both for deceiving enemies, shooting in one direction and then quickly using the super in the opposite direction to trick them and make them walk towards the bullets, and to recharge your super faster, shooting and then using your super. In case it hits the enemy, the bullets fired previously will charge some of your next super.

### Tips / Other

*Try to keep your distance from other enemies. Colt specializes in long-ranged combat and can out-range close-ranged Brawlers very easily. Avoid getting close as much as possible, since Colt tends to be defeated in close-quarters combat.
*Colt's Super can be used to devastate enemies directly or can be used to destroy enemy cover so that Colt or his teammates can rush in and finish the enemies off. In addition, his Super can also restrict enemies from passing through an area for its duration. Try using your super against lined-up enemies to get more value and recharge it faster.
* When being attacked by [[Mortis]], try to keep moving to one direction and shooting to the exact opposite way Colt is facing. Because Mortis lands slightly behind or in Colt with his attack, he has to follow him with subsequent attacks if Colt keeps moving the same way, making Mortis run into all of the bullets. Colt's Speedloader Gadget is a great counter-attack against him with Buffies equiped, since Mortis reloads slowly and Colt can steal at least one of his ammo, leaving him incapacitated to attack and vulnerable in case he has already used an attack. However, be careful with his Combo Spinner Gadget, as it doesn't consume ammo.
* Taking advantage of his high burst damage will help you deal with any Brawlers. When you're up against enemies with low health or squishy Brawlers, a quick burst of shots can take them out before they can even get close. Colt's basic attack reloads fairly quickly, so with precise aiming and positioning, you can deal significant damage in short bursts.
