Leon is a {{Rarity|Legendary}} [[Brawler]] who has a very fast movement speed, moderate health, a long range, immense utility with his Super, and a high damage output at close range, in exchange for a moderately slow reload speed. He was the 22nd Brawler added to the game, and released alongside the global launch of Brawl Stars. He attacks by flicking out four Spinner Blades in a narrow cone that sweep and deal more damage close and less damage further away. Leon's Super makes him temporarily invisible to enemies. His first Gadget, Clone Projector, spawns a clone of himself to confuse and deal very low damage to enemies, although the conjured clone takes twice the amount of damage. His second Gadget, Lollipop Drop, places his lollipop that turns his teammates and him invisible within its area-of-effect until it is destroyed. His first Star Power, Smoke Trails, increases his movement speed while invisible. His second Star Power, Invisiheal, heals him over time while his Super is active. His Hypercharge, Limbo, allows him to stay invisible when attacking while his Super is active.

### Attack: Spinner Blades

{{Quote|Leon flicks his wrist and fires four Spinner Blades. The blades deal less damage the farther they travel.}}Leon fires 4 long-ranged blades that sweep from left to right in a narrow cone. The damage dealt depends on how far they travel before hitting their targets. Targets close to Leon take the most damage, while targets that are farther away take significantly less damage. The damage ranges from full damage in the first 15% of Leon's attack range, to 35% of the max damage at maximum range. The attack cooldown is extremely short, and it takes 0.35 seconds to complete.

### Super: Smoke Bomb

{{Quote|Leon becomes invisible for 6 seconds. If he attacks, he will be revealed. Enemies close to Leon will be able to spot him.}}
[[File:Visibility Warning.png|left|40x40px|link=]]
Leon turns himself invisible for 6 seconds, allowing him to either retreat or ambush an enemy. A unique visual and sound effect plays when Leon goes invisible, even if he is in a bush. Enemy Brawlers can see him if he is within 4 tiles of them, and a symbol is displayed left of your health bar if they detect you. If Leon attacks while invisible, he loses his invisibility. If he takes damage during his Super, he will momentarily be visible.

Picking up items (like Gems in [[Gem Grab]], Power Cubes in [[Showdown]], or Bolts in [[Siege]]) or holding the Ball in [[Brawl Ball]] or the zombie head in [[Zombie Plunder]] while invisible makes him visible momentarily, but does not end the Super. Enemy minions (such as [[Nita]]'s bear, [[Tick]]'s head, [[Ash]]'s rats, and [[Larry & Lawrie|Lawrie]]) can't locate him while invisible, but allied minions (such as [[Tara]]'s Healing Shade) can still detect Leon while invisible. Knockbacks and stuns do not cancel Leon's Super. Aiming is not required when using this Super.

### Gadgets / Clone Projector

{{Quote|Leon creates an illusion of himself to confuse his enemies. The clone attacks enemies dealing minor damage. Cooldown: 13 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}Leon creates a clone of himself, which allows him to confuse enemies or to escape. The clone chases down the nearest enemy and will deal very low damage when it reaches the enemy. It replicates Leon's health (including in [[Big Game]] if Leon is the Big Brawler) at the Gadget's activation time and copies the number of items (such as the number of Gems, Power Cubes, etc.) that Leon himself has, as well as the status of his Super. However, the clone can't heal and all damage dealt to it will be doubled. The clone vanishes when defeated, if Leon summons another one, or after 10 seconds. The clone also doesn't drop anything when defeated. This Gadget's cooldown begins after the clone disappears.

{{Quote|Leon can reactivate the Gadget while a clone is active to swap positions.}}{{Buffie|Gadget}}
With the Buffie, Leon can use the Gadget again while a clone is active to instantly teleport and swap positions with it. The Gadget icon for swapping is different than the normal Clone Projector icon. The swap can only be used once, and if it was used the cooldown begins immediately.

### Gadgets / Lollipop Drop

{{Quote|Leon creates a stealthy area for his team to hide in. The lollipop slowly loses its health over time. Cooldown: 20 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}When this Gadget is activated, Leon drops a lollipop below him that creates an area that turns him and his allies invisible in a 4.33-tile radius. The lollipop has 3000 health, and will be destroyed if Leon drops another lollipop. It also decays by 300 health per second until it is destroyed. Like other invisibility abilities, Brawlers will become visible to enemies if they're within 4 tiles of them. This Gadget's cooldown begins after the attack is used.

{{Quote|Leon and teammates gain 15% movement speed inside the area.}}{{Buffie|Gadget}}
With the Buffie, Leon and his allies that are within its area-of-effect also gain a 15% movement speed boost.

### Star Powers / Smoke Trails

{{Quote|When Leon is not visible, he gains a 30% movement speed for the duration of his invisibility.}}{{StarPower|1}}Leon's movement speed is increased by 250 points while invisible, allowing him to move much faster.

{{Quote|The first attack after breaking invisibility deals +15% damage.}}{{Buffie|Star}}
With the Buffie, after Leon loses his invisibility, his first attack deals 15% more damage.

### Star Powers / Invisiheal

{{Quote|Leon recovers 1320 health per second while his Super is active.}}{{StarPower|2}}If Leon has missing health while using his Super, he regains 20% of his maximum every second for the duration of his Super, if he doesn't cancel his Super with an attack. He still heals even if he takes damage.

{{Quote|Super duration +1 second.}}{{Buffie|Star}}
With the Buffie, Leon's Super duration is increased from 6 seconds to 7 seconds.

### Hypercharge: Limbo

{{Quote|Leon stays invisible while attacking his enemies.}}{{Hypercharge}}
If Leon uses his Super during the Hypercharge or activates the Hypercharge during a Super, attacking will not remove his invisibility. However, if he uses a Hypercharged Super and the Hypercharge duration ends, the next attack will remove his invisibility. He also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

{{Quote|Leon's main attacks do at least 75% damage regardless of distance.}}{{Buffie|Hyper}}
With the Buffie, the minimum damage percent of Leon's attacks is increased from 35% to 75%.

### Tips

*Leon can deal a lot of damage if he hits all of his blades in his attack at short-range. Use obstacles and bushes to get as close as possible to enemies and unleash your attack.
*If you're stuck at long range, you can use the strafing technique to chip out a moderate amount of the enemy's health. Similar to [[Bo]], Leon's projectiles are fired from left to right, so you can concentrate or spread out by strafing left or right while attacking. You can walk left while shooting to narrow his attack width and increase his damage. Conversely, you can walk right while attacking to get a wide attack with minor damage for chipping away at clumped up groups of enemies.
*In [[Heist]], Leon's Super allows him to sneak behind the enemies to attack the enemy safe on the other side of the map.
*In [[Bounty]], Leon can use his Super allows him to pick off enemies and defeat them one by one, racking up huge amounts of stars for his team. However, Leon is not effective at eliminating more than one enemy, so he could find himself in a disadvantageous position after eliminating an enemy.
*Leon's Super is ineffective when used near an enemy when they know your path. Using his Super when hiding in bushes can make your escape route increasingly unpredictable. By doing this, enemies have a hard time finding him after firing at the now unoccupied bushes.
*Leon's Super can be used both offensively and defensively. If you're in a bad position, you can use your Super to sneak away from the enemy or even defeat one of them. If you are carrying a lot of Gems in Gem Grab or have a high bounty in Bounty, use your invisibility to escape, making it hard for the opposition to attack you. When using it to escape, his Invisiheal Star Power can give him a higher chance of surviving tough situations.
*You can flash your Super as you walk into a bush to trick your enemies into thinking that you've gone invisible. This allows you to heal up in the bush whilst your enemies scan the arena, thereby wasting their ammo. You can then actually use your Super to defeat them while their ammo is still low.
*Leon's blades have a deceptively long range and a spread that widens as they travel. Chip away at enemies and build up your Super before slipping into stealth to defeat Brawlers at close range. This strategy works well in Events where you have to eliminate enemies, such as Bounty, Showdown, or [[Knockout]].
*Conserving ammo is also particularly important with Leon. His relatively slow reload speed makes him vulnerable to an enemy who has managed to dodge, survive Leon's attacks, or both.
*Both of Leon's Star Powers are useful for [[Brawl Ball]]. His Smoke Trails Star Power allows him to snatch the Ball quickly while enemies are unsuspecting of Leon's presence until Leon retrieved the Ball and attempts to score, while Invisiheal can grant Leon an exceptional survivability boost which allows him to withstand much more damage from multiple enemy defenders who are present at their goal.
*Unlike most assassins, Leon is used for eliminating one enemy at a time and does not have the potential for multi-defeats because of his slow reload and non-piercing attack.
*In [[Showdown]], Leon has two options for play styles: one is a Roamer and the other is a Third Party. Roamers (like [[Shelly]], [[Darryl]], [[El Primo]], [[Fang]], [[Edgar]], etc.) walk around the map searching for potential targets to pick them off one by one. Third Parties (like [[Mortis]], [[Mico]], [[Rosa]], [[Frank]], or [[Crow]]) search for two people who are already engaged in combat and wait (preferably in a nearby bush) till one of the two is defeated. The Third Party then finishes off the winning Brawler and collects the remaining Power Cubes from both enemies.
*Leon's Smoke Trails Star Power grants him an incredibly fast movement speed for the Super's duration, making him incredibly hard to hit. This is also useful for making quick getaways or to engage someone faster.
