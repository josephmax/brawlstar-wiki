{{Quote|Hired by [[Finx]] to make Pyramid Quest's puzzles harder, her good nature actually lead her to help those trapped by his time-warping mischief instead.}}{{Portrait}}
Najia is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who has moderate health, a fast movement speed, a very fast reload speed, and a variable range and damage output. She was the 101st Brawler added to the game. She attacks by firing a jar that contains a paper snake that can be aimed mid-launch, which damages enemies over time. For her Super, she lobs three jars which spawns snakes that chase down the nearest enemies to poison them. Her first Gadget, Poison Puddles, temporarily creates poisonous puddles after being hit by her attacks. Her second Gadget, Najia Jar, allows Najia to spawn in a vase, and knock enemies bar after it's destroyed. Her first Star Power, Poisonous Protector, spawns a snake where the Brawler were defeated while poisoned. Her second Star Power, Venomous, scales the poison damage, based of a Brawler's current health. Her Hypercharge, Twin Snakes, allows her Super to spawn 6 snakes that also leave behind a trail of poison.

### Attack: Ssssnake Delivery

{{Quote|Najia hurls a jar containing a deadly 'snake'! While the jar is in flight, the snake can be released in any direction.<br><br>The jar deals damage while snake poisons its target. Snake poison can stack, dealing increased damage over time! Her jar pierces Brawlers!}}
Najia fires a jar that contains a paper snake. While the jar is in flight, using the attack again will release the snake from the jar's location; otherwise it will automatically release the snake at max range. The snake's direction can be aimed independently, and autoaims towards the nearest enemy if not manually aimed. If the attack button is held and aimed in a direction but not released, the snake will still automatically travel in the aimed direction. The jar pierces enemies, while the snake deals damage on hit and then two ticks of damage over time to enemies over 2 seconds. The damage on hit of the snake scales with distance travelled, only dealing 60% of max damage at minimum range. The damage over time stacks, meaning you can attack the same target multiple times to increase its effect. Najia has only one ammo slot, so kicking the ball in [[Brawl Ball]] doesn't consume any ammo. The ammo is only consumed once the snake is fired.

### Super: Danger Noodles

{{Quote|Najia unleashes 3 of her paper snakes, which slither a set distance and will poison any enemies in an area when they explode. The snakes can also be thrown from a jar to a target location!}}
Najia lobs 3 jars of snakes at a long range that break on landing and spawn paper snakes. The spread of the jars is always fixed and perpendicular to the direction she throws them. Upon landing, they deal poison damage over 3 seconds in a 2.33-tile radius, as well as a second less damaging poison effect in a 1.33-tile radius; the two poison effects stack. The snakes chase the nearest enemies, like [[Tick]]'s and [[Ash]]'s Supers, and once close enough, they dash into their target, also poisoning on contact to damage enemies over 3 seconds to a small area, 2.33 tiles in radius. The snakes also lose 16% of their health every second until they're destroyed. The Super can also be made to spawn from the location of the jar from her main attack while it is mid-flight, however, when stunned, muted, or being knocked back, if Najia is holding the Super button in any form, the jar will simply not release a Super and not use it up. Using the Super will consume ammo like her main attack does.

### Gadgets / Poison Puddles

{{Quote|All enemies currently poisoned by Najia will create poisonous puddles for the next 2 sec. Cooldown: 17 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
Activating this Gadget allows all enemies currently poisoned by Najia to leave a poisonous puddle trail for 2 seconds, which can deal 2 ticks of damage over time but does not stack. Summons 5 poisonous puddles in total, 1.33 tiles in radius, with approximately 1 second delay between each spawn. The puddles spawn with a 1 second delay at the enemy's last known location. This Gadget can be used only if enemies are poisoned by her main attack or from the inner area effect of her Super's jars' impact.

### Gadgets / Najia Jar

{{Quote|Najia hides inside a vase. Once the vase is broken, she will knock back any nearby enemies! Cooldown: 20 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
Activating this Gadget causes Najia to hide inside a vase, becoming completely immobile and unable to attack, but impervious to all damage for up to 4 seconds. After the vase is destroyed, all nearby enemies in a 3-tile radius will be knocked back by 2 tiles. This Gadget is similar to [[Charlie]]'s Personal Space Gadget, but instead of self healing, Najia knocks back enemies.

### Star Powers / Poisonous Protector

{{Quote|Brawlers defeated while poisoned by Najia will spawn a snake in their place.}}{{StarPower|1}}
Enemy Brawlers defeated by Najia's poison effects will spawn a snake in their place. These snakes have 50% less health than those from her Super, but deal the same amount of damage. Enemies defeated with the initial snake damage, the main attack jar, or the Super's jars will not spawn a snake.

### Star Powers / Venomous

{{Quote|Poison damage scales with the target Brawler's current percent health, dealing up to an extra 200 damage when a target is at 100% health.}}{{StarPower|2}}
This Star Power increases the poison damage dealt to enemies, based off their current health. The maximum possible bonus damage value is multiplied by the enemy's current health percentage to give the actual damage value.

### Hypercharge: Twin Snakes

{{Hypercharge}}
{{Quote|Najia throws 6 jars for her Super, and the snakes from those jars leave a poison trail.}}
When activated, Najia throws 6 jars of snakes, instead of 3. These snakes have an increased health of 3000 and move slightly faster, and also leave trails of poison behind that damage enemies over time. She also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips / Game Modes and Maps

*One of Najia's big advantages is her range, being one of the highest in the game at maximum main attack jar distance. However, it is backed off by her relatively slow projectile speed. The best positioning for Najia is at maximum distance on open maps and behind walls in other maps.
*Najia is terrible at high DPS. She is extremely damage-consistent, but lacks burst damage and self-defense. As such, it is best to play her in Team Game Modes where she excels at suppressing grouped enemies or in Assasin/Tank-unfriendly Maps where she excels at high range and aim.

### Tips / Recommended Build

*Najia's Venomous Star Power has a fixed damage boost that does not increase with buffs or debuffs. As a result, her Poisonous Protector Star Power is recommended over it.
*Najia works well with Damage and Shield [[Gears]]. Extra damage is preferred to increase Najia's DPS, while the Shield is preferred to increase Najia's low maximum health.
*Beware when using Najia's Najia Jar Gadget, as it will occasionally stun Najia for 2 seconds when destroyed, leaving her unable to move. It additionally will not be enough to tank high damaging attacks such as [[Mandy]]'s or [[Dynamike]]'s Super, and will result in Najia's defeat if she's hit by one as she's hiding inside the jar.

### Tips / Other

*Najia has a very unique attack, capable of aiming after releasing a projectile. This allows her to snipe enemies behind obstacles. However, it is incredibly hard to aim with such projectile, and it takes lots of time to learn. Once you mastered it, Najia gets to shine her incredible controlling skill.
*It is possible to auto-attack her main attack, but it often brings no value. The best time to use an auto-attack is right when the enemy is hit by the jar. This immediately places the snake's hitbox near the enemy, allowing a free hit.
*Her Super can be launched at very high ranges. This is done by aiming the super when releasing her main attack's jar. This allows you to have even more control of the snakes' positioning.
