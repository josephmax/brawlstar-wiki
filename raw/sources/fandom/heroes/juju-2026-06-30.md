{{Quote|Juju owns the Oddities Shop and is followed everywhere by her "pet" voodoo doll, Gris-Gris (pronounced Gregory). Although she can control the elements, Juju is also a talented toymaker who makes dolls to sell to Starr Park's many visitors.}}{{Portrait}}
Juju is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who has moderately low health and a variable attack range and damage output. She was the 85th Brawler added to the game. Juju's Trait allows her to move over water. She attacks by lobbing a voodoo toy that explodes onto the ground in a small radius, which gets a different buff based on the environment she is standing on that can either increase more damage on the ground; has more range in bushes; or slow enemies down when over water. For her Super, she throws a head doll spawns her voodoo doll Gris-Gris who has moderate health and attacks by firing needles at the enemies. Her first Gadget, Voodoo Chile, makes her next attack combine the buffs of all environments. Her second Gadget, Elementalist, gives Juju a temporary buff on the environment she is standing on that can either give her a damage-reduction shield on the ground, make her invisible to enemies in bushes, or increase her movement speed over water. Her first Star Power, Guarded Gris-Gris, provides Juju's next Super a shield that absorbs damage and decays over time. Her second Star Power, Numbing Needles, makes her Super's attacks slow enemies upon impact. Her Hypercharge, Bokor, increases her next Super's health, movement speed, and damage, while increasing the size of his needles.

Juju also appears in the [[Oddities Shop]], giving comments when entering it.

### Trait

{{Quote|This Brawler can move over water.}}{{Trait|Water}}
Juju's Trait allows her to be able to move on water.

### Attack: Elemental Energy

{{Quote|Juju throws projectiles empowered by the environment she is standing on, giving them different properties!<br>Ground: More damage<br>Grass: More range<br>Water: Has a slowing effect}}Juju lobs a voodoo toy that explodes in a 2-tile radius. Juju's attack will land on the ground sooner the closer she throws it to herself. Her attack gets a different buff based on the environment she is standing on, increasing her attack damage by 25% on the ground, increasing her attack range in bushes by 2.67 tiles, or slowing damaged enemies by 300 points for 3 seconds when over water. Juju's attack cooldown is reduced to zero when she changes the environment she is standing on.

### Super: From the Other Side

{{Quote|Juju summons Gris-Gris. This spooky voodoo doll drags itself to the nearest enemy and throws needles at them.}}Juju throws a doll head that spawns her voodoo doll Gris-Gris, which has higher health than her, who chases down and attacks enemies. Gris-Gris walks towards the nearest target, and when it gets within the range of the target, it stops and shoots it with medium-ranged needles. Unlike other spawnables, Gris-Gris can both be spawned on and move over water. If there are no enemies available to be attacked, Gris-Gris will follow Juju.

### Gadgets / Voodoo Chile

{{Quote|Juju's next attack has the power of all three elements. Cooldown: 14 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
Activating this Gadget allows Juju's next attack to combine the buffs from all environments. The attack cooldown is reduced to zero for the next attack, but reloading ammo is slower while this Gadget is active. A Gadget symbol will glow above Juju's head signaling this Gadget's activation, as well as a glowing attack joystick. This Gadget's cooldown begins after the attack is used.

### Gadgets / Elementalist

{{Quote|Juju gains a temporary buff based on the current environment she is standing on!<br>Ground: Damage reduction<br>Grass: Invisibility<br>Water: Increased movement speed<br>Cooldown: 17 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
[[File:Visibility Warning.png|left|40x40px]]
Juju gains a buff for 3 seconds based on the current environment she is standing on: on the ground, she gains a shield that reduces all damage she takes by 30%; in bushes, she turns herself invisible, allowing her to either retreat or ambush an enemy; when over water, her movement speed is increased by 40%. Unlike most other invisibility abilities, Juju will not lose her invisibility if she attacks or is damaged. Enemy Brawlers can see her if she is within 4 tiles of them, and a symbol is displayed left of your health bar if they detect you.

### Star Powers / Guarded Gris-Gris

{{Quote|Gris-Gris is summoned with a protective shield.}}{{StarPower|1}}[[File:Hardcore Icon.png|left|40x40px|link=]]
When spawned, Gris-Gris instantly gains a shield that starts with 30% of his maximum health. The shield decays for 5% of its maximum health every 0.5 seconds, so the damage it can block decreases over time. If an attack deals more damage than the shield's current health, the shield disappears and Gris-Gris is damaged by the remaining damage.

### Star Powers / Numbing Needles

{{Quote|Gris-Gris' attack now also slows the enemy for 1 sec.}}{{StarPower|2}}
Enemies hit by Gris-Gris' attacks will be slowed down by 350 points for 1 second. However, this Star Power also reduces the width of Gris-Gris's attacks from 1.67 tiles to 0.67 tiles.

### Hypercharge: Bokor

{{Quote|Juju summons a Hypercharged Gris-Gris giving him bigger needles, 25% more speed, 20% more health, and 20% more damage.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Juju spawns a Gris-Gris that has a 25% faster movement speed, 20% more health, deals 20% more damage, and fires needles that are larger. She also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips

*When you have her Super charged, you can use Gris-Gris as a tank and get closer to her enemies to deal high burst damage. This is more efficient when paired with her Numbing Needles Star Power to increase the accuracy of your hits.
*When enemies approach you, because her attacks on the ground are the highest among the three types of elements, it is best to leave bushes to deal higher damage, or escape onto water while slowing the enemy down.
**On the contrary, when enemies are escaping from you, it is highly advisable that you hide in bushes to increase your range and try dealing as much damage as possible.
*Since Gris-Gris attacks with medium frequency and low accuracy, deploying it near enemies gives a higher threat to your enemies than letting it chase your enemies from far away.
