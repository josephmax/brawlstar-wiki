{{Quote|Once a brilliant maestro, now a train conductor, Chuck traded the symphonies of the concert halls for the cacophonies of the Ghost Station, all in the hope of discovering the next big thing in music!}}{{Portrait}}
Chuck is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who has a fast movement speed, a medium attack range, high health, a high damage output at close range, and immense utility with his Super, but suffers by having a slow reload speed. He was the 72nd Brawler added to the game. His Trait allows his Super to charge itself over time. He attacks by firing three clouds of steam that deal more damage up close. For his Super, Chuck places down a Post that damages and knocks enemies back if out of any of his Posts' range, and dashes to the nearest Post as well as other Posts if in any of his Posts' range. His first Gadget, Rerouting, removes the nearest Post and recharges his Super. His second Gadget, Ghost Train, allows his next dash to pass through obstacles. His first Star Power, Pit Stop, allows Chuck to place down a fourth Post. His second Star Power, Tickets Please!, allows Chuck's next dash to remove ammo from any enemy Brawler it hits while restoring his ammo. His Hypercharge, Full Steam Ahead!, allows Chuck's next dash to have unlimited range, while firing clouds of steam behind him that deal damage.

### Trait

{{Quote|This Brawler charges Super over time}}{{Trait|Auto}}
Chuck's Super will charge itself over time, similar to [[Darryl]]. It takes 5 seconds to fully charge his Super without other Super-charging abilities. However, unlike Darryl, this Trait does not charge his Hypercharge.

### Attack: Steam Engine

{{Quote|Shoots a cloud of steam from Chuck's hat, piercing and damaging enemies. Deals more damage the closer Chuck is to an enemy.}}
Chuck fires three medium-ranged clouds of steam that pierces through enemies. Targets closer to Chuck take the most damage, and targets near Chuck's maximum range take significantly less damage, similar to [[Leon]]. In addition, the attack size increases with distance. Chuck's attack has a long cooldown, and it takes 0.5 seconds to complete.

### Super: Choo-Choo!

{{Quote|Dash to the nearest Post in line of sight, damaging enemies on the way. While no Posts are in line of sight, throw a new Post to the ground.}}
If the player manually aims the Super joystick, Chuck lobs a Post that damages and knocks back enemies near where it lands in a 1.83-tile radius. Despite the description, he can still place Posts while within line of sight of another Post by manually aiming. A maximum of 3 Posts can be placed. Spawning more Posts causes the others to disappear in the order they are placed. The Posts stay indefinitely until a goal is scored in [[Brawl Ball]] or a round ends in [[Knockout]] or [[Duels]].

If the player taps on the Super joystick when Chuck is within 6.67 tiles of a Post, Chuck dashes to it, dealing high damage to enemies in the way. After hitting a Post, if there is another Post within 10 tiles and it is not obstructed by obstacles, Chuck dashes to it, repeating until he has dashed to every Post or there are no more Posts within 10 tiles without obstacles blocking it. If there are multiple Posts able to be dashed to, he chooses the closest one. Chuck also gains a shield that reduces all damage he takes while dashing by 50%. While dashing to Posts, Chuck can still be stunned or slowed. Despite the description, he can still place Posts while not within line of sight of another Post by auto-aiming.

### Gadgets / Rerouting

{{Quote|Chuck removes the nearest Post and recharges his Super. Cooldown: 10 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
Upon activation, Chuck removes the closest Post and recharges his Super instantly. This Gadget can't be used if Chuck already has his Super charged or does not have line of sight of a Post within range.

### Gadgets / Ghost Train

{{Quote|Chuck's next Super can go through walls! Cooldown: 17 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
Activating this Gadget allows all of the dashes in Chuck's next Super to connect to Posts behind walls. A Gadget symbol will glow above Chuck's head signaling this Gadget's usage, as well as a glowing Super joystick. If Chuck uses his Super to instead throw a Post, the Gadget remains active and his next dash can still go through walls. This Gadget's cooldown starts 2.5 seconds after it is activated. This Gadget can only be activated if his Super is charged, but does not require any Posts to be placed.

### Star Powers / Pit Stop

{{Quote|Increase the maximum amount of Posts by 1.}}{{StarPower|1}}
This Star Power increases the maximum number of Posts to 4.

### Star Powers / Tickets Please!

{{Quote|Dashing through enemies with his Super will also steal 33% of their ammo.}}{{StarPower|2}}
Enemies hit by Chuck's dashes will lose one-third of their ammo. Meanwhile, Chuck gains the stolen ammo to his own ammo bar. If an enemy Brawler's ammo bar is empty, Chuck will not receive any more ammo even if the dash hits them, but Chuck can still remove enemies' ammo even if his ammo bar is full. This Star Power does not work against the [[Heist]] safe, the IKE turret in [[Siege]], or spawnables.

### Hypercharge: Full Steam Ahead!

{{Quote|Chuck has unlimited range on his next dash and leaves clouds of damaging steam behind him!}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Chuck's dashes have unlimited range, and he continuously fires 2 trails of piercing steam to the sides while dashing, which deal 183 damage. The steam clouds have a range of 3.33 tiles, but start at a distance of 1.67 tiles away, preventing them from dealing too much damage at close range. He also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips

*Chuck is a great pick in [[Gem Grab]] due to the fact he can place a Post on the enemy side to dash quickly and take out the other team's Gem carrier, or he can place a Post on his own side, allowing him to retreat quickly with lots of Gems.
*Chuck is also a good pick in [[Hot Zone]] as he can set up multiple Posts leading from his spawn right into the Zone. With a good Post setup, Chuck can both quickly arrive in the Zone and heavily damage enemies in it, allowing him or his teammates to finish them off.
*Chuck can deal incredible damage to stationary targets with his Super, so he's great in [[Heist]] and Siege. Once he has his Posts spawned, he can use his fast-charging Super to repeatedly dash on to and back from the safe or the turret. This allows him to quickly deal damage and retreat with unbelievable speed. In [[Siege]], he can place his posts to quickly dash onto the bolt's spawn point, or align the Post from his Super with the enemy's IKE turret, then use it to dash, dealing 3500 damage on the way. He will receive a 50% damage reduction shield while using his Super, which, combined with his fast-moving Super speed, allows him to repeatedly damage the IKE turret while taking minimal damage. He can also deal high damage to defending enemies if they align with the turret and your team's robot advancing, potentially taking them out.
**As Chuck does not have exceptional DPS, it's recommended for players to ignore him in Heist, instead focusing offensive efforts on the enemy safe and reducing the chance for him to charge his Super.
**Another counter for Chuck in heist is to use a Brawler that can negate his Super. Cordelius is a good counter since he can use his Gadget to mute Chuck, preventing him from escaping. He can also use is Super to send him to the shadow realm to stop him from escaping or hitting the safe. However, keep in mind that this makes you reliant on your teammates to destroy the safe.
*Chuck can throw a Post over a wall, and then at any time he can activate his Ghost Train Gadget and use his Super to get through the wall and quickly eliminate anyone hiding behind the wall near his Post.
*Chuck has a generally slow reload speed, so it is always best to be at or near full ammo when using his Super to dash, or hitting people with his Tickets Please! Star Power equipped.
*Chuck can be predictable if activating his Super with the exact same Posts, allowing for enemies to camp out his final Post where he will stop. To avoid this, use his Rerouting Gadget to remove a Post, and place it somewhere else, possibly changing his final destination.
*Chuck already has insane mobility without any assistance, but his Pit Stop Star Power can increase his mobility even more by allowing him to have up to 4 (from 3) Posts on the ground, allowing him to cover even more ground with his dashing Super.
*As Chuck’s posts glow when his Super is ready, this can be a handy indicator as to when Chuck is going to charge in. As such, when playing him, do ensure to have adequate HP and ammo to deal with any campers that may be awaiting your arrival.
*While Chuck isn't a great [[Showdown]] Brawler and is weak against assassins, his posts can actually be used for decent knockback to prevent enemies from getting close.
*Chuck's Ghost Train Gadget goes on cooldown even before it has been used, allowing you to use two ghost trains back to back.
