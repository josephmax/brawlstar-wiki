Griff is an {{Rarity|Epic}} [[Brawler]] who could be unlocked early for a limited time by winning the Griff Challenge. Griff has moderate health and long range but a high damage output at close range, in exchange for a moderately slow reload speed. He was the 49th Brawler added to the game. He attacks by firing 3 waves of 3 coins that each spread out in a cone. His Super throws 5 banknotes that travel forward and return to him, deal more damage the farther they travel, and spread out in a cone. His first Gadget, Piggy Bank, throws a piggy bank that explodes after a delay, dealing area damage and knocking enemies back while also destroying obstacles. His second Gadget, Coin Shower, creates an shower of coins on an area of the map, dealing damage to opponents inside it. His first Star Power, Keep the Change, adds a fourth wave of coins to his attack. His second Star Power, Business Resilience, periodically and fractionally heals his missing health. His Hypercharge, Tax Rebate, allows his Super to pass through obstacles and pierce through enemies, which creates another wave of 5 banknotes when returning to him after a short delay.

### Attack: Coin Toss

{{Quote|Angry Griff pelts opponents with coins. He throws three salvos that spread out in a cone.}}Griff fires 3 waves that each consist of 3 coins, with the coins having a long range and spreading out in a cone formation. The attack deals a high amount of damage at close range, where most or all of the coins can hit an enemy. Because his attack fires 3 waves, the unload speed is slow, taking 1 second to complete.

### Super: Cashback

{{Quote|Griff throws a bunch of sharp-edged banknotes in a wide cone. Too valuable to just give away, they return to him, and may cause paper cuts twice to the same target. Opponents further away take more damage.}}Griff throws 5 piercing banknotes that travel forward, spin in place for 1 second, and then return to him, dealing damage when traveling and when returning. The Super deals high damage if multiple banknotes hit an enemy. The banknotes can't travel through obstacles when thrown, but can travel through obstacles when returning. The banknotes spread out in a wide cone and deal more damage the farther they travel. If Griff can hit an opponent with his Super at point blank, all notes will hit and deal a total of 3600 damage at Power 11.

### Gadgets / Piggy Bank

{{Quote|Griff throws a firecracker-filled piggy bank that, after a short delay, explodes destroying obstacles and dealing 1600 damage to opponents within range. Cooldown: 20 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}Griff lobs a piggy bank that explodes after a 2.5-second delay, dealing ~286% the damage as one coin and knocking enemies back while also destroying obstacles in a 2.67-tile radius. The enemies can be knocked back and damaged even if they were behind a wall (including indestructible walls).

{{Quote|Griff can shoot the piggy bank to charge up and increase its explosion radius and damage! The piggy bank explode instantly when it's fully charged.}}{{Buffie|Gadget}}
With the Buffie, Griff's piggy bank has 5040 health (which is equal to 9 coins' worth of damage, without any outside effects), which he can shoot to charge it and increase its explosion radius and damage. If its health is depleted, it becomes fully charged and the piggy bank explodes instantly. Note that allies cannot damage the piggy bank. It can deal up to 50% more damage and destroy obstacles in up to a 50% larger radius when fully charged. The piggy bank's health scales with outside buffs such as [[Showdown|Power Cubes]].

### Gadgets / Coin Shower

{{Quote|Griff throws a shower of coins at the target area, dealing 400 damage per second to opponents inside. Cooldown: 20 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}Griff throws a shower of coins in a 2.4-tile radius on the map that will damage enemies inside it, dealing 400 damage every second. The effect lasts for 5 seconds, dealing a total of 2000 damage if an opponent is inside the area for the whole duration of the Gadget.

{{Quote|The coins shower grows in size throughout its duration.}}{{Buffie|Gadget}}
With the Buffie, the radius of the shower of coins gradually increases in size for the duration, up to a radius of 4 tiles.

### Star Powers / Keep the Change

{{Quote|Griff gains a slightly wider attack and adds another row of coins to the coin toss.}}{{StarPower|1}}Griff's attack is 50% wider and shoots a 4th row of coins, making him unload his attack slower than without the Star Power.

{{Quote|Attacking with full ammo also fires a mega coin that deals extra damage!}}{{Buffie|Star}}
With the Buffie, when Griff has full ammo, the middle coin becomes 60% larger in width, and it deals 50% more damage.

### Star Powers / Business Resilience

{{Quote|Every 2 seconds, Griff regains up to 15% of his missing health.}}{{StarPower|2}}This Star Power heals Griff for 15% of his missing health every 2 seconds, so the less health Griff has, the more he will heal. Unlike self-regeneration, this Star Power heals Griff even when attacking or taking damage. The amount of health Griff can heal can be affected by outside health buffs or healing debuffs.

{{Quote|When Griff's Super projectiles return, they heal Griff for 5% of his missing health.}}{{Buffie|Star}}
With the Buffie, when Griff's Super projectiles return to him, they will Griff for 5% of his missing health. The duplicated banknotes from the Hypercharge count separately, which would heal him for 10% of the missing health in total.

### Hypercharge: Tax Rebate

{{Quote|Griff's banknotes multiply at max range before returning, with the newly created banknotes after a delay. They also pierce the environment.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Griff's Super passes through enemies and obstacles, and travels faster than his regular Super. In addition, his Super spawns another wave of 5 banknotes at max range that return after a 0.5-second delay, similarly passing through enemies and obstacles. Griff also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

{{Quote|Griff's main attack also pierces enemies.}}{{Buffie|Hyper}}
With the Buffie, Griff's attack can also pierce through targets. However, his reload speed is slightly slower while this effect is active. The Buffie additionally increases Griff's Hypercharge duration by 2 seconds.

### Tips / Game Modes and Maps

*Griff's attack and Super’s high damage output coupled with his reasonable reload speed make him viable in [[Heist]]. Griff's Keep The Change Star Power greatly increases his damage, making it a lot more punishing for opponents to leave him alone with the Safe. His Piggy Bank Gadget is useful in defense and offense to knock enemies away from the team’s Safe or to deal extra damage to the enemy safe, and can additionally be used to destroy walls and make it easier for your team to reach the enemy Safe.
*Griff has high damage potential high-close, making it easy for him to open Boxes and defeat Tanks and Assassins in [[Showdown]]. His Business Resilience Star Power additionally helps him survive longer during fights.
*Griff is a good pick in [[Brawl Ball]], as he is able to open maps to score easy goals with his Piggy Bank Gadget. His high damage output additionally helps him defeat opponents who are carrying the ball easily.
*Griff is good in [[Basket Brawl]], as the tighter nature of the maps make his Super a lot easier to hit and recharge. His Piggy Bank Gadget is additionally able to break the walls commonly found around the map.
*Griff is a good pick in [[Hot Zone]], and has various abilities that help him in the mode. His Super can cover the whole zone and deny access to the enemy team, and his Piggy Bank Gadget can open walls to either make access to the zone easier to your team, or destroy any hiding spots for the enemies while your team attacks them from the Zone, and his Coin Shower Gadget can help take control of the zone. Additionally, his Keep the Change Star Power makes it easier to hit enemies within the zone, and his Business Resilience Star Power allows him to stay on the zone for longer while under enemy fire.

### Tips / Recommended Build

*Griff's Keep the Change Star Power offers him more protection against close-ranged Brawlers as it deals more damage at close range. Griff’s Super can be combined with his attack to defend against those Brawlers.
*Griff’s Business Resilience Star Power will heal you even when you’re attacking or taking damage. You can use this to heal while fighting, which makes it more difficult for enemies to defeat you, or to heal while working towards a mode objective as enemies will be attacking you while doing so.
*Griff's Piggy Bank Gadget can be very useful at breaking walls in maps, making him a great supportive option alongside long-range Brawlers such as [[Brock]], [[Colt]], [[8-Bit]], [[Nani]], [[Pearl]], and [[Rico]]. This is especially useful in [[Brawl Ball]], where opening the opposing side's walls can allow for the ball to be scored easier.
*Griff's Coin Shower Gadget can be useful for taking control of the Zone in [[Hot Zone]], or for dealing damage while you're unable to directly attack the Safe or the Turrets in [[Heist]] and [[Brawl Arena]].
*Though Griff's Keep the Change Star Power increases his damage per ammo, it actually decreases his DPS as it takes longer for him to finish his attack, making it easier to miss projectiles. It additionally delays his ammo reload, limiting its viability.
*Griff's Piggy Bank Gadget is also helpful for area control, as it will limit where the enemy can move for a few seconds if Griff doesn't hit it. It makes it easier to predict the opponents' movements and as a consequence, it makes it easier to aim and hit shots.

### Tips / Strategies

*If enemies are hiding and taking cover behind obstacles, you can use his Piggy Bank Gadget to destroy the obstacles and burst them down afterwards.
*If an assassin, such as [[Edgar]] or [[Mortis]] is approaching you, dropping your Piggy Bank Gadget and shooting it will push them back and deal damage to them, making it easier to avoid and defeat them. Make sure you have enough ammo to quickly detonate the Piggy Bank.

### Tips / Other

*Although Griff can hit more banknotes with his Super at close range, the Super deals less damage at close range which results in less Super charge. Prioritize his Super on farther enemies to deal more damage, recharge more of his Super, and give a sufficient amount of time to reposition himself so that the banknotes hit enemies while they’re returning.
*Griff struggles in long-ranged combat due to his slow unload speed. His attack does deal high damage, but its full potential requires Griff to be at close range. However, Griff can still be effective at long-ranged combat if he attacks enemies at the tip of his range, where he can hit most of or all of the coins.
*Griff's Super can force enemies out of where it travelled. You can use this for area denial such as blocking off the zones in [[Hot Zone]], the Gem mine in [[Gem Grab]], or the Bolt spawns in [[Siege]].
*Griff’s Super has high burst damage regardless of range. Because of this, his Super can be used in a lot of situations such as using it to devastate enemies directly, keeping close-ranged enemies away from you, or to quickly defeat enemy Gem carriers in Gem Grab. Griff's Super is still active even if he is defeated, dealing a little more damage and allowing for some Super recharge after being defeated.
*Try to hit at least 2 or more opponents with his Super, as this will give Griff back more of his Super, and put most Brawlers into a range in which they can be finished off with Griff's main attack or another Super. This is rather easy to do due to the long range and wide spread of Griff's Super.
*Despite his high damage, Griff's slow unload speed may betray him if he's attempting to ambush opponents. To succesfully defeat opponents in an ambush, Griff would ideally have or be close to a charged Super, as it deals more damage than his main attack up close, and shoots quicker as well.
