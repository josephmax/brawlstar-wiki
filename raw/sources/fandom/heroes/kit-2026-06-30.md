Kit is a {{Rarity|Legendary}} [[Brawler]] who has a short range, a very fast reload and movement speed, moderately low health, and a moderate damage output, but immense utility with his Super in his normal mode. In his alternate form, he has a long range and very high damage output. He was the 75th Brawler added to the game. Kit's normal form attacks with a quick short-ranged claw with an extremely fast cooldown and reload speed. His Trait allows his Super to charge itself over time. For his Super, Kit quickly jumps a moderate distance. If he jumps on an enemy Brawler, he stuns and deals damage to them over time. If Kit jumps on an ally Brawler, he momentarily rides them, transforming into his alternate form and healing them over time in the process. Kit's alternate form attacks by throwing his ball of yarn over obstacles that blow up and deal high damage in a medium radius, while making him invulnerable for the duration of the alternate form. For his alternate form's Super, Kit jumps off the ally Brawler and transforms back into his normal form. His first Gadget, Cardboard Box, doubles his Trait's Super charge rate if he is standing still and makes him temporarily invisible to enemies. His second Gadget, Cheeseburger, immediately heals him and the ally he is attached to. His first Star Power, Power Hungry, increases the health and damage buffs from every [[Showdown|Power Cube]] collected. His second Star Power, Overly Attached, increases Kit's alternate form duration when attached to an ally. His Hypercharge, Making Biscuits, allows Kit to move and attack normally, while stunning enemies with his Super, but when using his Super on an ally, he heals them more, and his attack range increases in the alternate form.

### Trait

{{Quote|This Brawler charges Super over time.}}{{Trait|Auto}}Kit's Super charges automatically over time, similar to [[Darryl]]. It takes 20 seconds to fully charge his Super without other Super-charging abilities; however, unlike Darryl, this Trait does not charge his Hypercharge.

### Attacks / Basic: Claws

{{Quote|Claw nearby enemies in a cone.}}
Kit attacks with a quick short-ranged claw, hitting enemies in a wide cone. There exists an extremely short 0.1-second delay to the attack, but there isn’t any travel time, so the attack instantly takes effect after the delay. However, the attack cooldown overall is very short, and it takes 0.4 seconds to complete.

### Attacks / Attached: Yarn Balls

{{Quote|Kit throws yarn balls while attached to a friendly Brawler, dealing damage in an area.}}
Kit throws a ball of yarn over obstacles that bounces upon hitting the ground, and blows up after 0.8 seconds, dealing massive damage to any enemies caught in its 1.5-tile blast radius. However, Kit has a slow reload speed and slower attack cooldown in this form.

### Super: Carry Me

{{Quote|Jump and attach to the back of another Brawler. Heals allies and damages enemies over time.}}
Similar to [[Edgar]], Kit jumps into the air and over obstacles briefly for 0.5 seconds before landing onto the area where the Super was aimed. If there are lakes, his jump range is extended to the nearest ground. While in the air, Kit is completely immune to all damage besides status effects and damage applied over time. When he lands on an enemy Brawler, he instantly deals damage to them, and stays attached to them for 1.5 seconds, during which he stuns them and deals low damage over time. Kit cannot move or attack while attached to an enemy; however, he can hold the ball in [[Brawl Ball]]. 

When Kit lands on an ally Brawler, he transforms into his alternate form, becomes immune to all damage, attaches to his teammate for 8 seconds, and instantly recharges his Super. While attached, Kit heals both himself and the Brawler he is attached to over time for 15% of their maximum health per second. If the duration ends, the ally Brawler is defeated, or Kit uses his Super in the alternate form, he jumps off, transforming back into his normal form and loses his Super charge. If he uses his Super to detach, he dashes a short distance; otherwise, he drops in the same spot he was immediately before detaching. Kit cannot jump on allies or enemies if they have an invulnerability shield.

### Gadgets / Cardboard Box

{{Quote|Hide in a cardboard box becoming invisible for 3 seconds. Charges the Super 100% faster while stationary. Cooldown: 23 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
[[File:Visibility Warning.png|left|40x40px|link=]]
When activated, Kit hides in a cardboard box, turning himself invisible for 3 seconds, allowing him to either retreat or ambush an enemy. Similar to [[Leon]]'s Super, if Kit takes damage during the Gadget activation, he will momentarily be visible. If Kit uses his main attack while invisible, he loses his invisibility, but if he uses his Super, he does not. In addition, if Kit stands still, the effectiveness of his Super charge rate from his Trait is doubled. This Gadget can be activated in both his normal form and his alternate form, though using it in his alternate form is useless.

### Gadgets / Cheeseburger

{{Quote|Heals himself and the friendly Brawler he is attached to for 30%. Cooldown: 15 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
Upon activation, Kit heals both himself and the friendly Brawler he is attached to for 30% of their maximum health. This Gadget can only be activated if Kit is in his alternate form.

### Star Powers / Power Hungry

{{Quote|Kit gains 150% power from each Power Cube.}}{{StarPower|1}}
This Star Power increases the damage and health buffs from every [[Showdown|Power Cube]] collected by 1.5x.

### Star Powers / Overly Attached

{{Quote|Kit attaches to friendly Brawlers for 5 seconds longer.}}{{StarPower|2}}
With this Star Power equipped, Kit's alternate form lasts 5 seconds longer, increasing its duration from 8 to 13 seconds.

### Hypercharge: Making Biscuits

{{Quote|Kit's Super stuns the enemy but he can move and attack normally. Attaching to teammates will heal them more and his Yarn Balls have a longer range.}}{{Hypercharge}}
While activated, when Kit uses his Super on an enemy Brawler, he will stun the enemy whilst still being able to move and attack. However, he will no longer deal damage over time to enemies he hits. If Kit uses his Super on a friendly Brawler, he heals them for 25% of their maximum health per second instead of 15%, and his attack range in the alternate form increases significantly. However, Kit will not heal himself for twice as much as normal. The healing on the friendly Brawler is increased for the Hypercharge's duration and goes back down to normal once the Hypercharge ends, but the attack's additional range stays until the Super duration ends. Kit also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield.

### Tips

*Kit's Super is best used in team game modes, where he can attach to an ally and deal massive damage with his attack, while also healing himself.
*Do not jump on an enemy if they have their teammates near them. Instead, use the Super on isolated enemies to allow your teammates to finish them off, or onto low health enemies to defeat them.
*Kit is a decent choice in [[Showdown]] as while he lacks in damage output and health, he has a lot of mobility and map control to make up for it. His Power Hungry Star Power is an excellent choice for Showdown as every two Power Cubes he obtains counts as three, so he gets more powerful quicker than others. He works better in [[Duo Showdown]] as he can heal his teammates, control and damage his enemies while being invulnerable, and acquire double Power Cube buffs. His Cardboard Box Gadget is also useful, as it allows him to speed up his Super charge rate, sneak up on enemies, or flee from enemies.
*Be aware that Kit's Super will be cancelled if used on enemies currently moving during their Super such as [[Buzz]], [[Fang]], [[Chuck]], [[Berry]], [[Darryl]], or [[Bull]], with Kit even being pulled along and suffering the aftereffects of the Super. As such, try not to target such Brawlers when they're in situations where they are anticipating using their Super, granting you the element of surprise.
*If Kit is attached to a teammate when a round resets, he will have his Super when the next round begins. This allows Kit to use his yarn ball attack to guarantee a takedown without worrying about wasting his Super.

### Tips / Strategies

*Kit is powerful with tanks such as [[Draco]], [[Frank]], [[Hank]], [[El Primo]], [[Bull]], or [[Darryl]] as Kit can instantly heal a good amount of health with his Cheeseburger Gadget and do a good amount of damage when attached. Kit can also heal a good amount of HP overtime to the attached teammate.
