{{Quote|Damian LOVES to make noise. He fronts a band with his brother, [[Draco]], but music puts him into an uncontrollable rage, where he destroys anything in sight! His shows usually end with him smashing everything near the stage... including other Brawlers.}}{{Portrait}}
Damian is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who has a variable damage output, high health, a fast movement and reload speed, and a short range. He was the 102nd Brawler added to the game. Damian's Trait allows him to charge his Super from enemies damaging him. He attacks throwing a punch that charges his bar; once it charges, the next attack knocks enemies back, and, after a short delay, they explode. Enemies caught in the radius will explode in a chain reaction. For his Super, he jumps over obstacles, and enemies will be trapped in a large radius when he lands. For his first Gadget, Spiritual Healing, he lobs a microphone that, upon being picked up, heals himself and allies. For his second Gadget, Wall of Sound, he throws a wall of 3 unbreakable speakers. His first Star Power, Crowdkill, temporarily stuns enemies after being knocked back to a wall by his charged attack. His second Star Power, Vulgar Display of Punch, instantly charges his bar during his Super. His Hypercharge, Jump in the Fire, allows his Super to leave a burning area inside the mosh pit, burning enemies in the process.

### Trait

{{Quote|This Brawler charges Super from damage received.}}{{Trait|Tank}}Whenever Damian takes damage from enemy Brawlers or spawnables, he charges his Super. The Super charge rate depends on the damage the enemy deals. It takes 2.4x Damian's max health of damage to charge his Super completely, and all damage is calculated after shielding and other effects.

### Attack: Power Trio

{{Quote|Damian throws a vicious punch. After 2 punches in a row, his next attack is an explosive kick that knocks back enemies sets them on fire. After a delay, this fire explodes and spreads to nearby enemies.}}
Damian initially throws a close-ranged, piercing punch that deals low damage. When Damian hits a punch, he will fill a bar by 50%, and if he hits the next punch, the bar will fill, empowering his next attack. This causes the next attack to deal extra damage, knock back enemies 4 tiles, and marking them. This mark causes an 2.67-tile radius explosion centered on the enemy after a 1.5-second delay, or instantly if they die from the punch. Any other target that is hit by the explosion but survives will also be marked and explode themselves after the delay. However, if they are defeated from other sources of damage during the delay, or if Damian himself is defeated, the explosion will not trigger. The explosion's Super charge rate scales with Power Levels, and also with any damage buff or debuff.

When it's charged up and ready, the bar slowly depletes over a 5-second time span, and once it's empty, he loses the empowered attack and must charge it again. However, the bar does not decay while partially charged. Hitting multiple enemies in one attack will instantly charge the bar, though it will not instantly use the empowered attack if three or more enemies are hit. Missing an attack will fully deplete the bar regardless of its progress, meaning Damian can waste his empowered attack if he hits no enemies. Despite that, he gains a 30% speed boost for 1.5 seconds when he uses the empowered attack, regardless of whether it lands or not.

For both attacks, the projectile increases in width the farther it travels. Also, Damian's attack cooldown is reduced to zero for the moment he switches from his normal attack to his charged attack.

### Super: Mosh Pit

{{Quote|Damian leaps and creates a personal Mosh Pit lasting 5 seconds. Inside the pit, enemies are knocked back when hitting speakers, dealing 1600 Damage.}}
Damian jumps a long distance, staying airborne for 1 second, regardless of the distance traveled. If required to cross over a larger amount of blocks or over lakes, his jumping distance will be increased up to a limit or to the nearest piece of ground. While in the air, Damian is completely immune to all damage besides status effects and damage applied over time. Upon landing, he creates a mosh pit in a large circle centered on him, with the boundaries of the circle consisting of 10 sets of speakers. The speakers are similar to walls, except they only last 5 seconds, do not impede the movement or projectiles of Damian and his teammates, and can be destroyed, having 2000 health. Since they are actually spawnables with health, attacks that can break walls will not instantly destroy the speakers, instead both piercing and damaging them. Using another Super or being defeated will instantly make the current set of speakers disappear.

The speakers have an effect on Brawlers that touch them depending on their position: if they are outside the mosh pit, they are knocked away with no damage, but if they are inside it, they take moderate damage and are launched in the air and pushed towards the center by 1.4 tiles. The speakers will also knockback Brawlers they spawn on top of toward the center, increasing the Super's effective range. While in the air, the enemies can't move or use their attack, Super, Gadget, or Hypercharge, but will be completely immune to all damage besides status effects and damage applied over time. Enemies can be pulled over walls and other obstacles; if required to cross over a larger amount of blocks or over lakes (including the speakers themselves), their travelling distance will be increased up to a limit or to the nearest piece of ground. The speakers will break instantly when knocking back an enemy from the inside, but not when pushing them from the outside.

### Gadgets / Spiritual Healing

{{Quote|Damian throws his microphone to a target location. The first teammate to pick it up is healed for 3200 health. Cooldown: 15 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
When aiming using the Gadget button, Damian lobs a microphone that heals himself and allies when picked up after a short delay. The microphone can be picked up even if the player that picked it up is at full health.

### Gadgets / Wall of Sound

{{Quote|Damian summons a temporary wall of indestructible amps! Cooldown: 20 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
When aiming using the Gadget button, Damian creates a wall of 3 speakers each covering a tile that lasts for 7 seconds. Despite what the description may imply, this wall acts like any other wall in the game and can be destroyed with wall-breaking abilities. The spawn pattern of the walls is always a line, roughly perpendicular to the direction he throws them. Unlike [[Sprout]]'s walls, these walls will not be destroyed upon reaching overtime in [[Brawl Ball]].

### Star Powers / Crowdkill

{{Quote|Knocking a target onto a wall stuns them for 0.5 sec.}}{{StarPower|1}}
When Damian knocks a target onto a wall with the charged attack, they will be stunned for 0.5 seconds. The speakers from his Super do not count for this effect.

### Star Powers / Vulgar Display of Punch

{{Quote|Damian has a flaming fist during his Super, which burns enemies.}}{{StarPower|2}}
When Damian uses his Super, he instantly charges his bar and his next attack is empowered.

### Hypercharge: Jump in the Fire

{{Hypercharge}}
{{Quote|Damian's Super also sets the ground on fire when he lands, dealing 1200 damage per sec to enemies caught in his mosh pit!}}
When activated, Damian's Super leaves a burning area on the ground that lasts for 4 seconds. It deals 1200 damage, but it can only hit 3 times if the enemy remains standing on the area. He also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

### Tips

*At the start of the match, try to use the Tank trait to get your first Super. Once you have the Super, you can launch yourself into the enemies to easily attack and regain another Super.
*When you are using the Wall of Sound Gadget, make sure the rotation of the wall is correct (horizontal or vertical).
*If you are playing [[Gem Grab]] and you are the Gem carrier, you can use the Super to defend yourself by moving to a safe place. The walls created by the Super will impede the opponents from reaching you.
**Alternatively, you can use the Super to move to the place with a lot of enemies to make it difficult for them to leave the area, which will lessen the number of opponents your team's Gem carrier has to deal with.
*Try to land the Super in such a way that the enemies are located on the rim of the Super so they take damage and get knocked in to you for colliding with the boomboxes.
*In [[Brawl Arena]], make full use of the bots to chain your explosions off of; not only can the do immense chain damage when positioned near a turret, but they can function as handy ways of spreading the explosions to opponents just out of your reach, seeing as they're launched slightly further than brawlers. All of these explosions also help Damian charge his Super extremely fast.
