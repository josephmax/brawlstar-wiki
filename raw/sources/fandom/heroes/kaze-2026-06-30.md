{{Quote|Kaze was raised to be a living weapon by her parents, but this was not the life she wanted. After escaping to Starr Park with [[Kenji]], she can now live how she wants. However, she is still torn between the two aspects of her life, being a humble geisha and a deadly ninja.}}{{Portrait}}
Kaze is an {{Rarity|Ultra Legendary}} [[Brawler]] with moderately high health and two modes. She was the 92nd Brawler added to the game. In her geisha aspect, she has a very fast movement speed, a variable damage output, and a very fast reload speed, and immense utility with her attack. She attacks by dashing forward and slashing, dealing moderate damage. For her Super, she throws a hand fan which summons a storm that makes her and allies invisible to enemies in it, dealing damage overtime to enemies that touch the outer edge. In her ninja aspect, she has a normal movement speed, a higher damage output at close range, and immense utility with her Super, at the expense of having a moderately low reload speed. Kaze attacks by throwing two knives that deal more damage up close. For her Super, she travels a long distance quickly. Enemies caught in her path will be temporarily marked, which detonates shortly after for high damage. For her first Gadget, Gracious Host, Kaze gains a temporary movement speed boost when switching to ninja, and recovers health when switching to geisha. For her second Gadget, Hensojutsu, Kaze becomes temporary invisible to enemies when switching to ninja, and dashes forward when switching to geisha. Her first Star Power, Advanced Techniques, temporary slows enemies down if she hits enemies in the Strike Spot in her geisha aspect, and nearby enemies will be damaged when marked targets explode in her ninja aspect. Her second Star Power, Gratuity Included, removes ammo from any enemy Brawler caught in the storm in her geisha aspect, and increases the duration of her Gadgets in her ninja aspect. Her Hypercharge, Ancient Energy, allows her Super to slightly damage enemies inside it over time in her geisha aspect, and increases the Super dash speed, while instantly damaging marked enemies in her ninja aspect.

### Ultra Trait

{{Quote|2 Brawlers in 1! Activate Gadget to change aspect!}}
{{Gadget|0}}
Kaze has two aspects, geisha and ninja, with different main attacks and Supers. Her Gadgets, Star Powers, and Hypercharge affect each aspect differently. She starts in her geisha aspect at the start of the battle and whenever she respawns, and uses her Gadget to swap aspects. Uniquely, if Kaze is Power 1-6 or does not own a Gadget, she will have a free default Gadget with a 6-second cooldown that only switches aspects. This Gadget becomes unusable once Kaze has either Gracious Host, Hensojutsu, or both.

### Attacks / Kaze's Delivery Service

{{Quote|Kaze dashes a short distance and strikes the nearest target. Kaze has a 'Strike Spot' at a fixed angle around herself.<br>Hitting an enemy in a Strike Spot deals 200% damage and resets the Strike Spot to a new position.}}
In her geisha aspect, Kaze dashes forward a short distance, similar to [[Mortis]] and [[Kenji]], and then slashes in a short 2-tile range circle around her, dealing moderate damage, but not behind walls. Like many dashing abilities, Kaze's dash will be cut short if she attempts to dash into walls or water, causing her to slash early. Unlike Mortis and Kenji, the dash itself deals no damage. Kaze's attack also features a Strike Spot that initially covers a 120-degree cone at the top of the circle; if an enemy is hit within the cone, they will take double damage and the Strike Spot will be repositioned 90 degrees clockwise after. More specifically, the centre of the enemy's hitbox must be within the 120-degree cone.

### Attacks / Silent Shuriken

{{Quote|Kaze throws 2 knives in succession, dealing increased damage to closer targets.}}
In her ninja aspect, Kaze fires two knives in a line. Targets closer to Kaze take the most damage, and targets near Kaze's maximum range take significantly less damage, similar to [[Leon]] and [[Chuck]]. The second knife thrown deals slightly more damage at all ranges except at minimum range.

### Supers / Fan Storm

{{Quote|Kaze throws her fans, creating a deadly storm of fans in an area. Enemies within the Fan Storm can't see outside of it. The Fan Storm will also deal damage to an enemy if they touch it.}}
In her geisha aspect, Kaze throws a hand fan that creates a storm in a large radius that lasts for 5 seconds. Enemies inside are unable to see Brawlers or projectiles outside of the storm, and any projectiles will not be visible to them once they travel outside the storm. Enemies that are caught at the edge of the storm will be damaged, and the damage can hit multiple times if the enemy remains standing on it. More specifically, the centre of the enemy's hitbox must be within 1 tile of the edge.

### Supers / Fist of the Brawl Star

{{Quote|Kaze dashes a long distance and marks enemies she passes through. Marks explode after 2.5 seconds.}}
In her ninja aspect, Kaze quickly dashes 9 tiles. Enemies caught in her path will be marked for 2.5 seconds, after which the marks detonate, dealing high damage. Kaze can't use her Super to dash over walls or water.

### Gadgets

In both aspects, both her Gadgets' cooldowns start immediately after they are activated.

### Gadgets / Gracious Host

{{Quote|Switch to Ninja Aspect and gain 30% Movement Speed for 2.5 sec. Cooldown: 6 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}}
When activated, Kaze switches to her ninja aspect and gains a 30% movement speed buff for 2.5 seconds.

{{Quote|Switch to Geisha Aspect and heal for 30% of max health. Cooldown: 6 seconds.}}
When activated, Kaze switches to her geisha aspect and recovers 30% of her maximum health.

### Gadgets / Hensojutsu

{{Quote|Switch to Ninja Aspect and gain short invisibility. Cooldown: 6 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}}
[[File:Visibility Warning.png|left|40x40px|link=]]
When activated, Kaze switches to her ninja aspect and turns invisible for 1.5 seconds. If Kaze takes damage or uses her main attack during the Gadget's duration, her invisibility is cancelled, but if she uses her Super, it is not. 

{{Quote|Switch to Geisha Aspect and dash forward. Cooldown: 6 seconds.}}
When activated, Kaze switches to her geisha aspect, and she quickly dashes 3.33 tiles forward.

### Star Powers / Advanced Techniques

{{Quote|Hitting an enemy in a Strike Spot also slows them by 30% for 2.5 sec.}}{{StarPower|1}}
Enemies hit by Kaze's Strike Spot will be slowed down by 30% for 2.5 seconds.

{{Quote|When a marked enemy is defeated, they will deal area damage to other enemies.}}
When marked enemies are defeated, the explosion will also affect nearby enemies within 2 tiles for the same amount of damage.

### Star Powers / Gratuity Included

{{Quote|Removes 34% ammo from brawlers initially caught in the Fan Storm.}}{{StarPower|2}}
In her geisha aspect, enemies caught in Kaze's storm will lose one-third of their ammo when the storm lands. However, enemies walking in the existing storm will not lose their ammo. In addition, this only affects enemies hit by the centre of the storm, enemies only hit by the edge will not be affected. More specifically, the centre of the enemy's hitbox must not be within 1 tile of the edge. This Star Power does not work against the [[Heist]] safe, the IKE turret in [[Siege]], or spawnables.

{{Quote|Increases duration of Gadget effects by 50%.}}
In her ninja aspect, this Star Power increases the duration of her Gadgets by 50%, resulting in a 3.75-second speed boost with Gracious Host or 2.25 seconds of invisibility with Hensojutsu.

### Hypercharge: Ancient Energy

In both aspects, Kaze gains a 20% speed boost, and a 5% damage and shield boost.{{Quote|The center of the Fan Storm damages enemies over time.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated in her geisha aspect, Kaze's storm now deals 136 damage per second, including the Hypercharge damage buff to enemies, up to a maximum of 680 damage over 5 seconds. This only affects enemies hit by the centre of the storm, enemies only hit by the edge will not be affected. More specifically, the centre of the enemy's hitbox must not be within 1 tile of the edge. This effect can stack with multiple storms.

{{Quote|Kaze gains increased dash speed and her marks instantly damage if the target's health is low enough to be defeated.}}
When activated in her ninja aspect, Kaze's Super dashes 30% faster and marked enemies will be damaged instantly if the damage would be enough to kill them. The threshold is 2720 by default and is affected by damage buffs and debuffs on Kaze, including the Hypercharge damage buff, bringing the starting threshold to 2856. This ability can activate even if the target is over the threshold if the target has damage-increasing features such as [[Belle]]'s Super, [[R-T]]'s attack, and [[Draco]]'s Expose Star Power.

### Tips / General

* Her geisha form has a very fast movement speed whereas her Ninja form only has normal movement speed. Try to use her geisha form to maneuver around the map as well as approach enemies. Due to her very quick reload speed in geisha form, she can also switch between forms to reload.
* Her ninja form Super is very versatile and strong. Try to gain positioning using geisha form and then switch to ninja form to Super onto enemies and ambush them.
