Bull is a {{Rarity|Rare}} [[Brawler]] who has high health, a normal reload speed, great mobility with his Super and his fast movement speed, a medium range, and a high damage output at close range. He was one of the 15 Brawlers who released at the first Beta launch. His Trait allows him to charge his Super from enemies damaging him. His attack is a shot of multiple shotgun shells that deal high damage up close. His Super makes him charge in a certain direction, damaging enemies and destroying obstacles in his way, allowing him to get closer to or escape from enemies. His first Gadget, T-Bone Missile, partially heals him after hitting an enemy Brawler. His second Gadget, Stomper, allows Bull to briefly slow enemies down; if the enemies are already slowed, it will stun them instead. His first Star Power, Berserker, doubles his reload speed when he falls below 60% health. His second Star Power, Tough Guy, shields him when he falls below 30% health. His Hypercharge, Jaws of Steel, allows his Super to gain a significant damage-reduction shield while it's active.

### Trait

{{Quote|This Brawler charges Super from damage received.}}{{Trait|Tank}}Whenever Bull takes damage from an enemy Brawler and the enemy Brawler's spawnables, he charges his Super. The Super charge rate depends on the damage the enemy deals. It takes 2.4x Bull's max health of damage to charge his Super completely, and all damage is calculated after shielding and other effects.

### Attack: Double-Barrel

{{Quote|Bull's double-barreled shotgun deals heavy damage. It has very short range, so Bull likes to get up close and personal.}}Bull fires a burst of 5 medium-ranged shotgun shells that deal little damage by themselves. However, if all of Bull's shells hit, they can deal very high damage. The range of Bull's attack is much shorter than [[Shelly]]'s, but Bull's shells deal much more damage. The attack deals more damage at close-range since more shells are likely to hit the enemy.

### Super: Bulldozer

{{Quote|Bull puts his head down and bulldozes through obstacles, damaging opponents along the way and slowing nearby enemies at the end. He's always been headstrong!}}Bull charges forward in the direction he aims his Super. He destroys obstacles he charges into, and knocks back and damages enemies in his path. At the end of the range, he slows enemies within a 3-tile radius for 2.5 seconds. Note that Bull can still take damage while using Bulldozer, unlike airborne abilities. Bull's Super cannot be aimed so that his final destination would be in water. Knockbacks won't interrupt his Super, but stuns will.

### Gadgets / T-Bone Missile

{{Quote|Bull throws a projectile at the target Brawler, dealing 1600 damage and healing for 1600 if he hits. Cooldown: 18 seconds.}}
{{Gadget|1}}
When aiming using the Gadget button, shoots a shot that heals him for 100% of the damage dealt to an enemy Brawler. This Gadget can't be used when Bull is using his Super.

{{Quote|Also marks the target for 5 sec, gaining 25% damage against the target as health.}}{{Buffie|Gadget}}
With the Buffie, the Gadget hit also marks them, causing Bull to heal for 25% of the damage he deals against that target for 5 seconds.

### Gadgets / Stomper

{{Quote|Bull's stomps the ground, slowing nearby enemies. If a target is already slowed, they are stunned! Cooldown: 20 seconds.}}
{{Gadget|2}}
Bull immediately stomps the ground in a 2.67-tile radius, slowing all enemies caught by 50% for 1 second. However, if he uses the Gadget on a target that's already slowed down, he will instead stun them for that duration. There is no direction of travel to this Gadget, so aiming is not required, though aiming can be used to show you the area-of-effect. The stun activates even if the slow didn't originate from Bull.

{{Quote|Stunning an enemy grants +30% movement speed for 3 sec.}}{{Buffie|Gadget}}
With the Buffie, when Bull stuns an enemy, he will also receive a 30% movement speed boost for 3 seconds.

### Star Powers / Berserker

{{Quote|When Bull falls below 60% health, his reload speed doubles!}}
{{StarPower|1}}
When Bull's health falls below 60% of its maximum, his reload speed will double, allowing him to reload as fast as [[El Primo]] and [[Bibi]]. The effect is lost if Bull heals back above 60% health. When activated, he will turn red and angry particles will emit from him.

{{Quote|Defeating a Brawler while this effect is active instantly charges Bull's Super.}}{{Buffie|Star}}
With the Buffie, when Bull defeats a Brawler while his Star Power is active, his Super charges instantly.

### Star Powers / Tough Guy

{{Quote|When Bull falls below 30% health, he gains a shield that reduces all damage he takes by 30%.}}
{{StarPower|2}}
When Bull's health falls below than 30% of its maximum, he gains a damage reduction shield that reduces 30% of all damage he takes. The effect is lost if Bull heals back above 30% health.

{{Quote|Defeating a Brawler while this effect is active makes Bull immune to damage for 0.5 sec.}}{{Buffie|Star}}
With the Buffie, when Bull defeats a Brawler while his Star Power state is active, he becomes immune to damage for 0.5 seconds.

### Hypercharge: Jaws of Steel

{{Quote|Bulldozer reduces damage taken by 80%.}}{{Hypercharge}}
When Bull is actively using his Super, he will receive a shield that reduces damage he takes by 80% until the Super ends, whether from reaching the end of its range, Bull getting stunned, or hitting an indestructible wall. He also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost.

{{Quote|Bull's main attack deals less damage at long range but pierces all enemies.}}{{Buffie|Hyper}}
With the Buffie, Bull's main attack also pierces all targets, but only one projectile can deal damage to an enemy. The projectile's damage is scaled from 5 bullets' worth at minimum range to 1 bullet's worth at maximum range. The projectile speed and size are greatly increased, increasing the main attack's effective range. Increased Hypercharge Duration by 2 seconds.

### Tips / Game Modes and Maps

*Using his T-Bone Missile in Heist is usually a good choice, since Bull relies on the length of his Super to reach the Heist safe from a far distance away, so using Stomper is redundant if there are no enemies guarding it. T-Bone Missile also allows Bull to stay in the fight longer, which allows just enough time to deal a large fraction of the safe's health.
*Bull is very useful in [[Heist]], as he can rush over water and break the obstacles in front of the enemy safe. The opening that he creates by his Super can also be utilized by teammates to attack the Safe. He's also viable in the mode with his heavy damage at point-blank range, which can be utilized to destroy the Safe easily.
*Bull's Berserker Star Power doubles his reload speed, allowing Bull to have one of the highest overall damage outputs of any Brawler. Bull can use this power to dominate the robots in [[Boss Fight]] and [[Robo Rumble]]. Bull is also an ideal Power Cube Carrier in Boss Fight and a solid Brawler in [[Takedown]], as you can use the Power Cubes and Bull's high burst damage to engage the Big Boss, while surviving longer with his T-Bone Missile Gadget and his Tough Guy Star Power and his high health, or to defeat the Boss quicker with his Berserker Star Power and Stomper Gadget, respectively. You can also use this in Heist if you happen to be fairly low on health whilst approaching the safe to incinerate the enemy safe. While he is the Boss in [[Big Game]], you can also use this as an extra boost to his damage output, though being the Boss already boosts your reload speed tremendously.
*Bull is also effective in [[Brawl Ball]] with his high health and Super that destroys obstacles, because he can easily clear openings in the map, assisting his teammates in scoring. He can also self-pass, by kicking the Ball forward and charging towards it, then attempting to score. However, avoid using your Super to make enemies lose the ball by knocking them back, as he can break your team's goalpost and other vital walls, as this only assists the enemies and allows them freely to retrieve the ball and score. Instead, burst your attacks on them at close-range to quickly defeat them with your high damage.

### Tips / Recommended Build

*Bull's T-Bone Missile Gadget is extremely useful to heal up when in danger, especially if you don't have your Super to escape or don't have healers on your team, and it can also allow you to tank damage effectively.
*Use Bull's Stomper Gadget to catch flanking enemies by surprise. Bull's high burst damage will allow him to get shots before an enemy can react. In some cases, Stomper can also cancel Supers that will inadvertently lead you into danger or Supers that enemies have already countered. 
*Bull's Berserker Star Power is especially effective in [[Heist]] mode because it increases his attack speed when his health drops below 40%, allowing him to deal massive damage to the enemy safe. This rapid firing lets Bull unload a large amount of damage in a short period, making him a key asset for quickly chipping away at the safe’s health. With his tanky health pool, Bull can survive long enough to deal damage to the safe, ensuring he's consistently a threat during critical moments of the match.
*Bull's Tough Guy Star Power is a fantastic for survivability. It gives Bull a shield that reduces damage when his health gets low. Use this to your advantage when you're pushing into enemy territory, especially when you're low on health. It can make a huge difference in surviving long enough to get back to safety or finish off opponents.

### Tips / Strategies

*Bull's Super allows him to charge a very long distance in any direction, destroying all obstacles in his way. Since Bull can deal maximum damage when he's close to his target, this ability can be used for getting Bull close enough to enemies to defeat them. Make sure to aim you Super correctly and predict the enemy's movement, so you can reach the enemy properly to defeat them, or use his Stomper Gadget to get as close as possible to the enemy. Bull's Super can also come in handy when Bull needs to escape from enemies if in danger because of his Super's long charging distance, which is useful in [[Bounty]] when overwhelmed by enemies. However, he's not impervious to damage while charging, so be wary of nearby enemies. Also, be careful in [[Showdown]] when escaping as his Super's long distance may force you into the poison clouds, however, this can be circumvented with his Stomper Gadget to interrupt his charge.
*Using the Super will slow the enemies, so using Stomper Gadget right after can convert it into a stun.

### Tips / Other

*Bull can deal a lot of damage when he's within close-quarters of his target and has high health, but can't deal maximum damage when he isn't close. Take advantage of bushes, obstacles, or thin choke points to ambush and defeat enemies. Therefore, in more open areas or maps, try to dodge enemy attacks. Bull has a higher movement speed than most Brawlers so he can rush enemies and avoid their attacks more easily. It's ideal to dodge enemy attacks and not tank damage, as that allows enemies to quickly charge their Supers.
*Bull fares well with throwers who are great at area control such as [[Barley]], [[Tick]], [[Grom]], [[Dynamike]], or [[Larry & Lawrie]], especially in [[Duo Showdown]]. They can annoy enemies by placing their attacks everywhere, and Bull, being a Brawler with high health and damage output, can quickly finish those enemies off.
