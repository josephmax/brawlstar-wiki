Buster is a {{Rarity|Mythic}} [[Brawler]] who could be unlocked as a [[Brawl Pass]] reward at Tier 30 in Season 15: Ghost Station. He has high health, a fast movement speed, a medium attack range, and a potentially high damage output at close range, but a moderately slow reload speed. He was the 61st Brawler added to the game. His Trait allows Buster to passively charge his Super if an ally is within a radius of him. Buster attacks by projecting a wave of light in a cone that deals more damage up close. His Super is a barrier that blocks projectiles and converts them into a counterattack projectile. Buster's first Gadget, Utility Belt, heals allies inside his Trait radius with the healing increased with more allies in it. His second Gadget, Slo-Mo Replay, allows his next attack to pull in enemies. His first Star Power, Blockbuster, increases the damage of his attack for every ally inside his Trait radius. His second Star Power, Kevlar Vest, grants him a shield and immunity from slows, stuns, or knockbacks while his Super is active. His Hypercharge, Plot Armor, makes his Super a circular barrier that blocks projectiles and converts them into a counterattack projectile, which has increased damage.

### Trait

{{Quote|This Brawler charges Super by staying close to teammates.}}{{Trait|Radius}}
Buster's Trait is a 2.83-tile radius around him. All allies inside the radius charge Buster's Super. It charges his Super and Hypercharge faster if there are multiple allies inside the radius, and the amount of Super charge per second is 2.5% multiplied by one more than the number of allies present within the circle. This results in the Super charging by 5% per second each or over 20 seconds for one ally, and his Hypercharge by 2% per second each or over 50 seconds for one ally. Ally Busters will have their Trait radius shown, while enemy Busters will have their Trait radius hidden.

### Attack: Lens Flare

{{Quote|Buster shoots a cone of light from his projector, damaging all enemies within range.}} Buster projects a wave of light in a cone shape at a medium range that pierces through enemies. Targets closer to Buster take the most damage, and targets near Buster's maximum range take significantly less damage. Buster's attack has a fast cooldown of 0.35 seconds.

### Super: Montage

{{Quote|Buster projects a shield in front of him, blocking all enemy projectiles and reflecting the energy back towards enemies.}} Buster projects a reel barrier a short distance in front of himself for 3 seconds, which deletes enemy projectiles  on contact. This includes projectiles that can pierce through enemies or walls. However, lobbed projectiles will simply pass over the barrier, (i.e. Brawlers of the [[Artillery]] class' attacks, or [[Brock]], [[Amber]], [[Byron]], and [[Janet]]'s Supers). The barrier can block projectiles from all directions, including from behind, but the projectiles must come directly into contact with the barrier to be blocked, so Buster can still be hit from other angles or from inside the Super. The barrier is completely ineffective against area effects like [[Hank]], [[Jacky]], or [[Doug]]'s attack, as well as single-target melee attacks like that from [[Fang]] or [[Nita|Bruce]]. The barrier also does not interfere with the movement of enemy Brawlers or spawnables in any way, so it cannot block [[Mortis]], [[Mico]], or [[Kenji]]'s attack. It also does not block projectiles from allies.

When a projectile is blocked by Buster's shield, the energy gets reflected in back in the direction the projectile came from as a counterattack projectile that takes the form of a film reel. There is a small cooldown time of 0.25 seconds between reflected attacks, and projectiles blocked by the barrier within that cooldown will not be reflected. The counterattack projectiles don't charge Buster's Super, and the damage is constant and doesn't scale with the blocked projectile's damage, [[Showdown|Power Cubes]], or outside buffs. In addition, the counterattack projectiles will not copy any additional effects that the original projectiles had, such as the pull from Gene's Super.

Bounce attacks like [[Rico]] and [[Ruffs]]' can bounce off the barrier, and attacks that spawn a splash radius or other effect at the end will have their paths cut short by the barrier but will still spawn their effects (like [[Mr. P]], [[Squeak]], or Brock's attack). [[Emz]]'s attack gets absorbed by Buster's Super but doesn't get reflected. If Buster is stunned, pulled, knocked back, or uses his main attack while his Super is active, his Super will be cancelled. While his Super is active, his movement speed is decreased, and he cannot carry the ball in [[Brawl Ball]].

### Gadgets / Utility Belt

{{Quote|Buster heals himself and all nearby allies for 900 health for each ally in his Super charge area. Cooldown: 16 seconds.}}{{Gadget|1}} When activated, Buster and any allies in the radius of his Trait will instantly recover 9% of his maximum health for each ally inside of it.

### Gadgets / Slo-Mo Replay

{{Quote|Buster's next Lens Flare pulls enemies towards him. Cooldown: 17 seconds.}}{{Gadget|2}} When activated, Buster's next main attack will pull enemy Brawlers towards him by 3 tiles, like [[Frank]]'s Irresistible Attraction Gadget. A Gadget symbol will glow above Buster's head signaling the Gadget's usage, as well as a glowing joystick. The Gadget's cooldown starts after using this attack.

### Star Powers / Blockbuster

{{Quote|Buster's Lens Flare deals 15% more damage for each ally in his Super charge area.}}{{StarPower|1}} Buster's attack deal an additional 15% more damage to enemies for every ally in the radius of his Trait. This damage boost stacks with more allies inside of his Trait radius, and stacks with other damage boosts such as [[8-Bit]] and [[Belle]]'s Super.

### Star Powers / Kevlar Vest

{{Quote|While Montage is active Buster takes 10% less damage and is immune to knockbacks, slows and stuns.}}{{StarPower|2}} While Buster's Super is active, he gains a shield that reduces all damage he takes by 10% and immunity from slows, stuns, and knockbacks.

### Hypercharge: Plot Armor

{{Quote|Buster's Shield is now 360 degress around him and reflected projectiles deal more damage!}}{{Hypercharge}}
When activated, Buster projects a circular barrier for 3 seconds, which behaves identically to his normal Super. However, despite the description, the damage from the counterattack projectile is not increased. Buster also gains a 20% speed boost, as well as a 5% damage and shield boost, though the speed boost will be cancelled while the Super is active.

### Tips

*It is recommended for Buster to close the gap between him and the enemy, as Buster's main attack does significantly more damage up close than farther away. Take advantage of bushes, obstacles, or thin choke points to ambush and defeat enemies.
*Buster's wide and piercing attack makes it very effective against grouped-up enemies. Use this to your advantage to charge your Super faster.
*When using his Super, make sure to point it in the direction of possible enemy attacks. Remember to strafe in the same direction as the enemy to deflect their attacks and avoid getting hit from the side.
*Be mindful when using Buster's Super, as it can be interrupted (unless Buster has his Kevlar Vest Star Power equipped) and will cancel Buster's Super.
*Buster's barrier only counterattacks when it is hit, so it is possible to hit him from the side. Attacks from above, such as Artillery Brawlers, [[Willow]], and [[Berry]]'s main attack, will also not be reflected.
*Use his Utility Belt Gadget in more supportive game modes like [[Gem Grab]] and [[Bounty]]. Buster's Slo-Mo Replay Gadget is handier in more aggressive modes like [[Brawl Ball]], where he can disarm the ball carrier.
*Buster's Kevlar Vest is extremely useful against [[Bo]], as if you trigger a Bo's Super mine, then activate your Super you will not be knocked back, and you can withstand more damage.
*When dealing with Brawlers with slow unload speeds, such as [[8-Bit]], [[Griff]], [[Pam]], [[Lola]], [[Rico]], and [[Eve]], you can angle your Super to be placed right in front of them, completely preventing them from dealing damage and ensuring their projectiles are reflected.
