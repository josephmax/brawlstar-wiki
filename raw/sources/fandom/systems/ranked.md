## Visible Page Text

Ranked is a competitive mode unlocked from the Trophy Road at 1000 Trophies.

It replaced Power League in the February 2024 update.

In Ranked, Brawlers below Power Level 9 cannot be played.

In Mythic Ranks and above, Brawlers below Power Level 11 cannot be played.

Players can progress to higher Ranks, or drop to lower Ranks, based on the outcome of each Ranked match.
