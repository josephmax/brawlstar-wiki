## New Buffies

Fandom states that six Brawlers receive new Buffies in this update, with Buffies affecting Gadgets, Star Powers, and Hypercharge.

### Rico

- Gadget 1: Multiball Launcher rework. Rico deploys a 4000 HP vending machine; when destroyed, it fires projectiles in all directions.
- Multiball Launcher Buffied: shooting the vending machine loads it with more projectiles.
- Gadget 2: Bouncy Castle rework. Rico fires a giant gumball that splits into smaller projectiles when it hits a wall or enemy Brawler.
- Bouncy Castle Buffied: both projectile sets also slow enemy Brawlers.
- Star Power 1: Super Bouncy. After first bounce, main attack and Super bullets deal +240 damage.
- Super Bouncy Buffied: bounced bullets give 10% Super charge on hit.
- Star Power 2: Robo Retreat. Rico moves 35% faster when under 40% health.
- Robo Retreat Buffied: movement speed scales up to 60% at 10% HP.
- Hypercharge: Trick Shot King. Rico's Super projectiles bounce farther.
- Trick Shot King Buffied: projectile speed increases and main attack fires 2 additional projectiles.

### Brock

- Super: Rocket Rain rework. Rocket barrage now tracks Brock's movement.
- Gadget 1: Rocket Laces is now aimable; Brock rocket jumps, knocks back enemies, and deals 1000 damage on takeoff and landing.
- Rocket Laces Buffied: Brock gains a +2 sec movement speed boost when Rocket Laces hits an enemy Brawler.
- Gadget 2: Rocket Fuel fires a large terrain-destroying rocket for 1000 damage.
- Rocket Fuel Buffied: the mega rocket splits into 4 smaller rockets dealing less damage.
- Star Power 1: More Rockets adds an extra salvo to Brock's Super.
- More Rockets Buffied: Brock gains a speed boost while channeling Super.
- Star Power 2: Rocket No.4 increases Brock's ammo by 1.
- Rocket No.4 Buffied: every 4 sec, Brock's next attack gains increased range.
- Hypercharge: Rocket Barrage makes Super rockets cluster bombs.
- Rocket Barrage Buffied: Brock's rockets gain two smaller side rockets dealing 60% less damage.

### 8-Bit

- Gadget 1: Cheat Cartridge teleports 8-Bit to his Damage Booster.
- Cheat Cartridge Buffied: fully heals the Damage Booster and heals 8-Bit for 3600.
- Gadget 2: Extra Credits rework. 8-Bit fires 18 projectiles; it can now be used during main attack and is aimable.
- Extra Credits Buffied: projectiles also bounce to nearby enemies.
- Star Power 1: Boosted Booster increases Damage Booster range by 50% and damage by an additional 15%.
- Boosted Booster Buffied: every 3 sec, Damage Booster spawns ammo that lasts 8 sec and reloads 1 ammo.
- Star Power 2: Plugged In gives 8-Bit a 25% speed boost near Damage Booster.
- Plugged In Buffied: teammates also get a 15% speed boost from Damage Booster.
- Hypercharge: Aimbot gives 8-Bit's turret a laser that shoots nearby enemies for 230 damage.
- Aimbot Buffied: 8-Bit gains infinite ammo during Hypercharge and can interrupt main attack by firing a new main attack.

### Meg

- Super: Mega Machina rework. Meg enters Mecha and deals 3000 area damage to nearby opponents when entering.
- Gadget 1: Jolting Volts rework. Can only be used in Mecha form; Mecha and nearby teammates heal 11% per sec for 7 sec.
- Jolting Volts Buffied: healing lasts +2 sec.
- Gadget 2: Tool Box rework. Meg ejects and launches Mecha forward for 1600 damage; after ejecting, Meg fully charges Super over 4 sec.
- Tool Box Buffied: Mecha dash leaves a fire trail dealing 30% of the damage for 3 sec.
- Star Power 1: Force Field gives Meg 40% shield when exiting Mecha.
- Force Field Buffied: Meg gains a speed boost while shield is active.
- Star Power 2: Heavy Metal rework. Mecha recovers 1200 health for each enemy hit with Meg's Super.
- Heavy Metal Buffied: also steals 1 ammo from enemy Brawlers.
- Hypercharge: Tungsten Toughness updated for new Super. Feel the Steel has increased range, and enemies hit when Meg calls Mecha are slowed.
- Tungsten Toughness Buffied: Mecha attacks explode on enemy hit for small-area damage; while outside Mecha, Meg attacks fire an extra projectile and home in on enemies.

### Max

- Gadget 1: Phase Shifter is now aimable; Max dashes forward and is immune to all damage while dashing.
- Phase Shifter Buffied: Max can dash again 3 sec after the first dash.
- Gadget 2: Sneaky Sneakers quality-of-life rework. Max throws a lightning bolt; she can reactivate or wait 3 sec to blink back and recover damage taken since placement.
- Sneaky Sneakers Buffied: on blink back, Max refills ammo and heals nearby allies for the same amount as herself.
- Star Power 1: Super Charged rework. Hitting an enemy with main attack extends Super duration by 0.25 sec.
- Super Charged Buffied: teammates affected by Super gain a shield for 4 sec.
- Star Power 2: Run N Gun reloads faster while moving.
- Run N Gun Buffied: Max gains a speed boost when hitting enemies with Faster Blaster.
- Hypercharge: Unlimited Energy.
- Unlimited Energy Buffied: Faster Blaster charges Max and nearby allies' Super when hitting enemies.

### Surge

- Gadget 1: Power Surge rework. Surge increases his Super charge rate by 30% for 5 sec.
- Power Surge Buffied: also increases reload speed by 30%.
- Gadget 2: Power Shield absorbs 50% damage of the next hit within 2 sec and reloads 2 ammo.
- Power Shield Buffied: if Surge is hit during Power Shield, cooldown is reduced by 50%.
- Star Power 1: To the Max! rework. Surge's main attack now bounces off walls.
- To the Max! Buffied: wall bounces create 2 smaller projectiles.
- Star Power 2: Serve Ice Cold rework. Surge always spawns with Super.
- Serve Ice Cold Buffied: while in Stage 3, Party Tricks grants a speed boost when using Super.
- Hypercharge: Stage 5 rework. Surge's Super gains increased range and shoots Justice Juice in every direction on landing.
- Stage 5 Buffied: Justice Juice fires a second projectile dealing less damage.
