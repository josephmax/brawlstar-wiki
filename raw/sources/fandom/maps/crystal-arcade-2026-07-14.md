Crystal Arcade (originally called Crystal Cavern) is an official map for the [[Gem Grab]] event.

[[Crystal Clearing]] was an identical replica of this map for use in the [[Bounty]] event.

==Layout==
The map has four 2x2 walls placed around the center and has bushes facing each team. To the sides are vertical walls with bushes curving inward to the edges of the map. Each corner has a large cluster of bushes, as well as a small amount of walls near the Brawler spawn points. The map is diagonally symmetrical.

==Tips==
*The map favors control-based Brawlers and has little room for short-ranged Brawlers. [[Mr. P]] is most recommended as he can attack Brawlers camping on the sides of the 2x2 walls. Mr. P, [[Jessie]], and [[Penny]] should place their Supers beside the side walls/2x2 walls.
*[[Ash]] is very useful as the walls play to his shorter range. Hide around the sides of the walls on the sides of the map, and then come in and swoop some gems. You can also gate-keep, and make sure that no enemies can reach the gem spawner.
*[[Jacky]] can stay near the sides if enemies are frequently camping there for protection, as her attack can go through obstacles. Otherwise, she should play behind the 2x2 walls pressuring enemies to switch lanes.
*Short or medium-ranged Brawlers such as [[Sandy]], [[Frank]], [[Chuck]], [[Surge]], [[Cordelius]], [[Gene]], [[Poco]], or [[Tara]] should stay behind the 2x2 walls charging their Super. Once they have fully charged it, they should move into the side bush clusters, waiting for an opportunity to attack.
*Brawlers that can make use of the open space and have moderately high health include [[Carl]], [[Amber]], [[R-T]], [[Lola]], [[Pearl]], and [[Emz]]. Brawlers that can use the open space and can support low health teammates include [[Poco]], Pam, [[Doug]], [[Berry]], and [[Byron]].
*You may need the enemy's 2x2 walls later in the match, so try not to destroy them, as this can severely impact the potential of tank teammates such as [[Rosa]], [[Frank]], Jacky, [[Bibi]], [[Draco]], and [[El Primo]]. Tanks are difficult to defeat because the map prefers control-based Brawlers like [[Bo]], [[Griff]], [[Gale]], [[Lou]], etc.
*[[8-Bit]] is really good in this map because of his health, damage output, and the speed from his Plugged In Star Power. Placing his turret in the 2x2 bushes on the right helps teammates control lanes better.
*Playing [[Gene]] in mid and [[Max]] can give you a great comp, as with Max's damage and her Supercharged Star Power with the Gene's Super, they can win most matchups.
*[[Piper]] is also good for this map with her Ambush Star Power.
*[[Rico]] and [[Ruffs]] could force enemies who hide in the two side bushes away due to their bouncing shots. This prevents the enemy team from going offensively once they have full health.
*[[Pam]] and throwers like [[Barley]] and [[Tick]] also provide sufficient area denial, albeit at a shorter range.
*[[Tara]], [[Bo]], [[Penny]], and Carl should act more aggressively on this map because they aren't restricted by the same counters they may have seen in [[Rustic Arcade]]. Staying just behind the center top walls is recommended. If enemies do retreat to the corner bushes, it will be easy to pinch them.
