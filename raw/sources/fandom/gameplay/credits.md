## Visible Page Text

Credits are an in-game currency introduced in the December 2022 update.

Credits allow players to unlock Brawlers from the Starr Road.

Credits can be obtained from the Shop, Starr Drops, the Daily Streak, the Trophy Road, Brawl Pass tiers, Quests and Challenges.

Once all Brawlers are unlocked, all Credits will go towards unlocking Fame tiers.
